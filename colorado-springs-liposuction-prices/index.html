Colorado Springs Liposuction Prices

Features of female physiology and genetically conditioned constitution cause that in some parts of the body fat deposits are accumulated.

The most problem areas are the abdomen, thighs, chin. Fitness and diets are in many cases unsuccessful, and the solution to the problem becomes an operation to remove fat.

The need for liposuction in Colorado Springs arises if there are local fat deposits at a normal weight. Technically, it is the suction of adipose tissue.

To do this, special tubes, called cannulas, through a puncture in the skin are placed in the subcutaneous space, and with their help and vacuum, subcutaneous fat is destroyed and aspirated.

Such an operation is performed on different parts of the body, that is, liposuction of the hips, abdomen, back, knees, sides, submental region, cheeks is possible.

What is fat?

Fat tissue is a cell that provides the body with a supply of energy and maintaining body temperature. Subcutaneous fat consists of deep and superficial layers. In the human body, there is a certain amount of fat cells. As we gain weight, the amount of fat increases.

The number and distribution of fat cells in the human body is established at an early age. As a result, in some parts of the body fat is preserved, even if it is easy to get rid of it in other areas.

During surgery, the surgeon works with a deep layer of fat. This is due to the minimization of risks of skin damage and the occurrence of unevenness.

Postoperative period

It should be remembered that any surgical intervention entails some inconvenience. Especially acute they are felt in the postoperative period. The first 5-7 days after the operation is the time of moderate pain, discomfort, swelling and a slight increase in temperature.

The next day after the procedure, the bandages change. It is recommended to constantly wear compression underwear during a month. You can take it off for showering, puncturing an antiseptic and examining the doctor. You can take a shower for 3-5 days.

In the first days of the postoperative period, swelling in the zones of liposuction is increasing. You may initially think that this is not sufficiently remote fat, but after two weeks the swelling begins to go away, and in a month you will be able to objectively evaluate the result of the operation. Much depends on the compression underwear. Due to compression in the areas of liposuction, puffiness decreases, and blood circulation improves.

In places where excess fat has been removed, the skin is relaxed. Compression underwear promotes tightening of the skin. In addition, it prevents the displacement of soft tissues. Due to the immobilization effect, the healing process is accelerated. If you feel that you really need liposuction to eliminate psychological discomfort and move to a new quality of life, the cost and minor inconveniences will not matter.

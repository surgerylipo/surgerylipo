What is Liposuction in Aventura?

Liposuction in Aventura comes from Latin lipos – fat and English suction – suction) is an independent method or method, supplementing surgical interventions designed to remove limited accumulations of fat in virtually all areas of the human body.

The task that liposuction (mechanical removal) is able to solve, is solely the removal of excessive local fat deposits in problem areas in a woman, the most typical of which are the lateral sections of the thighs and the fatty cushion of the abdomen, individual local deposits on the back or buttocks.

But to create with the help of liposuction the ideal contour proportions of the whole body is impossible.

It is impossible to remove fat from any areas by liposuction (for example, to make the hips as a whole or the legs thinner). That is why there are promising and most popular zones for liposuction, which for all the years of the development of the method have not been extended.

Aventura Aesthetics Laser Lipo

It is necessary to understand that liposuction is a very responsible surgical intervention aimed at removing local fat deposits, which has nothing to do with the treatment of general obesity.

The thing is that the fat is distributed on the human body in a certain way, not in a random way.

Try to take a sheet of paper and draw a contour of a man, not just an abstract person, but, for example, a 16-year-old girl, next to a 50-year-old woman or man, and you will see that the distribution of subcutaneous fat corresponds to the sex and age of the person, but does not always match accepted for today in society canons of beauty.

Excessive fat deposits in the upper part of the lateral surface of the thigh – “riding breeches”, on the inner surface of the knee joints, on the waist are often prevented to choose suitable clothing, contribute to the enhancement of the inferiority complex.

In men, the most common problem is the abdomen.

Thus, liposuction is indicated in the following cases:

  • Patients who want to get a young, contoured appearance, silhouette and size.
  • Patients who want to wear more tight clothing, look better in a swimsuit.
  • Patients who are concerned about local deposits of fat, resistant to diet and exercise.
  • Patients with excessive accumulation of fat in the areas of cheeks, chin, shoulders, abdomen, back, hips, knees, groin.

Liposuction can also be indicated to overweight patients as an addition to diet and exercise.

Is it painful?

The operation is painless, because an anesthesiologist works at the surgery.

If liposuction in Aventura is performed under local anesthesia, then the process of impregnating tissues with an anesthetic is unpleasant, sometimes painful.

Then there are no painful sensations. But after the operation there is pain. The operated zones ache a bit, completely this unpleasant sensation will pass approximately in a month.

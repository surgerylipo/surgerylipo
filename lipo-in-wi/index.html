Postoperative period, recommendations after liposuction

If the volume of fat tissue removed during liposuction was insignificant, you will be able to leave the clinic on the day of surgery. In the case of general anesthesia when carrying out liposuction in large volumes, as a rule, it may be necessary to find a patient in the hospital for 24 hours for the doctor to observe the first hours of adaptation of affected tissues and overall health.

Dallas Liposuction Prices

The variety of body shape is obvious, because the appearance of each person is individual. The obvious desire of many people is the desire to achieve a kind of “bodily harmony”, which will make them feel satisfied with their appearance.

But on the road to perfection, there are often barriers – neither diet nor sport can get rid of local fat traps. But it is local fat deposits that in most cases worsen the outlines of the body. Nowadays the problem of fat traps is completely solved by liposuction.

Today, there are several methods of liposuction. But again, the most effective is the classical technique, in which surpluses of fat are sucked off by a vacuum device with the help of special cannulas.

The method of liposuction in each case is discussed with the surgeon for advice. The patient can only describe his wishes, the plastic surgeon, in turn, suggests the methods by which these wishes can be realized.

The most popular zones of liposuction are the abdomen, hips, flanks, knees, chin area and so on.

Age: Liposuction in Dallas is performed from 18 years of age.

Preparation for the operation:

  • 10 days before liposuction, it is recommended to exclude alcoholic beverages;
  • A temporary cessation of smoking is recommended;
  • 10 days before liposuction should stop taking drugs that affect the process of blood clotting;
  • If liposuction is performed under general anesthesia – 10 hours before the operation, it is required to exclude the intake of any food;
  • purchase a set of compression underwear.

Anesthesia: Liposuction can be performed under local or general anesthesia, depending on the volume of the operation.

Duration of the operation: most often, liposuction lasts 1-3 hours. The duration of the operation determines the number of liposuction zones and the technique.

Stay in the hospital: most often it is not required. With large volumes of liposuction, the patient is in the clinic for 1-2 days.

First dressing: 1-2 days after liposuction.
Removing the dressing: After the operation on the operated area are superimposed special bandages and compression garment is worn. Compression underwear should be worn a month after liposuction.
Removal of sutures: sutures are removed 5-7 days after liposuction.

Recommendations after surgery:

  • you need to wear compression underwear;
  • A month after liposuction is forbidden to visit the sauna and bath;

  • In 1-2 weeks you can return to work;
  • 1-2 months is recommended to refrain from active physical activity.

Early postoperative period: the first days after surgery, pain and natural postoperative movement limitations are possible.

The first 3 days the swelling grows, then goes to loss. In the operated regions, bruises can also be observed, of varying degrees of severity in different patients. There may be a burning sensation and numbness of the operated zones, possibly a leakage of fluid from the area of the incisions in the first days after liposuction in Dallas.

Recovery period: in duration is individual for each patient and depends on the volume of liposuction performed. As a rule, already in a week patients already notice absence of any appreciable discomfort and can return to work.

Most often, the result of the operation is estimated not earlier than in 4-6 months, when all swelling subsides. The result of the operation is stable. It should be understood that if the patient gains weight, the fat will be evenly deposited throughout the body and “remote fat traps” will not occur.

Pain: small and short. The first 1-2 days often accompanied by taking painkillers.

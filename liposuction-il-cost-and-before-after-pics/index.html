Liposuction Illinois Cost And Before After Pics

Liposuction in Illinois is a surgical procedure that is performed to remove excess subcutaneous fat. During the operation, thin tubes with a blunt end are injected subcutaneously through small incisions.

Through them, fat is sucked off with a vacuum device. Such an operation is performed under general anesthesia. Most often, patients resort to liposuction of the hips, abdomen, and sides.

Recently, there are methods that allow you to carry out the procedure under local anesthesia:

Tumescent liposuction – before the suction of fat under the skin is injected a large amount of a solution with lidocaine and adrenaline, which turns fat into an easy-to-remove emulsion, well anesthetizes, reduces the risk of bleeding.

Ultrasound liposuction involves the use of ultrasound to dilute fat deposits. Especially recommend this type of liposuction for the abdomen, back, sides.

19 Year Old Man Treated With Liposuction PreOp & PostOp With Doctor Omeed Memar, MD, PhD, Chicago Dermatologic Surgeon

Laser liposuction uses the energy of laser beams to dissolve fat. Like the previous type, refers to non-surgical methods, which assume the death of fat cells by natural, rather than surgically.

Liposuction can not replace exercise or a diet to maintain a healthy weight. This procedure only accelerates the achievement of the result.

Most surgeons believe that the best candidates for liposuction are healthy people, whose weight is close to normal, but having fat deposits that do not respond to exercise.

Liposuction is one of the most popular aesthetic operations, allowing you to thin and give a spectacular outline to specific areas of your body.

Liposuction in Illinois can be performed alone or in combination with other procedures, such as abdominal plastic (abdominoplasty), aesthetic and reconstructive operations on the mammary gland. When performing liposuction, we use the traditional method of tumescent liposuction, and ultrasonic liposuction modern device company Soring.

Key points of the operation:

  • This operation is recommended under general anesthesia.
  • The average duration of the operation varies within 1-2 hours.
  • Stay in the hospital is 12-24 hours.

Recovery period:

  • For 1 month after the operation, it is necessary to wear compression underwear!
  • Restriction of physical activity last within 30 days after the operation.
  • For 5-7 days, maintenance drug therapy is continuing to ensure safe and comfortable recovery.
  • Suture removal on days 14-21 after surgical correction.
  • Aesthetic evaluation of the result of the operation after 1.5 months.

Doctor’s comment:

It is necessary to understand – liposuction is not a method of fighting obesity! This operation is perfect for correcting local “fat traps” in the abdomen, sides, hips – with such problem areas where neither diet nor exercise do not bring good results.

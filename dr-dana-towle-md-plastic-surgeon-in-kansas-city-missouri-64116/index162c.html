How long to wait after getting another plastic surgery?
How long between surgery.
Jun 1, 2018
Generally Surgeries can be done very close together even at the same time. The exception to this are too many surgeries at the same time can increase risk and two surgeries on the same body part that need to be staged may have a certain amount of time between to allow for the necessary healing. Talk to your surgeon about your overall goals and surgeries you wish done and they will be able to give you more specific advice about timing.

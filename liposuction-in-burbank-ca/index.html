Liposuction in Burbank can truly be called a revolutionary achievement of the twentieth century in the field of aesthetic surgery. So, if the cosmetic surgeons could correct the surplus fat on the abdomen, partly the hips, shoulders and chin by traditional methods, then the patients’ needs for local fat removal in more difficult areas such as buttocks, pelvis, shin, back were much more difficult, and often simply impossible.

For the first time about liposuction in the form in which it exists now, the French surgeon Illouz said in 1980. He gradually spread his technique to every area of the body.

A low percentage of complications quickly attracted the attention of colleagues, and for today liposuction is one of the most common operations in the arsenal of every aesthetic surgeon.

Liposuction Sculpts The Body By Removing Undesirable Fat

The essence of this technique is that through small punctures in unobtrusive areas with the use of blunt cannulas, fat is drained from the places of its clusters.

Fatty tissue is very similar honeycomb honey, in which honey is fat, and the cells themselves are vessels, nerve and connective tissue fibers.

Such a structure of adipose tissue allows to remove fat with the help of a vacuum device with minimal damage to the above structures.

Reserve fat.

There is such a thing as reserve fat. These special fat cells, which are located in such areas as the lateral sections of the hips, lower abdomen, buttocks, that is, in those areas that are often called “resistant” to the diet.

Such fat cells easily tolerate hunger and rapidly increase in the presence of glucose. Liposuction is highly effective for patients who could not lose weight, and no physical activity helped them to make a flat stomach or remove excess fat from the hips.

Indications for liposuction.

The most efficient operation in patients with localized fat deposits, and normal elasticity of the skin, allowing it to easily contraction postoperatively.
The “ideal” weight for a patient’s liposuction should be close to or slightly above the norm.

The operation does not allow to remove “unlimited” volumes of adipose tissue. The weight of fatty tissue to be removed should not exceed 5lb-6lb per operation, otherwise there is a real threat to the patient’s state of health.

It should immediately be stipulated that this operation is not intended for patients suffering from general obesity. Also, this operation does not eliminate the appearance of cellulite.

The technique of liposuction.

Vacuum removal of fat deposits from problem areas of the body is performed through mini-cuts of the skin from 5 to 7 mm. Most often liposuction is exposed to the outer “riding breeches” and inner thighs, the inner surfaces of the knees, abdomen, buttocks, sometimes the back and the inner surface of the shoulders, the area of the “double” chin. 1 seam overlaps the puncture. The sutures are removed on the 7th day after the operation.

In order to place the same amount of fat tissue on the site of liposuction as it was before the operation, several tens of years will be required.

Liposuction does not normalize the fat metabolism, and if after it does not watch for yourself, the fat will be deposited in other parts of the body. In the presence of endocrine diseases, treatment with hormonal drugs, as well as after pregnancy and childbirth, body weight can also increase due to fat deposition in adjacent areas.

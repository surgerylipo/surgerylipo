Lipo-modelling is a modern, low-traumatic and highly effective technique for modeling the body with the use of adipose tissue of the body.

With age, as a result of various factors, excess fat accumulation occurs.

This destructive fat, as proved by scientists, is beginning to create a uneven body skin in a few months, visible to the naked eye and frustrating patients.

Improving the shape of the body by lipo-modelling

Indications for lipo-modelling and liposuction are the presence of serious local fat deposits (the so-called “fat traps”) at normal weight.

Such traps include the fatty deposits that severely damage the figure and its outlines: double chin, fat apron, change in the shape of the waist, fat deposits in the intercostal area with X-shaped curvature of the legs, deposits on the buttocks, hips and legs, changing the volume and deforming appearance.

Lipo-modelling and liposuction should not be confused. Their main difference is that with liposuction, the usual removal of subcutaneous fat occurs.

During lipomodeling an excess of adipose tissue is used for surgical correction of the necessary zone.

The fat collected from problem areas is also a building material that is used to correct wrinkles on the face, to change the relief of the legs, increase the volume of the mammary glands, and fill the defects after injuries.

Lipo-modelling and liposuction is not an option for easy and fast weight loss, it’s a way of body correction.

If a person is overweight with preserved body proportions and absence of pronounced defects, a diet with reduced calorie intake, exercise, and if there is cellulite, use of cosmetic procedures is recommended.

Method of lipo-modelling

Orlando clinic uses a patented lipo-modelling technique. This technique promotes the free movement of fat cells within the operated area, thereby providing a more aesthetic contour of the body. To do this, the surgeon, using manual technique, produces a redistribution of fat cells in the subcutaneous layer.

The surgeon is a kind of sculptor, who creates a new contour of the body. At the end of the operation, an elastic bandage is applied to the “corrected” area.

It has special properties: it stimulates skin contraction and prevents the formation of unnecessary folds, which are subject to removal. The operation lasts for several hours.

Anesthesia

The type of anesthesia, in many respects depends on the size of the operated area, the patient’s desire, and also whether lipo-modelling is performed as an independent method of operative intervention or together with other methods.

Postoperative period

The operation of lipomodelling is of medium complexity. After it is carried out, the patient is transferred to a separate ward, under the constant supervision of medical personnel. After the operation, a special tightening patch is pasted onto the operated area. On the second day patients are already allowed to get up.

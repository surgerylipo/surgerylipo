Liposuction: Preparation for Surgery

About preparation for liposuction in Brazil the patient learns on consultation. As a rule, we are talking about taking food and liquids before liposuction, about smoking if the patient smokes.

An important point is taking medications. For the most favorable course of the operation and subsequent recovery, the patient must comply with all the doctor’s recommendations.

About the previously transferred operations and diseases the patient needs to inform his surgeon.

In some cases, liposuction can be delayed if, for example, a patient recently suffered from upper respiratory tract disease or suffered any infection of the skin. This information is important, so you need to give the doctor maximum information about your health.

If you smoke, then it is recommended to reduce the number of smoked cigarettes. Ideally, it is better to stop smoking during the operation and recovery period.

The reason is obvious – smoking slows the healing process.

For 10 days before liposuction, it is necessary to exclude the use of medicines containing salicylates (Aspirin etc.).

The action of salicylates increases bleeding both during the operation and after.

Without fail, a plastic surgeon should know about taking these medications, because an early stop of bleeding affects the result of the operation.

If you are taking any other medications – be sure to tell your surgeon.

Recommendations for women:

Taking contraceptive or other hormonal medications can also provoke bleeding. This question is individually decided on a consultation with a plastic surgeon. Most often it is recommended to stop taking this kind of drugs 10-14 days before liposuction.

During the period of menstruation, liposuction in Brazil can not be done, so it is necessary to plan the date of the operation so that it does not fall into the period of menstruation (it is necessary to exclude menstruation days, and also 3-5 days before and after it).

If the volume of liposuction is small – the operation is often performed under local anesthesia and after 1-2 hours the patient can leave the clinic on his own. Previously, the patient receives a list of necessary medications that should be taken during recovery period.

If you plan to remove a significant amount of fat, then liposuction in Brazil is carried out under general anesthesia. In this case, the patient is discharged from the clinic on the next day after liposuction. Further visits to the clinic for the appointment of your plastic surgeon.

How is liposuction performed?

Liposuction in Brazil can be performed using different types of anesthesia.

The volume of liposuction for each patient is different. If the estimated amount of fat removed is insignificant, the operation can be performed under local anesthesia. But if a significant removal of surplus fat is expected, then the use of general anesthesia is most often recommended. In particular, when performing electronic lipomodelling, local anesthesia is usually used. In some cases, local anesthesia can be combined with simultaneous intravenous administration of sedatives.

Kind of anesthesia is discussed in details with the plastic surgeon, after the examination.

Again, the duration of liposuction directly depends on the volume of fat tissue to be removed, the total area of the treated zones, the type of anesthesia and, of course, the technique used.

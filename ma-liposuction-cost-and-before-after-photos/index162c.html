Massachusetts Liposuction Cost And Before After Photos

Liposuction in Massachusetts is more than just a cosmetic surgery, it’s a chance to sculpt the shape of your body according to your choice! Surgical removal of locally located excess fatty deposits is called liposuction. The most susceptible to accumulation of excess fat are parts of the body such as the waist, hips, buttocks, calves, chin.

The body loses its proportionality and natural flexibility. Such an effective procedure as liposuction is offered by our clinic. Liposuction is performed here at the highest professional level and is a unique method for getting rid of excess fatty deposits.

It allows not only to reduce the volume of the body, but also to form its proportional outlines.When weight loss with diet and physical activity, weight decreases evenly throughout the body, but locally located fat clusters remain in place.

Only modern technology of liposuction, which is offered, is able to cope with the problem of removing zoned excess fat.

33 Year Old Man Treated With Liposuction Before After With Dr Christopher J. Davidson, MD, FACS, Boston Plastic Surgeon

The effect that gives liposuction is stable and has a favorable prognosis: fat removed from the body area will never return. The operation is indicated to patients of any age, but for the elderly person the effectiveness may be somewhat lower than for the young one due to the age-related loss of skin elasticity.

In Massachusetts clinic, the liposuction procedure is performed exclusively by modern methods – it is the safest, non-traumatic and easily transportable tumescent liposuction, ultrasound and laser liposuction and the newest procedure in the plastic surgery market – RF liposuction. By time, the removal of fat deposits takes from 30 minutes to one and a half hours, depending on the amount of fat removed.

Doctor Mark K. Markarian, Massachusetts Plastic Surgeon Body Fat Removal Surgery Recovery

The recovery period takes about 6 weeks, during which it is necessary to carefully follow the postoperative rules and doctor’s recommendations. As a result, the patient gets the desired slender and proportional body without harm to his health and undesirable consequences.

Liposuction of the outer thigh

Restoration of efficiency almost immediately after the operation, but it is necessary to avoid more physical exertion for a month and wear compression linen to restore the tone of the skin and fatty tissue. The final effect is already visible after 2-4 weeks.

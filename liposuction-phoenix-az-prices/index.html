Liposuction Phoenix AZ Prices

Liposuction (from Greek lipos – fat and English – suction) is an aesthetic (cosmetic) operation designed to remove localized fat deposits, on hips, buttocks, abdomen, as well as arms, neck and in the chin area. Sometimes liposuction is used to remove fat in other places.

Can i get rid of cellulite by Lipo?

Liposuction is not intended to eliminate cellulite, however, after surgery, there may be a certain improvement associated with a decrease in the layer of fat deposits.

What result can be expected?

Liposuction is ideal for patients with local fat deposits that are not amenable to diet and exercise.

This operation does not involve reducing the total body weight. Usually, the skin covering the areas of intervention is reduced after surgery, although in some cases, with significant excess of skin, they may need to be removed.

Dr. Carlos Mata, Phoenix Plastic Surgeon Laser Lipo Treatment Before And After Photos

How stable is the result of this procedure?

The removed adipose tissue, as a rule, does not form again on the site of intervention.

If the total body weight after the operation does not increase, the effect of liposuction remains constant. If you do not follow a diet, the fat tissue can accumulate in adjacent areas.

When can I restore the normal rhythm of life?

This is an individual question. Most patients can restore normal social activity one week after the operation. Often this period may be even shorter.

Types of liposuction and their advantages

If we consider the body from the point of view of the presence of problem areas, then to a greater extent they include the abdomen, sides and hips, which is why traditional abdominal liposuction and liposuction of the hips are considered the most demanded procedures conducted in the clinics of aesthetic surgery. The most effective methods of liposuction are vacuum, tumescent and ultrasound.

Liposuction by Vacuum

Vacuum, or mechanical method is most effective in cases, where a sufficiently large area of the body or a sufficiently large layer of adipose tissue is subject to treatment. In the course of this type of surgery, a special composition that helps to thin the fat mass and safe preparations designed to narrow the blood vessels are introduced through small incisions, after which the fat is pumped out using thin cannulae (laser needles of small diameter).

Dr. Shaun Parson, Phoenix Plastic Surgeon Liposuction Fat

The needle, penetrating deep into the interlayer, makes translational movements, contributing also to the mechanical destruction of adipose tissue, which significantly increases the effectiveness of the procedure and reduces its duration.

Tumescent liposuction

Tumescent method is considered to be the least traumatic, since it implies the mandatory conduct of infiltration of adipose tissue. As an infiltrate, which is introduced 20-40 minutes before tissue removal, Klein solution is used – a specialized composition based on epinephrine, saline and lidocaine, stimulating tissue dilution, minimizing discomfort and negative influence of interference on the state of blood vessels and muscle tissue.

Essential dilution of fat mass facilitates the process of its removal from the body and prevents the massive occurrence of bruising.

Ultrasound-assisted liposuction (UAL)

New Liposuction Of Leg By Dr Remus Repta, Phoenix Plastic Surgeon

The ultrasound method also features minimal trauma. Fat cells are destroyed non-mechanically, while the vessels and muscle tissue, which are more dense in comparison with the fat structure, are not affected by the shock wave.

Before the ultrasonic treatment, the fat layer is also saturated with the Klein solution, as a result of these two manipulations, the fat is transformed into a composition with a consistency of the emulsion, which ensures its more painless in comparison with other methods, removal from the body by vacuum equipment.

Liposculpture is a relatively new, jewelery technique that is very popular among celebrities. Through the tiny punctures, very thin cannulas are inserted into the interlayer, which guarantees maximum precision of fat removal and absence of such consequences as scars or slight unevenness of the relief.

This is a more complex and lengthy process than traditional liposuction, the price of such a procedure is much higher, however, the result is more perfect.

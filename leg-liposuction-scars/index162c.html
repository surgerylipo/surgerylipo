Features of Leg Liposuction

Liposuction of the legs is carried out by professional plastic surgeons exclusively non-surgical (laser, injection, radio waves) methods. Such procedures allow you to eliminate excess fatty tissue in the legs, making their outlines more slender, elegant and proportionate.

Liposuction allows you to get brilliant results even in difficult cases, when diets, massages and physical exercises do not bring the desired effect. Indications for leg liposuction

Liposuction in the leg area is recommended for patients with the following problems:

  1. Disproportionately large ankles.
  2. Sagging skin in the thigh area.
  3. Cellulite.
  4. Excess fat deposits on the inner and outer thighs.
  5. Massive knees.
  6. Presence of skin-cellulite deposits in the knee area.
  7. Absence of efficiency from diet therapy and physical training.

Contraindications to leg liposuction

Any type of liposuction in the leg area is contraindicated in the presence of the following health problems:

  • Chronic pathologies in the stage of exacerbation.
  • Obesity.
  • Infectious and inflammatory processes occurring in an acute form.
  • Violation of the integrity of the skin in the legs.
  • Pregnancy.
  • Breastfeeding period.

Do scars remain on the body of the patient who underwent liposuction of the legs?

Like any other surgical procedure, liposuction leaves behind scars on the body. Their magnitude will depend solely on the level of training of your surgeon and the field of liposuction. For this procedure, we use the cannula – a special medical tube, intended for insertion into the hollow human organs.

The diameter of the cannula is approximately 50 mm, but the diameter of the incision for this tube is even smaller – about 15-20 mm. If you fall into the hands of a truly experienced plastic surgeon, he will try to choose a place for incisions so that traces are lost in the natural folds of your body. In this regard, the likelihood that they will be seen after liposuction is very small.

But I want to emphasize once again – the scars after any surgical operation are inevitable! Fortunately, the markings remaining after liposuction are small. Over time, they dissolve completely. If you want everything to be like this, then trust an experienced certified surgeon, and not someone who offers you any “Christmas discounts”.

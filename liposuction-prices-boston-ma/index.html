It is difficult to find a person who does not have even a slight inclination for the deposition of adipose tissue. The abdominal zone, of course, occupies a leading position here.

And it is this fact that can explain the incredible popularity of liposuction abdomen. This operation is considered to be one of the most effective ways to get rid of fatty surpluses in the problem zone in a short time.

It is important to know! Liposuction of the abdomen is NOT a way to lose weight due to weight loss, like, say, aerobics or fitness.

In some cases, liposuction is only the initial stage of correcting the contours of the abdomen, after which an operation is required to get rid of excess skin in this problem area – abdominoplasty.

Types of liposuction in Boston

Liposuction is performed in many ways. If there is a large volume of excess fat deposits, a standard mechanical operation is used.

Dr Jeffrey M. Darrow, MD, Boston Plastic Surgeon – Liposuction

With a small amount of excess fat – tumescent, ultrasound or laser liposuction. The main difference is in the way the fat tissues are affected and fat cells are destroyed.

The most suitable method is selected individually, the choice depends on the patient’s “initial data” and the expected result.

Precisely choose the type of procedure, find out its features and clarify the cost of liposuction of the abdomen, you can by registering for a consultation in the clinic.

How is surgery performed?

The doctor photographs the problem area and marks the places that will be affected during the operation to remove fat from the abdomen. The patient is given anesthesia – local or general, after which several punctures are made (usually in the groin and near the navel).

In the puncture, a solution is injected, which includes anesthetic, vasoconstrictor and electrolytes. Fat is removed by means of thin cannulas connected to a special apparatus.

Recovery period

Liposuction of the abdomen refers to simple manipulations, therefore the term of stay in the clinic, as a rule, is small – 1-2 days. Immediately after the operation, you will be wearing compression underwear, which can not be removed within 3-5 weeks.

Swelling, bruising, bruising and numbness may bother you for one and a half months after liposuction. But these phenomena are temporary, and they will necessarily disappear.

How much does the liposuction of the abdomen cost?

It is difficult to identify the exact amount that you will need to spend on removing excess fat in the abdominal area. In each individual case, the price is calculated individually – depending on the final volume of pumped out fat masses, such as liposuction and concomitant treatment.

Liposuction of the abdomen: when it is impossible?

Do not remove the excess fat if you have the following contraindications.

  • Pregnancy and lactation period;
  • diabetes;
  • Somatic diseases during an exacerbation;
  • tuberculosis;
  • oncological diseases;
  • hepatitis.

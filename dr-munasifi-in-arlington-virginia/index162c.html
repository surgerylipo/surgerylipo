Best treatment to reduce/prevent jowling? Ultherapy or would a filler be better?
Several options available.
Dec 20, 2017
Ultherapy would be a great treatment option to help lift and tighten the jowl area. It's non-invasive and has no downtime. Fillers are good options as well, but they are best used to treat areas of volume loss. Both treatments can also be used in combination if needed to give optimal results.

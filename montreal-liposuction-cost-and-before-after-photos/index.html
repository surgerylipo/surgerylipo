Montreal Liposuction Cost And Before After Photos

The most popular surgical operation in the world today is liposuction – the removal of local fat deposits.

The popularity of this direction of plastic surgery is due to the fact that the physical removal of fat deposits remains the most effective way of modeling the body shape.

An important advantage of liposuction of the body and face is the fact that the fat cells removed by the operation are not restored.

In addition to surgical, laser liposuction and combined “Hollywood liposuction” are performed, which allows to achieve the perfect result.

INDICATIONS FOR BODY AND FACE LIPOSUCTION

Liposuction of the face is often performed simultaneously with a lift, however, in the presence of pronounced fat deposits in the zone of the chin and cheeks, the liposuction operation of these zones is prescribed separately.

33 Year Old Woman Treated With Liposuction Before & After By Doctor Jacques Haddad, MD, Montreal Plastic Surgeon

On the cheeks, neck and chin can be performed as tumescent vacuum liposuction of the face with the help of a cannula, and laser liposuction of the face. Correctly performed liposuction gives an excellent effect of rejuvenation, transforms facial features, makes the neck and cheekbones elegant and aesthetic.

Liposuction of the body is not a way to get rid of excess weight. Despite the fact that up to 3 lb 4 oz. of fat from one zone can be removed in one operation, the best results are achieved with a stable weight.

The main indication for liposuction in Montreal is expressed fatty deposits in any of the “problem areas”: on the hands, back, waist, sides, abdomen, thighs, buttocks. Also indications may be minor, but unaesthetic “fat stores”, for example, in the area of the knees.

Dr Carlos Cordoba, Montreal Plastic Surgeon Laser Fat Removal Recovery

Liposuction face and body

Liposuction is performed with the placement of the patient in a comfortable hospital, as well as with a mandatory examination before the operation, which allows to ensure the maximum level of safety.

During surgical liposuction of the body, special cannulas are introduced through the small incisions under the skin, through which, after carrying out a special solution, excess fat is “pumped out” by the vacuum method.

Traces from the incisions, as a rule, subsequently heal practically without a trace, in addition, they are usually located in natural creases and folds and are easily masked with underwear even during recovery.

The operation of liposuction lasts from 1 to 3 hours, depending on the number of zones and the volume of fat removed.

Despite the fact that this type of surgery refers to the classic of plastic surgery, a lot depends on the equipment of the operating room and the surgeon’s qualification.

Recovery after liposuction of face and body

1-2 weeks after liposuction, you can go back to work, the stitches are removed on the 7th day, you should wear a special compression underwear or bandages for several weeks, and after 1-2 months you can already live a normal life, without any special restrictions (you can exercise, visit the sauna), but with a new, more harmonious body. The final effect is manifested after 3-4 months (after skin contraction).

Liposuction of the abdomen

Liposuction of the abdomen, sides (flanks) and waist zone is one of the most popular operations. It should be noted that liposuction of the abdomen in Montreal gives the brightest and most impressive result in the event that significant fat deposits are located on the stomach and sides.

In the case of significant excess of skin and dilated flabby abdominal muscles, abdominoplasty is recommended. Experienced surgeon in the course of face-to-face consultation will select the optimal method for achieving the patient’s goal, describe in detail about the capabilities and features of various types of plastics, and offer the best solution in each situation.

Liposuktion thighs and buttocks

Really impressive results are given by liposuction of the hips: inner, outer surface. Often the excess of fatty deposits in these zones spoil as a whole a slim and harmonious body. Liposuction of the buttocks refers to complex operations from the point of view that here the work of a surgeon is akin to the art of a sculptor – it is necessary to create an aesthetic three-dimensional shape. Trust your body only to high-class professionals. So, like surgeons.

Liposuction In Montreal To Reduce Areas Of Unwanted Fat

In addition to liposuction of the abdomen, sides, waist, hips and buttocks, liposuction of the neck, chin, cheeks, armpits, arms, back of the shoulders, back, shoulder blades, knees, calves and even the liposuction of the pubic area are also performed.

LASER LIPOSUCTION

Laser liposuction is carried out by inserting under the skin the finest (1 mm in diameter) light guides, at the end of each of which is located a laser radiator that destroys fat cells.

Then, the stimulation of the dermis is carried out with the help of a laser beam, thus forming a connective tissue, a new collagen and elastin, directly in the course of laser liposuction, so that after the procedure the skin is tightened as soon as possible.

Products of fat decay under the influence of laser liposuction are deduced in a natural way, the recovery period is virtually absent, there is no need for hospitalization of the patient.

Liposuction Is Not A Preferred Treatment For Weight Loss

Laser liposuction is gaining popularity every year, but it should be understood that it is most effective most often as an addition to classical liposuction. In the clinic performed the complex “Hollywood liposuction” combining classical vacuum and innovative laser technology to achieve the best results: the bulk removal of fat deposits using vacuum tumescent liposuction and then smoothing and harmonization of results, giving ideal contours with jeweler laser operation.

The cost of liposuction in Montreal

The price of liposuction for the face and body depends on the zone and the degree of complexity of the operation (including the amount of fat removed). As a rule, the cost of liposuction consists of a number of factors, the most important of which are: the qualification of the doctor, the equipment of the clinic, the duration and complexity of the operation.

The doctor can name the exact price of liposuction only after a full-time consultation. It includes both the work of a doctor, and stay in a hospital, anesthesia, dressings and so on.

Liposculpture Before And After

View before and after photos of liposculpture patients who have been treated by various plastic surgeons. With age, in many people, local fat deposits become more voluminous. In some areas, on the contrary, in the course of time a lack of soft tissues is formed, which further breaks the contours of the face and body.

The optimal solution in this situation is considered to be liposculpture of the body – modeling the body at the expense of its own fat tissue.

In areas with excess fat tissue, liposuction is performed – fat removal, and in areas with insufficient volume – lipofilling, that is filling with your own fat tissue.

Liposculpture will not help with total obesity. However, this is the most effective method of reducing local fat deposits.

In the classical version, it assumes the presence of notches, through which the tissue is extracted from the fat traps.

The main correction zones are the chin area, the sagging areas of the soft tissues in the mandible zone, as well as the shoulders, abdomen, lateral surfaces of the trunk, the inner thighs, the “riding breeches”, knees, calves.

Types of liposculpture

Traditional (vacuum dry) surgical liposuction is rarely used, today more modern types are preferred – tumescent, ultrasound. Each technique is used for certain indications.

Ultrasonic liposuction is preferred for areas with thin or overstretched skin – such as the inner thighs and stomach. Tumescent liposuction has other advantages – it is not highly traumatic in connection with the use of thin cannulas and is applicable for any zones.

Often, liposuction is used as an auxiliary procedure in the performance of abdominoplasty, facelift and neck, correction of curvature of the legs.

The removed fat cells are not restored, therefore the expressed aesthetic effect of liposculpture is reached by means of one procedure.

Depending on the volume of the intervention, liposculpture is performed under local or general anesthesia.

With small amounts of fat removed, the operation is performed on an outpatient basis, with fat volumes exceeding 0.26 gallon or a large number of zones, hospitalization is required for one day. After the operation, the patient needs to wear special compression underwear for 2-4 weeks.

Body Lipofilling

Lipofilling deserves special attention. This is a procedure in which fat removed by liposuction is used to correct soft tissue abnormalities, curvature of the calves, increase the buttocks, improve the contours of the breast, and change the shape of the lips.

Lipofilling is considered one of the safest methods of rejuvenation. When using liposculpture in combination with lipofilling, the plastic surgeon works as a sculptor – removes the tissue in some zones and fills other zones with it. As a result, harmonious proportions of the patient’s body are formed, natural beauty is restored.

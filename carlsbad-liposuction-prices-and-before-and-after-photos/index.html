The average can range anywhere from $2,000 for smaller procedures to as much as $10,000 for major treatments.

Tumescent Liposuction

Tumescent liposuction in Carlsbad is a technique that has many benefits over earlier traditional liposuction techniques because it causes less damage to tissue surrounding the treatment area and reduces the amount of bleeding incurred during the surgery. It is a form of super wet liposuction which basically means that a liquid solution is injected into the area before the surgery begins.

Doctor Robert Singer, Carlsbad Plastic Surgeon Fat Removal From Back

Super wet just refers to the amount of fluid injected into the area. In the case of tumescent liposuction the injection is double, or sometimes triple, the volume of the body fat to be removed and consists of a local anesthesia and vasoconstrictor.

This injection creates a space between the fat cells and muscle tissue which aids in the suction of the fat.

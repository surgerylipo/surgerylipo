As you know, diet and fitness do not always justify the expectations of those who want to lose weight.

After all, it often happens that fat deposits accumulate because of genetic predisposition or located in places where physical exercises can not be influenced by them.

In such cases, liposuction is used to correct the body shape – an effective technique for removing subcutaneous fat, which in one session gives a result equal to the years of workout.

What is liposuction and types of procedure

– Vacuum (classical) liposuction. In small cutaneous incisions – up to 5 mm – tubes are inserted, the shape and diameter depends on the place and volume of work.

The incisions are made in inconspicuous places along the periphery of the problem areas. The second end of the tubes is connected to a special vacuum device that sucks in fat. The tube is inserted with fan-shaped movements in order to loosen the fatty tissue for easier removal. This method is considered the most traumatic, the volume of fat removed is not more than three liters.

A Process Of Surgical Removal Of Stubborn Fat Resistant To Diet And Exercise

  • Syringe liposuction. This technique is used to remove fat deposits of a small volume (up to 0.5 liters). The operation lasts from 15 to 30 minutes. In this case, the fat is sucked off with special syringes.
  • Tumescent liposuction. For 25-40 minutes before the operation, a mixture of adrenaline, lidocaine and saline solution is injected into the problem zone. As a result, fat deposits soften and dilute, which facilitates the suction of fat and reduces blood loss. Therefore, this type of procedure is less traumatic. The recovery period is faster.
  • Ultrasonic liposuction. Before the procedure ultrasound affect on the problem area. This destroys adipose tissue and simplifies its subsequent removal. Further suctioning occurs in the same way as in the case of classical liposuction.
  • Laser liposuction. This is the least traumatic kind of procedure. Its essence lies in the fact that under the influence of a laser beam, the fat cells are destroyed, their contents enter the blood stream and are excreted from the body with the help of the liver. For one such session, up to 0.3 kg of fat is removed, disappears 1.5-2 cm in the circumference of the body. The procedure is painless and has virtually no complications.

What kind of procedure to apply the doctor decides on the basis of individual peculiarities of the patient.

Contraindications

A prerequisite for liposuction in Pune is a good general condition of the patient. Therefore, it is extremely important to provide your physician with complete and reliable information. With special care, we should discuss possible risks for such violations as:

  • any renal and hepatic diseases;
  • diabetes mellitus and hyperthyroidism, endocrine obesity;
  • cardiovascular diseases;
  • stomach ulcer;
  • oncological diseases;
  • varicose veins, anemia, thrombophlebitis, blood clotting disorders.

For two weeks before and after the procedure, it is recommended to refrain from smoking – this will accelerate the healing of wounds. If you are taking any medications, also discuss this with your doctor.

Pune liposuction for weight loss

The undoubted advantage of liposuction is that there will never be new cells in place of the removed fat cells. But it is important to follow a healthy lifestyle and adhere to proper nutrition, otherwise the remaining fatty tissue may increase in volume and the problem will recur.

The procedure is indicated for people with local body fat. Most often these are zones of the second chin, cheeks, abdomen, thighs and “riding breeches.” These areas, even with a serious decrease in weight and physical exertion, can spoil the contour of the body. It can be caused by heredity or individual characteristics. Also, the procedure is effective in menopause, removal of lime and gynecomastia.

He also tells you whether to perform liposuction as an independent procedure, or more effectively apply complex intervention together with abdominoplasty, reduction of the mammary glands, lipofilling and so on.

Recovery period

Since liposuction is a surgical operation, it requires a restorative stage. Complete recovery and discharge from the hospital depends on the type of operation and the amount of work done.

Recovery after short procedures passes quickly. After more complex operations a person can return to work on average in a week, and to active physical activity in a month.

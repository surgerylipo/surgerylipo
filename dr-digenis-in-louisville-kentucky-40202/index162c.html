Neck lift and upper eye lid surgery, how long is down time?
Recover time for neck, face and eyelid lift
Aug 12, 2015
Every patient heals differently and different surgeon's techniques heal differently. In general, a neck and eyelid lift takes about a week for the stitches to come out and usually another 4 to 7 days to put makeup over any bruised areas. Could eyelid surgery change the appearance of my eyes?
Eyelid Asymmetry/Droop
Jul 14, 2015
Blepharoplasty or eyelid surgery can oftentimes improve the appearance of one's eyes. By removing excess skin , we can make the appearance of the eye more open, thereby giving you a more youthful or "refreshed look." Everyone has asymmetry but consult with a board certified plastic surgeon to determine if eyelid surgery is appropriate for addressing your needs.

Tighter pectoral muscle on one side pre surgery. Will this cause asymmetry?
Breast Asymmetry
Jul 14, 2015
Patients with tight pectoral muscles prior to breast augmentation surgery will tend to have their implants ride higher on the chest for the first few weeks or months. But a good plastic surgeon can help adjust the asymmetry based on the size of the implant, type of implant, and additional surgical procedures recommended by your plastic surgeon. Always consult with a board certified plastic surgeon before making your final decision.
Wanting perky breasts without getting a lift
Breast Augmentation/Lift
Jul 14, 2015
Breast Augmentation can sometimes provide a lift to patients that have minimal "ptosis," or lax skin in that area. If patient has a grade 2 or 3 ptosis, a mastopexy or "lift" may be needed as well. To determine the amount of breast droop, consult a board certified plastic surgeon to consider these options.

Irregularities are common unpredictable side effects with liposuction. However there are some techniques that help smooth out these "lumpy bumps" like the Z-Wave which we perform in office post-operatively. Check out your area for plastic surgeons who have these procedures in place.

LIPOSUCTION: BASIC INFORMATION

The variety of bodies is obvious, because the appearance of each person is individual.

The obvious desire of many people is the desire to achieve a kind of “bodily harmony” that will make them feel satisfied with their appearance.

But on the road to perfection, there are often barriers – neither diet nor sport can get rid of local fat traps.

But it is local fat deposits that in most cases worsen the outlines of the body. Nowadays the problem of fat traps is completely solved by liposuction.

Today, there are several methods of liposuction in Poland.

But again, the most effective is the classical technique, in which surpluses of fat are sucked off by a vacuum device with the help of special cannulas.

The method of liposuction in each specific case is discussed with the surgeon for advice. The patient can only describe his wishes, the plastic surgeon, in turn, suggests methods by which these wishes can be realized.

The most popular areas of liposuction are the abdomen, things, flanks, knees, chin area and so on.
Age: Liposuction is performed from 18 years of age.

Preparation for surgery:

  1. 10 days before liposuction, it is recommended that alcohol beverages be disused;
  2. a temporary cessation of smoking is recommended;
  3. 10 days before liposuction should stop taking drugs that affect the process of blood clotting;
  4. if liposuction is performed under general anesthesia – 10 hours before the operation, it is required to exclude the intake of any food;
  5. purchase a set of compression underwear.

Anesthesia: Liposuction can be performed under local or general anesthesia, depending on the volume of the operation.

Laser Liposuction Poland

Stay in the hospital: most often it is not required. With large amounts of liposuction, the patient is in the clinic for 1-2 days.

The first dressing is performed 1-2 days after liposuction.

Removing the dressing: After the operation on the operated area are superimposed special bandages and compression garment is worn. Compression underwear should be worn a month after liposuction. Removal of sutures: sutures are removed 5-7 days after liposuction in Poland.

Recommendations after surgery:

  • necessarily wearing compression linen;
  • a month after liposuction is forbidden to visit the sauna and bath;
  • in 1-2 weeks you can return to work;
  • 1-2 months is recommended to refrain from active physical activity.

Early postoperative period: the first days after the operation, pain and natural postoperative movement limitations are possible. The first 3 days of swelling increases, then goes to loss.

In the operated regions, bruises can also be observed, of varying degrees of severity in different patients. There may be a burning sensation and numbness of the operated zones, possibly a leakage of fluid from the area of the incisions in the first days after liposuction.

Recovery period: in duration, it is individual for each patient and depends on the volume of performed liposuction. As a rule, already in a week patients already notice absence of any appreciable discomfort and can return to work. Most often, the result of the operation is estimated not earlier than in 4-6 months, when all the swelling subsides.

Saving the result: persistent. It should be understood that if the patient gains weight, the fat will be evenly deposited throughout the body and “remote fat traps” will not occur.

Pain: minor and of short duration. The first 1-2 days are often accompanied by taking painkillers.

What are the best candidates for liposuction?

Ironically, liposuction in Poland is more suitable for people with normal weight or close to normal. In some cases, the patient is recommended to lose weight before liposuction. Especially it is necessary to wait with operation, if losing weight is included in the nearest plans.

Thus, you first need to bring your weight back to normal, and then have a liposuction. After liposuction, it is recommended to avoid significant weight fluctuations, because this may worsen the postoperative result.

Body Sculpting Liposuction With Dr. Sarah W. Holland, Poland Plastic Surgeon

So, if the patient’s weight is close to normal, then obvious fat indications for liposuction can be local fat traps in certain areas. Often in the list of similar zones fall – the area of breeches, belly, flanks, the area of the chin and so on. In other words, we are talking about those areas that persistently “do not want to lose weight” before obtaining the desired contour. These are the fat traps with which neither physical exercises, nor diets, nor massage can cope. Remove fat traps can only with liposuction.

The elasticity of the patient’s skin is important. In people with non-elastic, flabby skin, isolated liposuction can provoke new aesthetic problems – there will be a need to improve the appearance of the skin in the operated area.

For example, in elderly patients with reduced skin elasticity, the result of liposuction in Poland will certainly be worse than in young patients.

This question is discussed with a plastic surgeon, during the examination. According to the indications, an additional operation can be performed to remove excess skin.

Doctor Sirish Maddali, Poland Plastic Surgeon Lipo Procedure One Months Post Op

Before the operation, the patient should be healthy, in order to identify possible contraindications, a certain list of tests must be submitted. It is necessary to inform your doctor about your health, injuries, previous operations, medications taken. Among other things, the role is played by the patient’s psychological stability, and the realism of his expectations.

Do not expect excessive cardinal changes after liposuction, this operation has its limits of opportunity. Liposuction will certainly improve the contours of the body and increase self-esteem, but it can not change you beyond recognition. Before liposuction, necessarily discuss your expectations with a plastic surgeon.

Liposuction can be indicated as a single operation, or in combination with other surgical procedures.

Liposuction is contraindicated in patients with diabetes mellitus, heart and lung diseases, patients with obesity, with a marked breach of skin elasticity. The operation also can not be done if in the recent past the zone proposed for surgery has already been operated or traumatized.

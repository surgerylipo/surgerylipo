Liposuction New Orleans Prices

One of the most popular plastic surgery is liposuction. This surgery involves removing fat cells from different areas of the body in different ways.

The method is chosen by the attending physician at the examination stage of the patient.

Remember: liposuction is not a way to lose weight! Those clinics that guarantee in one session getting rid of 26 lb 7 oz of excess weight are misleading their potential patients.

With the removal of only 1.32 gallon of fat, the body’s blood loss is up to 30%, which requires strict control and therapeutic observation for a long time.

Types of liposuction and indications

This procedure is prescribed with accumulated fat in certain areas, which negatively affects a person’s body and his health. There are several methods:

  • Removal of fat deposits by ultrasound occurs by breaking the fat layer by means of a special device, as well as the subsequent removal of the liquid by vacuum.
  • The radio frequency method is designed to use high-frequency currents.
  • Laser liposuction is the most common way in which fat is split by exposure to it with a beam of laser light.For any type of liposuction, the fluid is removed by vacuum and only in isolated cases by mechanical suction with a syringe.

Process and result

Like any other surgical intervention, the preparation for liposuction in New Orleans requires attention, and does not tolerate haste. After consultation with the surgeon, the patient undergoes a complete examination and the to hove tests, as well as the chosen method for removing fat cells, which determines the service.

Lipoplasty Is Not Intended As A Weight Loss Surgery

Stages

The initial stage consists of preparing fat deposits to remove them from the body – Klein solution, vibration, ultrasound, laser or current. Further, the doctor makes microscopic incisions, through which, with the help of cannulas and vacuum suction, fat as a liquid is aspirated.

If it is necessary to aspirate a small volume of cells (up to 6 lb), a mechanical technique is used.

The postoperative period means the first days of being in the hospital (the term is determined by the doctor), as well as compliance with a number of requirements:

  • 1 month is necessary to wear a compression garment;
  • Strict adherence to diet;
  • a minimum of physical activity;
  • Exclude alcohol and smoking, as well as too high temperatures (beach, sauna, bath).

Tip: To prevent re-growth of fat mass, a proper diet should be observed, since liposuction is not an irreversible procedure. This is only a temporary push for further human actions.

Area of influence

Liposuction is allowed on any part of the body. Everything depends on the situation and the case. Liposuction of the abdomen is the most common procedure used by men and women. It is performed as a separate operation or in combination with abdominoplasty, which helps to remove excess skin and tighten the muscles.

Liposuction of the knees is permissible in cases of identifying problem zones on the knee joints, as well as an accurate diagnosis of the cause of the increase in mass solely due to fat cells, and not because of muscle mass and bone structure.

Liposuction of the hips is the top line of the “hit parade” of plastic procedures, to which people with “riding breeches” resort, as well as with disproportionate forms. Often in parallel with liposuction, an operation is needed to excise sagging skin.

Liposuction of legs (shin and calves) is required in cases of the formation of an elongated strand. After the operation, the legs look much slimmer and slimmer, as most women seek.

Liposuction of the chin can be an individual procedure, which is aimed at removing the second chin and excess fat cells. But most often this procedure is carried out in conjunction with liposuction of the face and subsequent lifting of the excess skin formed. After liposuction of the cheeks, face and chin improves the appearance of the skin, and the patient looks a few years younger.

To achieve these goals, you can seek help on liposuction of buttocks, back and sides, as well as arms, chest and shoulders. The need for this and that procedure is determined by the plastic surgeon.

Contraindications

The patient can receive a definitive answer “No” with the following health problems:

  • Unbalanced mental state.
  • Heart disease of any kind.
  • Problems of the endocrine system.
  • Oncological diseases.
  • Diseases of blood vessels.
  • Gastric ulcer, etc.
  • Also, the refusal will be 100% during pregnancy, breastfeeding and the age of 18 years, if the case is not exceptional.

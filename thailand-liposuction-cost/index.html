Thailand Liposuction Cost

Liposuction of the anterior abdominal wall is a technique of destruction and vacuum removal of excess fatty deposits in the abdomen.

Liposuction in Thailand gives the maximum effect with normal elasticity of the skin and the absence of obesity. In addition, its implementation has a long-term aesthetic result.

Liposuction of the anterior abdominal wall is performed under general anesthesia. The duration of the operation is about 3-4 hours.

Surgical liposuction of the abdominal zone is performed by various methods:

Vacuum (standard) liposuction is done with a special grooved cannula, which is introduced into the incision, movements in various directions destroy the fat cells, producing suction by means of a vacuum pump.

This technique allows you to remove up to 200 ml of fat. But at the same time there is a risk of damage to the subcutaneous blood and lymph vessels and nerve endings, which leads to bleeding with the formation of multiple hematomas;

Ultrasound liposuction is performed using a special device that emits ultrasonic waves of a frequency at which destruction of fat tissues occurs, while denser neighboring tissues remain intact. The advantage of the method is that there is no scars on the skin, the fat tissue is destroyed evenly.

However, this painless way of liposuction can be accompanied by complications, such as dehydration of tissues, damage to the skin, vessels, nerves and internal organs;

Ultrasound non-invasive liposuction in Thailand involves the removal of fat without surgery. With ultrasonic liposuction, the destruction of cells is also facilitated by ultrasonic waves that push out triglycerides. These substances excrete the split fats from the body naturally.

The time of one procedure of non-invasive ultrasonic liposuction is slightly more than half an hour. The full course involves 4-8 procedures, which can be repeated with a periodicity of 7-10 days;

Laser liposuction is performed using a thin needle in which the cannula and the light guide are placed.

The diode laser beam selectively, without damaging the surrounding tissues, destroys the fat cells and simultaneously coagulates the vessels and nerve endings.

In addition, due to the stimulation of collagen production by a laser, the technique has a lifting effect. Although this kind of liposuction allows you to remove no more than 0.5 liters of fat – due to the small internal diameter of the cannula.

Technique for liposuction of the anterior abdominal wall

As a rule, before carrying out vacuum, ultrasound or laser liposuction of the anterior abdominal wall, the surgeon makes a marked marking of the removed fatty tissues on the patient’s skin. With vacuum liposuction, the doctor makes several small incisions on the skin into which the cannula is inserted.

Doctor Tanongsak Panyawirunroj, MD, Thailand Plastic Surgeon – 41 Year Old Woman Treated With Liposuction

The reverse end of the cannula is connected to a special vacuum-aspirator device, which creates a negative pressure on the problem area of the abdomen. Then, the translucent fan-shaped movements of the cannula cause a mechanical destruction of the fatty tissue and the subsequent aspiration of the fat.

The final stage of vacuum liposuction of the anterior abdominal wall is the application of cosmetic sutures, which are removed after a week. Bruising, small seals, pain during movement after surgery disappear after two weeks.

In turn, with ultrasonic liposuction of the anterior abdominal wall, the doctor presses a special device on the problem areas, under the action of ultrasonic waves, fat cells are destroyed. After that on the abdomen in problem areas, the surgeon makes small punctures with special needles and the resulting emulsion is removed by suction.

Dr. Tanongsak Panyawirunroj, MD, Thailand Plastic Surgeon – 47 Year Old Woman Treated With Liposuction

When performing ultrasound non-invasive liposuction, the doctor does not make punctures on the abdomen. Also does not conduct anesthesia. Due to blood and lymph, the resulting fat emulsion is excreted from the body with the help of the liver.

As for the technique of laser liposuction of the anterior abdominal wall in Thailand, it is quite simple. Initially, the specialist makes punctures in the problem zones of the abdomen with a special needle, through which the laser processes fat. The doctor moves the needle forward and back, thereby dissolving the fat tissue.

With laser liposuction, one site is treated from forty-five minutes to one hour. After all these types of liposuction of the anterior abdominal wall, the patient is recommended to adhere to a diet for two weeks, not to visit the bath, not to resort to physical exertion.

General information about the fat tissue

The whole body fat mass is divided into several layers, which differ from each other not only by localization, but also by the peculiarities of metabolic processes occurring in adipose tissue:

Subdermal fat layer; The increase in the volume of fat cells against the background of poor elasticity of the vertical connective tissue bridges of the subdermal layer leads to the formation of an uneven contour of the skin in the form of small tubercles.

The development of this state was called “cellulite” or “small-hump lipodystrophy”. Treatment of cellulite in most cases, conservative (see cosmetology). The technique of liposuction of the subdermal fat layer with hypertinar cannulas, as a method of treating cellulite, has not received wide clinical application.

Superficial fat layer; It is located between the skin and muscles. The harmony of outlines and proportions of the body depends on the severity of the fat deposits of this layer. They are located on all parts of the body without exception, considerably varying in density and thickness. This layer can be modified by means of liposuction.

Deep fat layer; Located under the muscular fascia. Its volume is slightly susceptible to decrease with total weight loss.

Visceral fat; Located inside the abdominal cavity. The pronounced hypertrophy of this layer is more typical for men, which manifests itself in the form of large, convex and elastic bellies. Liposuction can not be.

INDICATIONS FOR LIPOSUCTION

Fatty deposits are divided into two types:

Local; Do not respond by changing the volume to diet and exercise. This type is more common in women, has clear contours and a characteristic registration: the outer and upper internal thighs, the inner surfaces of the knees, the waist, the front abdominal wall and the chin area. Local fat deposits are the main indication for liposuction with an aesthetically favorable result.

Generalized or diffuse type of fatty deposits. This type is the result of metabolic disturbances caused by genetic, constitutional factors, abnormal diet and physical activity. In the generalized form of obesity, the volume of fat cells of all layers increases.

This situation is almost useless to solve with the help of liposuction in Nashville, but should be treated with the involvement of endocrinologists and nutritionists. Significant weight loss can be achieved through the methods of bariatric surgery. *

METHOD

There are several types of liposuction in Nashville: ultrasound, mechanical and syringe. With ultrasonic liposuction, the fat tissue is cleaved by ultrasound. For the transplantation of fat cells into another anatomical region (lipofilling), a syringe method of tissue removal is used, in which the fat cells are not injured.

Liposuction can be an independent operation or performed as part of abdominoplasty, breast reduction, facelift and neck. In this case, general anesthesia is used.

Technique of plastic surgery

Liposuction is performed through small incisions, not more than 0.5 cm under operating conditions. This surgical operation is aimed at removing unwanted adipose tissue through a cannula tube by vacuum aspiration. Through the incision, into fatty tissue, the surgeon introduces a special solution, which loosens the adipose tissue. Then the fat is aspirated. The stitches are sewn or glued. At present, ultrasonic and laser liposuction is widely used. Not always these methods are suitable for patients. Consult a plastic surgeon for advice!

Duration of plastic surgery

The duration of the operation depends on the amount of unwanted adipose tissue removal and can last from 25 minutes to several hours.

Hospital stay after liposuction

If the operation was performed under general anesthesia, it is recommended to observe the patient in the hospital for one day.

After operation

In the early postoperative period after surgery, the patient notes some painful sensations that are removed by anelhetics. Sensation of pain tend to disappear within a few days.

Follow all the surgeon’s recommendations to avoid complications and get an excellent result! After liposuction, you must wear special compression clothes. Terms of wearing clothes are determined by a plastic surgeon. After 4-5 days, you can begin to lead an everyday lifestyle.

Not recommended exercise and heavy lifting for a month.

Before talking about the areas where liposuction in is used, it should immediately be noted that it is neither a panacea for obesity, nor a method of rapid weight loss, but only a method of correcting the body shape.

Want to lose excess – lead an active lifestyle and follow a diet. Liposuction is used to, for example, remove excess fatty tissue in the so-called problem areas, which can not be corrected even with the help of intensive exercise and diet.

The most popular procedure in Manila is liposuction of the abdomen. It is followed by liposuction of the hips and buttocks, and then liposuction of the back and legs. It should be noted that this method of correction is used not only for the body.

Types of liposuction in Manila

To date, there are several types of liposuction. However, classical liposuction implies the mechanical treatment of fat deposits with the help of a cannula (a small hollow tube) connected to a vacuum device that sucks the removed tissues. Therefore sometimes the procedure is called “vacuum liposuction”. In the surgical zone, as a rule, a medicinal solution is first introduced to soften the fat deposits, which greatly facilitates the process of their removal.

In addition to liposuction in its classical sense, there are other so-called types. Lately, ultrasonic liposuction, or cavitation, is gaining popularity, which is recommended in the case of treating large areas with a small fat layer, for example, backs.

There is also laser liposuction and a number of techniques for affecting fat cells, which are presented as non-surgical liposuction.

Specialists also distinguish male liposuction. By the way, many of them ascertain the fact that recently more and more often representatives of the strong half of mankind began to resort to the services of plactic surgeons.

Liposuction for men is most often used in the abdominal region and, as a rule, is accompanied by abdominoplasty.

Contraindications to liposuction

The list of contraindications to liposuction includes oncological diseases, liver and kidney function disorders, heart disease, peptic ulcer, diabetes mellitus, blood clotting problems, as well as varicose veins in the area of the proposed operation and hypertension. As for age-related contraindications to liposuction, as such they do not exist. However, it should be borne in mind that the young organism will easily transfer the procedure, the recovery phase will be shorter, and the skin will be able to adapt to new forms.

In addition, in the conduct of liposuction can be denied, if in the proposed area previously conducted surgical operations. In any case, before deciding to surgery, you need to get a detailed consultation of a professional in the field of plastic surgery.

Recovery after liposuction

As a rule, rehabilitation after liposuction occurs quickly and almost painlessly. Of course, a lot depends on the age and complexity of the operation. If the procedure was only half an hour under local anesthesia, on the same day the patient can return home, and the next day start working.

When the operation requires a “general” anesthesia and lasts for several hours, recovery after liposuction will take a couple of weeks or two. In any case, during the first time after the procedure, the patient is under the supervision of doctors. Within two to three weeks, doctors are advised to wear a special compression garment, as in sports can begin no sooner than a month.

The results of liposuction

The biggest advantage of liposuction in Manila is that the fat deposits are removed once and for all. If you are active and do not abuse the eating of buns for the night, you can be sure that the effect of the procedure will last for life. The second significant advantage is that there are almost no traces on the body.

As a rule, incisions are made in places of natural skin folds, or in places hidden by underwear. In addition, over time, cosmetic sutures become almost invisible. And one more plus of liposuction – complications after this procedure are extremely rare, though still happen.

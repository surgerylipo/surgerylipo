Cannulas and the amount of fat removed

Usual liposuction begins with small incisions on the skin, through which fat is removed with the help of small tubes (cannulas). Depending on the contours of the body and the amount of fat removed, cannulas of different sizes are used. As a rule, the operation is performed under general anesthesia.

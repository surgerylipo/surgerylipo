Parotid Fistula, Does Botox Heal?
Effect of botulinum toxin on parotid fistula
Jul 14, 2013
Parotid fistulas generally heal. Botulinum toxin type A can hasten healing by reducing the amount of salivary flow. Articles report treatment periods ranging from 2 weeks to 4 months. Injecting both the parotid and submandibular salivary glands seems to work better than injecting the parotid gland alone. The procedure is very safe.

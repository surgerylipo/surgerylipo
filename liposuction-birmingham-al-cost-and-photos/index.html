The method of ultrasonic liposuction is relatively young, but has already gained a certain popularity is carried out in the clinic. This method is a more gentle method of aesthetic correction of the body than its mechanical analog.

During this procedure, the destruction of fat cells does not occur mechanically, but by affecting the problem areas of ultrasonic waves. The ultrasonic wave is inaudible to the human ear, but it resonates perfectly in the membranes of the fat cells, which causes their destruction.

This method allows you to include in the main zones of lipolysis, not only the stomach, thighs and buttocks, but also the hands. With the help of ultrasonic liposuction, fat from the chin and face can be pumped out. The main advantage of this procedure is a very short recovery period. If the patient had to spend about 10 days in a hospital with mechanical removal of subcutaneous fat, after ultrasound liposuction the patient is discharged the next day.

How does ultrasonic liposuction work?

The effect of ultrasound on conglomerates of fat cells leads to the rapid destruction of their resonating membranes. Destroying, fat acquires a viscous consistency.

Such an emulsion is easier to remove from the body by both lipolysis and (if an ultrasound non-surgical procedure – based on natural intervention – surgery) by usual stimulating massages. With this type of lipolysis, fat is removed by the same method as laser liposuction.

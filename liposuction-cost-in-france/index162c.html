A Correctly Performed Liposuction

After the removal of fat cells, the space they occupied immediately filled with blood and interstitial fluid, which leads to a significant swelling of the tissues. Only after the beginning of the resorption of the liquid (at different times for different zones) there is a decrease in volume.

A good quality of operation is guaranteed in the event that as a result of it the vascular reservoirs did not suffer, and a uniform layer of subcutaneous tissue remained between the skin and the muscle.

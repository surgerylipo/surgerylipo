Liposuction is a modern and quite effective method for correcting the body. With liposuction, local fat volumes can be adjusted to specific problem areas on the patient’s body.

One of the main positive moments is the absence of traces after the procedure after removal of surplus fatty tissue, and if the recommendations of the surgeon and dietician are kept, the effect remains for a long time.

What is important and need to know about liposuction?

Liposuction is not a method of reducing excess weight. You will not become slim after surgery. And, if you do not change eating habits and, the rhythm of life, fat will accumulate elsewhere. Liposuction is a great method of correcting contours of the body. Liposuction can help reduce the volume of the thighs, reduce the tummy, adjust the local deposits of adipose tissue in other parts of the body, but there is no cardinal reduction in body mass.

Where is liposuction performed?

From the point of view of plastic surgery, the zones are most effective for liposuction:

  • The subscapular area;
  • All parts of the abdominal wall;
  • Outer and inner thighs;
  • shin;
  • Back of the shoulder;
  • Chin area;
  • The zone of large pectoral muscles in men with gynecomastia.

Main indications

The main indications for liposuction are the excess fat in the local problem areas. Most often it is: the chin zone, the external and internal surface of the thighs, the anterior abdominal wall and the lateral parts of the abdomen.

And the main thing is not to forget, we are not talking about reducing weight, but about correcting the shape of this or that zone.

Similarly, the liposuction technique can complement the operations of a tummy tuck (abdominoplasty).

Since if you remove excess fat from the anterior abdominal wall, then your tummy will look much more attractive and aesthetic.

Contraindications for liposuction

  • Chronic diseases in the stage of decompensation;
  • With oncological diseases;
  • Problems with the blood coagulation system;
  • Overweight (if you want to get rid of it only with the help of liposuction)
  • Acute respiratory conditions on the day of surgery;
  • Pregnancy and lactation.

Methods of anesthesia during the procedure

The operation can be carried out, both under local and under general anesthesia. It is important to remember that the method of anesthesia is solved after consultation with a plastic surgeon and anesthesiologist and is selected individually in each specific case.

How is liposuction performed in London?

Before carrying out the liposuction, a special Klein solution is injected into the area of the presumed removal of fatty tissue, containing a physical solution, lidocaine and adrenaline, soda, thus achieving better fat removal and reducing the risk of hematomas after surgery.

Then, through the puncture, which is done in places of natural folds or in places covered with underwear, a cannula is inserted, connected by means of a silicone tube with a special device – a vacuum aspirator.

Fat under negative pressure is removed from the patient’s body. As with any plastic surgery, the experience and professionalism of the plastic surgeon is important, as with an inadvertent movement of the cannula, internal tissues can be injured, and with uneven fat removal, deformation of the body contours can be.

Unfortunately, the formation of bruises and swelling after the operation is inevitable, but they, most often, resolve in a couple of weeks, and tissue swelling may gradually disappear for up to two to three months.

Recovery

Most often, with small amounts of liposuction, patients leave the clinic on the day of the operation, and if the removal of fatty tissue was on significant surfaces, it takes 1-2 days to stay in the clinic after surgery.

Compression underwear is a mandatory attribute after liposuction, which patients should wear. Special drugs and ointments are also prescribed to speed up the recovery process.

In the process of postoperative recovery, it is very important to conduct physiotherapeutic procedures, such as pressotherapy, vacuum-roller massage, medical wraps, lymph drainage massage. All this complex of measures allows achieving quick recovery and better cosmetic result.

When can I see the results of liposuction?

The fact that the decrease in the number of adipose tissue can be seen immediately. But the results of liposuction can be estimated beforehand 2-3 months after the operation. During this period, the edema disappears, the seals in the area of operation dissolve, and the wound healing takes place.

The final result can be estimated after 5-8 months. During this period, elasticity is restored in the operated zone, possible irregularities disappear, and the operated zone acquires the final form.

The liposuction performed according to the correct indications can adjust the contour of the body quite well and allows solving the problem of local deposits of fatty tissue. Certainly, the surgeon’s experience in many respects influences the result of the operation.

Benefits of liposuction

Liposuction, at present, is one of the most popular plastic surgery for body correction.

By popularity, it can compete with the installation of breast implants for breast augmentation. The popularity of liposuction is determined by:

  • high efficiency of the operation
  • fairly predictable effect
  • Relative safety
  • Short postoperative period
  • almost absence of scars
  • Long and stable effect.

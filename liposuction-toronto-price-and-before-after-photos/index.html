Liposuction Toronto Price And Before After Photos

Liposuction in Toronto is an operation of plastic surgery aimed at correcting the shape of the body by eliminating excess fat tissue in certain places.

Lipoaspiration is one of the most effective methods of correcting the body and improving the contours of the body, avoiding high invasiveness.

The visual effect of liposuction is much greater than the loss of a similar volume of adipose tissue by other methods. Normal weight loss with diet or exercise does not allow you to eliminate fat in certain areas.

The areas with which the surgeon works with liposuction are very different for men and women. However, most of all, regardless of the sex, you have to work with belly zones, sides, hips, neck, cheeks.

Separately, it is worth mentioning the correction of mammary glands in the woman.

Often, the structure of the body is determined not only by the impact of momentary factors, but also by genetic predisposition.

The formation of some fatty structures occurs precisely for this reason, and eliminate them by the usual methods (strict diet, exercise) is simply impossible or very difficult.

It is in this situation, in fact, the only option is liposuction, since this method allows you to eliminate fat from anywhere in the body. It should be noted that lipoaspiration is not intended to combat obesity and excess weight.

First, during the procedure, a relatively small amount of fat is eliminated. Secondly, the surgeon only works with a small part of the body area. Thirdly, liposuction does not eliminate the root cause of obesity.

Contraindications for liposuction take into account the following factors:

  • problems with blood coagulability, including hemophilia;
  • violation of blood circulation in the area of the procedure;
  • active skin diseases in the zone of the forthcoming procedure;
  • The recently transferred surgical intervention in the zone of impending liposuction;
  • general obesity (excess weight over 9lb);
  • the consequences of rapid weight loss leading to the formation of significant skin surpluses;
  • obesity, formed due to endocrine disorders;

  • pronounced impairment of kidney and liver function;
  • diabetes;
  • stomach ulcers;
  • thrombophlebitis – thrombosis, accompanied by inflammation of the vein wall and the subsequent formation of a thrombus closing the venous lumen;
  • Varicose veins in the area of the proposed operation;
  • disorders of cardiac activity (heart disease accompanied by defects of the interventricular or interatrial septum);
  • pronounced arterial hypertension;
  • chronic diseases in the acute stage;
  • acute infectious and viral diseases;
  • diseases of internal organs;
  • strokes and myocardial infarction;
  • period of pregnancy, breast-feeding;
  • low elasticity of the skin (age, acquired for other reasons).
  • diseases of the oncology sphere.

The patient should understand that liposuction is not among the procedures aimed at radical weight reduction, this is a corrective operation. As a rule, the volume of the removed fatty tissue does not exceed 0.79 gallon.

For this reason, people with a significant excess weight are not recommended for this procedure – it is necessary to get rid of fat with the help of diets and physical activity. You may need skin tightening. If obesity is caused by endocrine problems, it is first of all necessary to eliminate them, otherwise the result of correction will be very quickly leveled.

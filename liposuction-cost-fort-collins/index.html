Indications and contraindications for surgery

Liposuction of the legs, thighs, knees, arms is performed after a thorough medical examination, because one desire of a person to quickly remove fat under the skin of the face, from the sides, legs, and also from the inside of the thigh is not enough.

In order to do liposuction of the face, cheeks and chin, you must be completely healthy. In both men and women, subcutaneous fat on the body, abdomen, legs and face is removed, provided that there are no contraindications.

Do not expect that liposuction in Fort Collins will help you get rid of excess weight. Its task is to remove local deposits of fat.

How to get rid of subcutaneous fat: the procedure for performing the operation

The very principle of performing liposuction clearly demonstrates how you can quickly remove the internal subcutaneous fat from all areas of the body: from the inner parts of the hips, the sides, the chest (pectoral muscles), the lower abdomen, knees, face, legs, shoulders, axillae, press, arms, buttocks, under the arms, from the back and abdomen. The main instrument of the surgeon during the operation is blunt cannulas. They are needles with a hollow structure. Through small holes in the skin, fat is sucked off with a vacuum device. In this regard, this type of liposuction is called vacuum.

Tumescent liposuction in Fort Collins is similar to the vacuum method, but before the intervention the doctor injects under the skin a special solution that helps to narrow the blood vessels and swell the fatty tissue, which facilitates its removal.

The ultrasonic method allows to soften fatty tissue by using a special ultrasonic probe and can easily be sucked by cannulas.

Recovery after removal of subcutaneous fat

If you decide to remove excess fat from the abdomen, be prepared for the fact that you have to intervene under general, less often – local, anesthesia, lasting from half an hour to two hours.

Each puncture involves suturing. Sutures are removed on the 7th day, while in the hospital it will be necessary to stay from 2 to 3 days.

Within a month, you need to wear special compression underwear and avoid serious physical exertion. Painful sensations are almost absent, but outwardly there are edemas for some time, which soon disappear.

Sunnyvale, CA Liposuction

Liposuction: the essence of manipulation.

In this procedure, there are three main stages.

From the beginning, the subcutaneous fat is infiltrated with a special solution (Klein solution), then the fat is processed by one of the physical factors (high frequency current, vibration, liquid jet, ultrasound) that destroy the subcutaneous fat and release it and directly the aspiration itself.

High-frequency electronic lipo modelling is a minimally invasive technique that allows the removal of excesses of subcutaneous fat throughout the body.

This technique due to the effect of high frequency current and the use of thin cannulas is not very traumatic for the patient.

For carrying out the manipulation, small punctures with an average diameter of 2 mm are used!

You can not worry about scars, because they simply do not exist or they are located in the natural folds of the skin and are completely invisible.

After the anesthesia of the puncture site, the puncture itself is made, through which a thin cannula is inserted and a Klein solution is applied to the intended area, carrying out local anesthesia and preliminary emulsification (dilution) of adipose tissue.

Dr. Joel B. Beck, Sunnyvale Plastic Surgeon Liposuction Recovery

After infiltration of the solution, the fatty tissue is processed by a high frequency current that the patient does not feel. And only after that, the destroyed fatty tissue is removed outside by the same cannulas.

At the same time, due to the “softness” of the liposuction technique used in Sunnyvale, the blood vessels are not damaged.

The current of high frequency does not cause burns of surrounding tissues and besides it has a lifting effect on the skin, due to which there are no problems with sagging of the skin. A new approach to figure correction opens water-jet liposuction.

To implement this principle, the body-jet device was created, which gently separates the fat cells from the connective tissue with minimal traumatization of the patient’s tissues.

The principle of the innovative body-jet device of hyman-med company is based on the use of the water jet force, representing a completely new approach to liposuction and correction of the body as a whole. Unlike traditional methods, when using the body-jet device, a jet of water emits and removes fat cells while maintaining the integrity of the blood vessels and nerves.

Thus, traumatization of the patient’s tissues is minimal. This manipulation is safe, effective and easy to implement. With its help, you can get excellent results with minimal risk and a short postoperative period for patients.

Complications.

The most common complications after liposuction in Sunnyvale are: intradermal hemorrhage (bruise); reduced sensitivity of the skin, puffiness of the treated area; edema in the region of the lower leg and foot; seroma.

Dr. Robert M. Lowen, Sunnyvale Plastic Surgeon Lipo Of Arms

The bruise disappears within 7-12 days, the reduced sensitivity of the skin is restored in a month, minor puffiness can last up to a month and a half.

Swelling in the region of the lower leg and foot are usually a consequence of wearing compression underwear and after it is removed disappear on their own.

Seroma is a congestion of serous fluid in a limited area of the treated area. It is rare and mostly after the removal of significant amounts of fat.

The treatment involves 2-3 punctures to evacuate the serous fluid and in a longer time to wear compression underwear.

Postoperative period.

The main task of the patient in the postoperative period is a clear implementation of the doctor’s recommendations, which will help to avoid complications and get a good aesthetic result.

During this period, it will be necessary to wear special compression underwear for 3 weeks, it is possible to take a full shower from 3 days, baths and saunas are not recommended for 1-1.5 months, physical exercises are possible 2-3 weeks after operation.

It is not recommended to get direct sunlight on the treated areas for 1 month.

The result is noticeable immediately after the operation, but the final effect will appear after 2-3 months. Redistribution of subcutaneous fat deposits after lipoaspiration does not occur, i.e. if fat is removed in the region of the thighs, then it will no longer be in another area.

Changes in the body are stable and generally do not need correction anymore. The amount of removable subcutaneous fat is not limited by the technique, but is determined in each specific case by the volume that must be removed in order to achieve an aesthetic result.

It is also important to understand that liposuction is not a method of combating obesity, but a way of the body correction.

The term of stay in the hospital is 1 day, if the volume of the operation is small, the patient can be discharged on the same day. In addition, eliminating the disproportion of the body shape will improve your psychological state, and, in general, the quality of life.

The main types of surgical liposuction

Laser liposuction involves the destruction of fat cells by the energy of the laser beam. The best application of this technique is the correction of small parts of the body with a delicate skin, including face and neck.

Water-jet liposuction is considered to be the least traumatic variant of the operation, it allows one to remove up to 6 liters of fat at a time.

It is carried out with the help of a directed jet of a special solution, which is fed to the desired area under high pressure.

Radiofrequency liposuction Body Tite. Here the effect is achieved with the help of directed radio wave energy. Due to this, in addition to correction of fat deposits, there is a moderate skin tightening.

The main types of non-surgical liposuction

Cryolipolysis on the device Zeltiq is a simulation of the contours of the body with the combined effect of low temperature and vacuum allows to perform selective correction of practically any problem zones with minimal trauma.

Ultrasonic liposuction. The surgeon acts on the fat cells with sound waves, which makes the procedure almost painless and relieves the patient from unnecessary blood loss and long-lasting hematomas.

It is believed that this kind of liposuction is best suited for removing fat deposits from the abdomen, hips and sides.

Injectable non-surgical liposuction (injections of lipolitics). The drug is injected into the problem area, which destroys the membranes of fat cells, after which their contents are excreted from the body naturally. It has limited indications and suitable not for everybody.

How is surgical liposuction performed?

During the operation, the plastic surgeon uses special blunt cannulae – needles with a hollow structure, through which the fat is sucked off with a vacuum device. Before this, the doctor injects into the skin a special solution that dilutes the fatty tissue and narrows the blood vessels. This greatly facilitates the removal of fat.

Recovery after liposuction in Sacramento

If you decide on surgical liposuction, remember that the procedure is under general anesthesia in the operating room, and lasts from 1 to 2-3 hours. Each puncture of the abdomen for the cannula means the subsequent imposition of a cosmetic suture in this place. Sutures are removed after 7-9 days. The patient can stay in the hospital for 3 days.Within one month the patient must strictly follow all the prescriptions of the treating doctor. Outwardly swelling may occur at the site of liposuction.

Indications for liposuction in Tacoma

In the life of every woman comes the moment when she finds in her figure more and more flaws. And in most cases, claims to your body are absolutely fair.

Stronger workouts in the gym and tight diets do not help to reduce volumes in problem areas. Those who in desperate attempts to “lose weight in places” were defeated, turn to plastic surgeons to do liposuction and get rid of fat forever.

Modern methods of body shaping

Plastic surgery does not stand still, it evolves. In liposuction for the past 20-30 years, there was a big breakthrough – from the traumatic and dangerous methods of “pumping out” fat plastic surgeons smoothly switched to ultramodern techniques for modeling the body.

We will dwell on the most popular liposuction techniques that are being performed in various clinics today.

Laser liposuction

The possibilities of the laser in aesthetic medicine have been appreciated for a long time.

Laser treatment proved to be very effective in combating excess fat tissue, which disrupts the harmonious proportions of the body, localizing in problem areas.

Laser liposuction is the destruction of fat cells under the influence of a laser beam.

With laser liposuction, you can remove the abdomen, reduce the waist and hip circumference, get rid of the sides on the back and “cushions” in the knee area. For facial correction (reduction of cheeks, elimination of the second chin), two-wavelength laser lipolysis is used, which carefully corrects these delicate zones.

Water jet liposuction in Tacoma

Post Liposuction Procedure Results With Doctor Robert M. Grenley, Tacoma Plastic Surgeon

In addition to laser liposuction, water-jet liposuction is also popular. Here the key point is that the fat cells are literally “washed out” by a stream of liquid. Water-jet liposuction has a sparing effect on fat cells, these “living” cells, which have retained their integrity, find application in lipofilling. After processing, they simulate those zones that, due to age-related causes or sudden weight loss, have lost volume.

RF liposuction on Body Tite

However, when it comes to bulk fat deposits in patients with problem skin (inelastic, with a bad turgor), resorting to laser or water-jet liposuction techniques would be risky. If you remove a large amount of fat at a time, then the skin, deprived of the opportunity to contract, will hang, leading to such a deplorable result as the effect of the washing board. To avoid this, plastic surgeons conduct radiofrequency liposuction (RF liposuction).

The surgeon conducts radiofrequency liposuction on the Body Tite. Due to the capabilities of this technique, it is possible to simultaneously eliminate excess fat and tighten the skin by warming it up to a certain temperature.

RF liposuction thus completely eliminates the risk of skin sagging and allows one to remove an impressive amount of fat in one operation, which can not be done during laser or water-jet liposuction.

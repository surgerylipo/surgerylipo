Is this swelling normal after hyaluronic injection?
swelling with fillers
Feb 1, 2015
Swelling is completely normal after injections. Some patients experience more swelling than others It will likely resolve in 2 days but may take 2weeks. Antihistamines such as allegra can help. Ice is helpful. Head elevation while sleeping helps too.
Does number of passes per area during Fraxel restore treatment for acne scaring affect the final
fraxel acne scars
Feb 1, 2015
Fraxel restore laser resurfacing is the best treatment for acne scarring. The best results happen after 4-5 treatments at least. The higher percentage of skin treated per treatment will achieve faster results and also correlates with discomfort during treatment and postprocedure swelling. The laser can be set to cover the desired skin coverage and is usually 8passes.

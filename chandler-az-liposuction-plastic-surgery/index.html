Chandler, AZ Liposuction Plastic Surgery

Liposuction in Chandler is a surgical correction of the body contours due to the removal of fat deposits by vacuum suction.

In general, liposuction is used for local forms of obesity associated with the violation of body contours.

Most often in women, there is a deformity of the thighs, fat deposits on the inner lateral surface of the knee joint, deposits in the thigh, anterior and lateral surfaces of the abdomen.

The essence of the operation is the surgical correction of the contours of the body due to the removal of fatty deposits.

With the help of vacuum suction through small incisions of the skin eliminate local deposits of fat. There are several types of operation.

Types of liposuction in Chandler

Vacuum liposuction. This is the oldest method. The surgeon works by hand, and very much depends on his skill.

Through small incisions, special instruments are inserted under the skin, subcutaneous fat is diluted and then removed by vacuum (sucked off). The method is traumatic: it is assumed that within two months after the operation the patient will wear specially underwear, bandages.

Ultrasonic liposuction in Chandler. During this operation, the fat is destroyed by ultrasound. The method allows you to remove a large amount of fat, but causes thermal damage to tissues. After it, the healing also goes quite slowly, and special underwear should be worn for up to a month.

Soft-liposuction. The most modern development, whose name translates as “sparing lipomodeling.” Fat is destroyed by high-frequency currents and is removed through tiny punctures. Bandages are recommended to be worn for two weeks after the operation. This method does not require a hospital stay: you can go to work two or three days after the operation.

However, it should be remembered that whatever method you decide to use to correct the deficiencies of the body, it is not recommended to remove at a time more than two liters of adipose tissue. If you delete more, there is a so-called “pituitary response”: the body, “loss” of the usual body volume, feverishly will recover it.

Surgeons do not recommend doing liposuction with expressed excess weight. If you lose weight and want to “finish” your work by surgical modeling of the body, maintain a constant weight for at least six months, and only then decide on the operation.

Liposuction Can Help You Get The Body You Want

Where powerless medicine?

However, this does not mean that liposuction should be neglected. Simply it should be used for the intended purpose: do not require the surgeon to lose weight for you. The surgeon can only correct some mistakes of nature – reduce the excessive volume of the hips, change the shape of the bust, and remove the sagging stomach.

By the way, it is necessary to remember that liposuction will not help to eliminate cellulite or stretch marks. The surgeon will reduce the volume of the thigh, but give it the desired elasticity and smoothness, he can not. Conversely, the presence of stretch marks can limit the amount of fat removed, since stretch marks reduce skin contractility.

Nevertheless, it can not be denied that sometimes liposuction is used in the treatment of obesity – in this case, fat suction is used as a measure of despair and does not at all seek to correct the contour of the body. If the conservative methods of treatment are ineffective (diet, drug therapy, fitness), serial liposuction is performed, aimed not at eliminating body contour disorders, but not at reducing the body weight of the patient.

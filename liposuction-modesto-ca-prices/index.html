How to lose weight by a simpler method? Liposuction is usually considered just a method of losing weight for the lazy.

After all, it seems that everything is simple: in the morning you come to the clinic, and by the evening you are already a happy owner of an ideal body, and if so, you can afford to enjoy a harmful, but such delicious food.

In fact, everything is not quite so simple. Of course, liposuction is a very popular and very effective procedure, but nothing but daily work on yourself can not make you an ideal body, and the result will be noticeable not immediately.

Only after two or three months, the rehabilitation period will end, and you will see the final effect in six months.

In addition, it is important to remember that liposuction is not able to solve problems with excess weight, it can only correct the proportions of the body.

So, let’s take a closer look at what actually means liposuction in Modesto and what types of this procedure can offer you cosmetic clinics and plastic surgery centers. Types of Liposuction of the abdomen

Vacuum

This is exactly what we usually mean when we hear about liposuction in a conversation. The procedure is a common but rather serious plastic surgery.

Experts of aesthetic medicine recommend it only in really serious cases, when a lot of excess weight, the body looks disproportionate and, most importantly, it worries the patient.

The essence of the operation is that incisions are made on the abdomen, into which special ultrathin and plastic tubes called cannulas are introduced, through which the surgeon removes excess fat with the help of vacuum.

Such a procedure has a number of serious drawbacks: it is possible to see the final result only after six months, although small scars will remain, it will be necessary to wear a special bandage, maintain the correct diet, most patients will face pain and other unpleasant sensations after the operation.

Tumescent

Today, all surgical operations are trying to do with the minimum number of incisions and punctures, so the appearance of tumescent liposuction did not become a surprise in the world of medicine.

This technique has become quite a logical continuation of the already developed method. The main difference between such a procedure and a vacuum one is the presence of micro-punctures instead of incisions.

In the rest everything is the same: ultra-thin cannulas are injected under the skin, with the help of which excess fat is removed. On average, a person loses 0.79 gal of fat tissue. The cost of liposuction is much lower and starts from $ 1200 to $ 2500, but also has less and the number of possible complications. To date, most plastic surgeons recommend it.

Laser liposuction in Modesto

Today is one of the most popular cosmetic procedures. Technology is constantly being improved. Today, in most clinics, laser liposuction combines light and thermal effects, which greatly enhances the effect.

The main advantage is that it is a cosmetological and non-invasive procedure, that is, it does not involve punctures or incisions. After a couple of days, full recovery begins. The effect is also noticeable immediately.

An essential advantage of laser procedures is an additional bonus, which you get: skin lifting. In addition, laser abdominal liposuction will reduce you in volume, it will also tighten your abdominal skin, make it more elastic.

Mini Liposuction Or Mini Tummy Tuck

Cosmetic surgery is the solution for stubborn fat deposits that won’t go away no matter how hard you work out or go on diet.

However, liposuction is a risky procedure and you should consider alternatives and talk to doctors before taking a decision.

Regarding a liposuction alternative, one of the trends in plastic surgery nowadays is mini liposuction, a smaller version of traditional liposuction that targets a smaller part of the body and sucks out less than 3 lbs of fat.

Think of double chin, knees, ankles, arms as preferred body parts to treat with a miniliposuction.

Mini liposuction is also performed on larger body parts that have that little extra fat.

This is especially true for the stomach. If no matter how many crunches and sit ups you do, love handles are still there, a mini liposuction is what you need.

But then, you research possible surgical methods and a question arises: “Mini liposuction or mini tummy tuck”?

A mini tummy tuck is a procedure that removes saggy, excess, skin and fat from the tummy area and tightens the abdominals. The procedure starts with a little incision just above the pubic area. Saggy skin will be cut away together with fat.

The procedure is not comparable with (mini) liposuction in terms of fat removal as the liposuction procedure targets only fat but in larger amount and sucks out also the fat underneath the skin and tissues.

In fact, sometimes a liposuction is performed before a tummy tuck. In that case the tummy tuck is done only to remove the saggy skin left by the loss of the fat deposits. After a mini tummy tuck, there’s no need to reposition the belly button as after a “full” tummy tuck.

Mini tummy tuck is performed in local anesthesia with mild sedation and the recovery period is 1 to 3 week to return to full activity, while for light activity just 2-3 days are enough. A compression garment must be worn for 2-3 weeks. This is to prevent from infection and to adjust the tightened skin.

As you can see a mini tummy tuck is a little more invasive than a mini liposuction, although both are less invasive and therefore safer than a traditional liposuction or tummy tuck. In a mini tummy tuck the incision is larger than the one in a mini liposuction, which is almost invisible and scarring is something to think about.

Now that you know what a mini tummy tuck is for you should understand which procedure you need. If your problem is some lose, saggy skin around the stomach area, go for a mini tummy tuck. If you have fat in the area but not excess skin, a liposuction is what you need.

A mini tummy tuck is often performed after a massive weight loss, or after a pregnancy. The skin that has been stretched by the fat or due to pregnancy becomes saggy if the stretching cause is eliminated.

Mini tummy tuck prices vary a lot. The procedure is more invasive than a mini liposuction and go from $2,000 to $5,500. Being a cosmetic surgery, insurance do not cover the cost of the intervention.

Tummy Tuck Prices: Why Do They Vary?

Are you looking for different tummy tuck prices so you can effectively compare them? Good for you. The more there is the better because it would likely mean that you will get the best offer available. However, surgeons seldom give quotes because tummy tuck prices can change in no time especially with new technology and innovation that constantly come out. Prices also vary from patient to patient as well as from country to country.

Basically, the price depends on factors such as the patient’s needs, the type of procedure done, the credibility of the doctor, the clinic’s location, and the facilities of the clinic. Therefore, a tummy tuck cost can range from as low as a few hundreds to a couple of thousands of dollars.

The complexity of the cosmetic procedure will generally depend on the amount of work that needs to be done as well as the patient’s body size. Of course, the larger the size of the patient means that there will be a larger area that needs to be worked on hence a higher fee is expected.

Just like with availing new liposuction treatments, the type of cosmetic technique used is also a factor because there is a difference when it comes to choosing either the traditional method or the newer method like the mini tummy tuck. The traditional tummy tuck method is more expensive because the surgery is more extensive and more invasive. This is the more appropriate choice for patients who require more tissue removal.

Mini tummy tuck prices are less expensive because the procedure is simpler and only involves minor surgery. This mini liposuction like procedure is ideal for patients who only require less tissue removal. Other important factors that you should consider when canvassing prices for tummy tuck procedures include the experience of the doctor as well as the location of the clinic.

The more reputable and popular the doctor is, the more expensive it will be for you. As they say, the best always comes with a price, especially if this doctor lives thousands of miles away from you. Other than the costs of the actual procedure, you also need to consider fare and accommodation if you decide to get the procedure done overseas. Moreover, the best doctors also use the best equipment. That, too, is another additional cost for you.

Shopping for tummy tuck prices can be a stressful activity. To lessen the burden, you can ask for referrals or check various web sites where client reviews are dependable and unbiased. Eventually, you will get the best tummy tuck price that works well for your needs and your budget.

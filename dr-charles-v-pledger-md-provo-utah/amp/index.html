3 months post op. Is this the start of capsular contracture?
Start of capsular contracture
Jul 13, 2015
It sounds like capsular contracture could be starting. If you haven't already, you should be doing some form of massage to stretch the pocket. When the pocket starts to tighten it can cause some pain. If the massage doesn't work, then you will have to surgically open the scar tissue, but if caught soon enough, with your physician's help there's a good chance it can be resolved without surgery.You should see your surgeon as soon as possible.

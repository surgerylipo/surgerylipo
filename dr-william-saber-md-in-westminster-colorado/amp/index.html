Is a leaking saline implant dangerous and when should I have it replaced?
Jul 24, 2014
After a deflation of a saline (salt water) filled implant breaks, the salt water is harmlessly absorbed by the body. Removal of a deflated saline shell is generally a straight forward procedure and can be performed by any Board Certified Plastic Surgeon. Leaving a deflated implant in for a prolong period of time, generally does not make the surgery more complicated and has no immediate risk to your health. However it is recommended to eventually have the deflated device removed.

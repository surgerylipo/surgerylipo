Types of liposuction

The first operations to remove fat were carried out through the thick labia without the introduction of a special solution (“dry” method) in the subcutaneous fat layer.

Within a short time, this method had to be abandoned because of increased bleeding tissues and the appearance of irregularities, which negate all the advantages of carrying out an aesthetic procedure for correcting the contours of the body.

Dr Brett Edward Lehocky, MD, Bakersfield Plastic Surgeon – Liposuction

The use of solutions based analgesics and vasoconstrictors possible to eliminate these shortcomings essentially reduce the trauma and prevent bleeding. The type of liposuction that involves injecting Klein solution is called tumescent.

But plastic surgery did not stand still and after a while new methods of liposuction appeared with the use of laser, water-jet, ultrasound and radio-frequency effects.

Nevertheless, tumescent liposuction remained the most common, although the operation itself underwent significant changes.

Carrying out tumescent liposuction in Bakersfield

  1. Preoperative examination (5-7 days before the procedure);
  2. Introduction of a large volume of a special solution into the subcutaneous fat tissue;
  3. Waiting for the completion of the exposure process (up to 20 minutes) – spasmodic blood vessels;
  4. Removal of fat through a high-pressure vacuum pump through thin cannulas.

The use of thin tubes for pumping fat has significantly reduced the traumatic nature of the operation, eliminating the possibility of irregularities.

Also tumescent method allowed to reduce the terms of the recovery period, which allows you to enjoy the aesthetic effect of the procedure much earlier.

Liposuction areas

The zone of liposuction is a conditional concept and according to generally accepted norms it corresponds to the size of the palm of the doctor.

Most often, liposuction is performed on the abdomen (4 zones), thighs (2 zones on the inside and 2 or 4 with the outside) and waist (2 zones). However, in addition to these areas, liposuction can be performed:

  • Shins;
  • Backs;
  • The knees;
  • Mammary glands;
  • Chin;
  • Shoulder;
  • Neck.

In most cases, the number of zones is paired, since the adjustment of the contours of the body assumes a symmetrical performance. But there are exceptions, for example, the pubic area, which represents only 1 zone.

Liposuction Is A Procedure Performed Under General Or Twilight Anesthesia

Liposuction

Liposuction is used for complex correction of body contours. For this, it can be combined with abdominoplasty of the upper abdomen and waist, or facelift and neck. Excellent results can be achieved with the elimination of X-shaped deformation of the legs.

Due to liposuction of the inside of the thighs and knees, you can achieve a visual effect of harmony of the legs. Incredible effect ensures the operation in the area of “riding breeches.” The appearance of the patients after surgery radically changes for the better.

Depending on the number of treated areas and the volume of fat removed, surgical intervention can be performed under general or local anesthesia.

Before the beginning of the procedure marking of the treated areas is carried out.

They are saturated with Klein’s solution and after the exposure is completed they undergo mechanical destruction of the fatty layer and remove its excess. When the operation is completed, neat seams are superimposed on small functional incisions (no more than 5 mm).

Recovery period

After liposuction in Bakersfield, patients can wear only compression underwear for several weeks. The amount of fat removed depends on the tasks and can range from 0.05 to 1.32 gallon.

The duration of the postoperative period depends directly on the amount of fat removed. Therefore, surgeons of our medical center in such cases recommend conducting several operations with a certain break.

The recovery period begins with hospitalization. The duration of in-patient observation depends on the volume of pumped fat and in most cases is 1 or 2 days.

In cases of outpatient liposuction under local anesthesia, hospitalization is not required at all, so within 4 hours you can return home. Stitches applied to the puncture sites of the skin and are removed after 4-5 days. You can return to the usual active life in a week.

The main criteria for planning liposuction:

  • Number of zones;
  • Volume of fat removed;
  • Nature of the treated areas.

The duration of the surgery (up to 1 hour), the need for hospitalization, the safety procedures, the terms of the recovery period and much more on this depends.

If you want to achieve the maximum result and at the same time avoid complications, follow all instructions of the surgeon of the clinic. In some cases, in pursuit of rapid results, patients tend to undergo liposuction of a large number of zones, but such a sharp adjustment can lead to unpleasant consequences in the form of excess skin, folds and wrinkles.

Complications after liposuction

One of the most common problems encountered after liposuction is the manifestation of uneven contours in the area of exposure or asymmetry in naturally-symmetrical areas.

Thanks to the use of thin cannulas, the great experience of our specialists and modern equipment, the risk of these complications is minimized. But even then, the problems are completely eliminated by a single adjustment of the contours of the body 4-6 months after the operation.

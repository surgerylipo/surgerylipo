The purpose of liposuction and lipomodeling is to improve the shape of the body.

It consists in the vacuum removal of local excesses of subcutaneous fat tissue after their mechanical destruction.

Most often liposuction in Pompano Beach, FL is exposed to the pelvis, hips, abdomen and chin in women, chest and abdomen in men.

The purpose of liposuction is to improve the shape of the body, which is transmitted to us genetically.

Our genes control the distribution of fat cells in the body, cause fat deposits, especially local – “fat traps.” It is impossible to remove them with diet and exercise.

Patients with localized fat deposits are the most suitable candidates for liposuction, although theoretically this method can remove subcutaneous fat from any part of the body.

Favorite places of “fat traps” are the pelvis, hips, abdomen, chin in women, chest and abdomen in men.

  • Liposuction of the hips is the removal of excess fatty deposits on the inner, anterior and lateral surfaces of the thighs.
  • Liposuction of the abdomen is the removal of excess fatty deposits of the anterior abdominal wall, more often near the umbilical region.
  • Liposuction of the chin is the removal of the “second chin” – excess fatty deposits in the chin area.
  • Liposuction is not a method of fighting general obesity.

The index of skin elasticity affects the result of liposuction in Pompano Beach, FL.

In younger people, the elastic skin is able to contract well, which makes it possible to obtain a smooth, even surface, limited only to liposuction.

In different parts of the body, the elasticity of the skin is different, so often liposuction is combined with excision of excess and skin tightening.

Preparation for surgery

Before the operation, patients undergo general clinical examination. At the consultation, patients’ wishes, realistic expectations and surgical interventions are examined.

The nature and location of fat deposits, the elasticity of the skin, the amount of surgical intervention are determined.

For 2 weeks before liposuction in Pompano Beach, FL, stop taking drugs that increase the bleeding of tissues.

On the eve of the operation and directly on the day of operation, tranquilizers (sedatives) are prescribed.

Operation

Depending on the amount of intervention, the operation can be performed under local anesthesia, a combination of mild anesthesia and local anesthesia, as well as under a spinal blockade. The time of operation also depends on the volume of fat removed.

The essence of liposuction is the following: a special solution is introduced into the fat layer to be removed, which contains substances for anesthetizing, narrowing of the vessels and dilution of fat. Through small incisions (1-1.5 cm) in hidden places with a special tool (cannula) fat is removed.

The cannula has a rounded end, and therefore when moving its vessels and nerves are not damaged. As the fat is removed, the contours of the body are modeled. At the end of the operation, the cavities after the removal of fat are washed, the wounds, if necessary, drained or sewn by cosmetic sutures.

Special postoperative uderwear is worn to form the desired contour and eliminate cavities. At a single-stage removal of not more than 0.79 gallon during liposuction and after intravenous injection of a small amount of salt solutions to compensate for fluid loss (tissue) during liposuction. Blood transfusion is not performed in such cases. After monitoring for several hours and the appointment of treatment, the patient can be discharged.

After operation

In the postoperative period, the patient takes antibiotics and pain medications. In the early days there may be a slight increase in body temperature, soaking clothes. Bleeding and suppurative complications after liposuction are rare, there are often fluid accumulations (seromas) that are eliminated by punctures. Pain in the operated areas, as a rule, moderate.

Liposuction Is Carried Out Under General Anesthesia Or Intravenous Sedation For The Comfort Of The Individual

There are always bruises, swelling, which take place within 7-14 days. Postoperative underwear should be worn in the first 10-12 days constantly, take off only for showering, then wear up to 4 weeks only during the day.

With 3-5 days of physiotherapy, massage is prescribed. Moderate physical activity (sport) is allowed after 3 weeks. Return to work, in most cases, occurs in 10-12 days. Within a few months, the skin in the operated regions restores sensitivity, completely shrinks and becomes naturally smooth.

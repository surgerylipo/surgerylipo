Facelift Recovery
Facelift recovery
Jun 14, 2018
Recovering from a facelift will require that you take some time off work. If you work from home, then 4-5 days is fine. Otherwise, I would recommend 2-3 weeks. When you go back to work after your facelift, you want to look rejuvenated, not swollen. You could go back to work faster, if you had to, but a few extra days are worth it.

Liposuction of the abdomen in Dubai involves the removal of a relatively small amount of adipose tissue from a certain area.

Do not take this procedure as a method of rapid weight loss.

The main task of the operation is to correct the body shape, change the parameters of the body, and the removal of fat tissue is only an additional bonus.

Remember that this operation can be resorted only if the diet and regular exercise do not give the desired results.

Ironically, overweight is a contraindication to liposuction. Such patients are always recommended for a few months before the procedure to sit on a strict diet.

For successful liposuction of the stomach requires a good skin tone. If the skin is not elastic enough, after the removal of adipose tissue it can simply sag, which is hardly the effect that you would like to see after the procedure.

Any serious diseases, as well as diseases in the acute stage, are an obvious contraindication to the operation.

These include: oncology, tuberculosis, allergy, type 1 diabetes. Violations in the work of the cardiovascular system are also a serious reason to abandon the operation.

Surgical liposuction in Dubai involves anesthesia, and this is always a very heavy burden on the heart.

Mental illnesses are one of the most frequent reasons why a plastic surgeon refuses a patient for surgery. If it becomes clear in a conversation that a person is inadequately perceiving himself, his body or, say, expecting too much from the operation, the doctor directs the person to the psychologist.

If you have not found contraindications, and the outline of the figure still wants to correct, then you need to seriously approach the process of preparing for liposuction.

Types of modern operations

Modern medicine does not stand still, with each passing day there are new and new technologies, methods of conducting surgical interventions are being improved.

If more recently, liposuction was a complex and traumatic operation with a high risk of complications, but now it is considered safe and sparing.

In the clinics of aesthetic medicine can offer the following types of liposuction:

  • Tumescent
  • Vacuum
  • vibration – rotational
  • Ultrasound
  • Radio frequency
  • Laser
  • Syringe
  • non-surgical.

All methods, despite the different names, are inherently very similar. Fatty tissue is destroyed, transformed into an emulsion and excreted from the body. The oldest and, therefore, the most traumatic way is the vacuum one. Adipocytes are destroyed mechanically, quite simply, they are broken with the help of cannulas. The resulting liquid mass is sucked off by a vacuum pump. Thus at a time you can remove up to 0.79 gallon of fat.

Arm Liposuction Orange County

The tumescent technique differs from the vacuum technique in that the subcutaneous tissue is pre-impregnated with a special solution. This allows faster destruction of adipocytes and easier to remove them from the body. Blood vessels and nerve endings with this method are also less damaged.

To combat large and medium fat deposits, a vibration-rotational technique is used. The movements of the cannula are carried out in three planes and are provided with compressed air. For anesthesia and narrowing of blood vessels, the operating area is preliminarily infiltrated with a special solution. Dilution of adipose tissue can also be carried out by a high-frequency electric field. Improvement of metabolic processes and activation of microcirculation in the treatment zone provides an excellent lifting effect, skin tightening.

Laser liposuction in Orange County is performed using modern equipment. It is low-traumatic and has a very short recovery period and a stable result. The laser beam is fed into the subcutaneous tissue through very thin cannulas, rendering on adipocytes not only a thermal but also a mechanical effect. Due to the fact that the laser seals small blood vessels, the risk of hematomas and hemorrhages during the operation is reduced to almost zero.

The advantage of this method is also the stimulation of collagen fibers. The skin in the place of the procedure is well tightened and smoothed. Despite the rather high price, the laser procedure is quite popular and in demand. Ultrasonic waves are also capable of destroying adipocytes. The effect is exclusively on them, other tissues are not affected. When removing a small amount of adipose tissue (up to 0.13 gallon) in areas where only minimal pressure is allowed, surgeons use syringe liposuction.

It allows to produce pumping in very small portions, and the skin remains smooth and smooth. What method of liposuction is worth doing and how many sessions are needed, the surgeon decides on the initial consultation.

Everything depends on the area of operation, the technical equipment of the clinic, the amount of fat tissue to be removed, the individual characteristics of the patient, and so on.

List of contraindications

Before doing liposuction, the surgeon must make sure that the patient has no contraindications to the procedure. For this purpose a number of laboratory tests and, if necessary, consultations of narrow specialists are assigned.

In many clinics, preoperative examination is already included in the cost of the operation.

Contraindications are the following conditions:

  • Acute infectious diseases
  • Problems with the blood coagulation system
  • Cardiovascular pathology
  • Exacerbation of chronic diseases of internal organs
  • diabetes
  • oncological diseases
  • Pregnancy and lactation period
  • Violations in the mental sphere.

Obesity caused by endocrine pathology is a condition in which liposuction is impractical. Also, it is not done with alimentary obesity (due to overeating). Liposuction is used to correct certain areas of the body only after a general weight loss.

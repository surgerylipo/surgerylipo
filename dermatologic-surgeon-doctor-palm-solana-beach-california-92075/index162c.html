How many treatments of Emsculpt for abdomen? How long between treatments?
EMSCULPT 4 treatments, 2x/week
Jul 1, 2018
EMSCULPT clinical studies and current practice recommendations recommend four, 30 minute treatments.  They are to occur twice weekly over two weeks.  Maintenace with a single treatment every 3-6 months is more likely to yield long lasting results.

Emsculpt for arms and shoulders (off label)?
EMSCULPT cleared for buttocks and abs
Jul 1, 2018
EMSCULPT was studied in 7 clinical trials in over 200 patients for treatment to the abdomen and buttocks.  Other areas that may be treated in the future could include the upper arms or thighs.  However, these are off-label (not studied and not FDA-cleared) and an extensive conversation and consent is required between physician and patient to weigh the risks and benefits of treatment before proceeding.  
Are Emsculpt results permanent?
Good Things Come to Those with Maintenance
Jul 1, 2018
Results from EMSCULPT treatment are long lasting due to the supraphysiologic muscle contraction delivered from HIFEM technology.  The fat loss may be permanent, the muscle effects are long lasting (likely 6 months are longer).  After an initial series of 4 treatments, periodic maintenance (every 3-6 months) is recommended.  Reasonable dietary and exercise habits are also recommended.
How much does Emsculpt cost?
Package pricing encourages full treatment
Jul 1, 2018
National averages for EMSCULPT pricing per session is roughly $750-$1250.  Four treatments are recommended and likely necessary for significant results, therefore a total price of $3000 to $4500 is reasonable.  EMSCULPT providers are held to a minimum advertised price.  If a price is lower than what experts are quoting, carefully ensure that you are being treated with an authentic EMSCULPT device.
Can you have Emsculpt on the abdomen area if you have had a naval hernia?
Avoid in treatment of prior surgery
Jul 1, 2018
Great question!  It is best to avoid this elective, cosmetic procedure in an area of prior hernia surgery.  The EMSCULPT device causes intense contraction of the muscles in the abdomen.  If there is a weakness in the area of the surgery, an adverse event from treatment could occur.  Best to be safe.

Aspirin prior to Cellfina?
Avoid all anti-platelet meds and supplements prior to Cellfina
Feb 18, 2016
All medications, supplements, and foods (think flax seed and chia seeds) should be avoided for 7-10 days prior to Cellfina.  They alter the platelets and are ability to clot blood after injury.  Use of these prior to the procedure are likely to increase the severity of side effects following Cellfina, particularly bruising, swelling, and tenderness.
I need vein treatment for varicose veins and Cellfina. Does it matter which procedure I do first?
Treat Varicose veins first, then Cellfina!
Feb 18, 2016
Varicose veins can be related to significant health morbidity (problems/conditions).  Cellfina treats a cosmetic diagnosis, cellulite.  I would recommend treating the medical condition (varicose veins) first prior to treating a cosmetic issue.
Would weight loss affect cellfina results?
Cellfina and weight loss
Feb 18, 2016
Large weight increases could affect the outcome of Cellfina.  However, it is less likely that weight loss would affect the fibrous septae that lead to cellulite.  Extreme weight loss may lead to skin laxity that should be address with skin tightening procedures or surgical procedures.

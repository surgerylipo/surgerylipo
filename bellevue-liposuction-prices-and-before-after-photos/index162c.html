Bellevue Liposuction Prices And Before After Photos

You want to get rid of the excess fatty deposits that spoil your body. You have already tried different regimens and diet for weight loss, but some problem parts of the body still can not be corrected:

– the internal surface of the knee joint region;
– external surface of thighs;
– underbelly;
– buttocks;
– “Belt” and fatty folds on the back;
– double chin.

Liposuction in Bellevue is not a treatment for obesity, but allows you to adjust the body shape.

Technique of operation

The operation is performed under anesthesia and takes from 40 minutes to 2.5 hours, depending on the treated areas, their area and the amount of adipose tissue that must be aspirated.

In one operation, no more than 0.92 gallon of fat is removed. Surgeon through small incisions (no more than 0.5 cm) with a special low-traumatizing tool sucks fat from the problem areas.

Body Contouring Can Help You Slim Down Areas That Have Not Responded To Other Methods

Liposuction can be syringe, tumescent, ultrasound, pneumatic, ultrasound, laser, water jet, radio frequency. During one operation, various methods of liposuction can be used.

Despite all their diversity, the essence of correction is one – lipoaspiration through the cannula and the subsequent modeling of the body shape.

Advantageously different devices that allow with a minimum degree of invasiveness to eliminate the maximum amount of adipose tissue, while tightening the skin.

Postoperative period

You are in the Bellevue clinic for no more than 1 day. Bed rest is not required. Pain syndrome is not very pronounced and is eliminated by usual painkillers.

After the operation, bruises and edema appear, which peak at day 2, and gradually disappear within 1-3 weeks. You may experience numbness in the area of intervention, which gradually disappears.

Doctor Stephen B. Anderson, MD , Bellevue Facial Plastic Surgeon – Liposuction Procedure Of Abdomen Before & After

  • Restoration of normal health and disability comes about a week later.
  • Within a month, it is mandatory to wear special compression underwear.
  • The final effect of liposuction is achieved in 2-3 months.

We recommend

Before liposuction, consult a dermatocosmetologist. He will recommend procedures that will make the operation more effective: a hardware effect to increase the elasticity of the skin, lymphatic drainage, the introduction of lipolitics, and so on. After the operation, it is also desirable to undergo a course of cosmetology procedures aimed at improving skin tone and treating cellulite.

In areas prone to liposuction, fat cells are not formed again. However, this does not mean that you can overeat every day. Ultimately, this will lead to the fact that fat will be delayed in those places where there was no operative intervention.

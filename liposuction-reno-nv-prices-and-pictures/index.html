Liposuction Reno NV Prices And Pictures

Liposuction in Reno is a plastic surgery that surgically affects certain areas of the body in order to change the nature of fat deposits and remove excess fatty tissue.

Immediately, we draw your attention to the fact that liposuction is not a treatment for obesity, as well as coding is not a means of treating alcoholism: the operation only removes excess fat and corrects the body shape, but does not affect the pathogenesis of the disease in any way.

With liposuction, you can achieve a strong visual effect, which is difficult to achieve by other methods of weight loss: the effect of plastic surgery will be more pronounced and significant.

Indications for liposuction in Reno

Invasive Lipo By Dr. Phillip Dahan, Reno Plastic Surgeon

Most often, indications is purely aesthetic. For help to a plastic surgeon, women who want to remove excess fat on their hips (the so-called “riding breeches”), abdomen, waist, buttocks, hands and other parts of the body usually address.

Men usually want to remove fat from the chest, neck, back, abdomen, waist and buttocks. It is very important that at the time of surgery your skin does not lose elasticity and natural elasticity, so liposuction is indicated to patients at young and mature age and is not recommended for older people.

Your weight should be medium or slightly above average, and your health – satisfactory.

At the time of surgery, there should be no acute illness, all tests should be normal. And most importantly, resort to surgery is recommended only if you have fat deposits that can not be removed even with diet and exercise.

Contraindications to liposuction in Reno

A number of warnings and prohibitions on liposuction can be divided into two parts: temporary contraindications and categorical. It should also be mentioned that the number of contraindications is significantly lower for non-surgical liposuction, or procedures designed to remove fat and cellulite by cosmetic methods.

Temporary contraindications, as it is displayed in the name itself, can be neutralized with the passage of time by appropriate treatment or simply wait for the necessary period until favorable conditions for the operative correction of the body shape come about.

As a rule, temporary interdictions can include a recently transferred operation in the place where there is a need to remove the fatty layer. Also if there is an exacerbation of dermatitis in the zones determined for the plastic surgery. Plus, it is contraindicated to do liposuction with exacerbation of chronic diseases or during acute colds and infectious diseases.

If you have even minimal doubts about your health, we strongly recommend that you conduct a comprehensive examination and discuss with your doctor the possibility of liposuction. The list of contraindications, which carry a categorical nature of the prohibition on the operation, begins with a marked violation of blood circulation in the zone of the proposed operation of liposuction in Reno.

Also, surgical correction of the body shape is not indicated, when the patient has blood diseases that affect its clotting. When the operation removes the fat layer, destruction of tissues with small blood vessels occurs, which can lead to serious blood loss, if you ignore the fact of the presence of this disease.

Plastic surgery is not performed when the patient has heart disease and pathology of other internal organs, as this can lead to severe consequences due to the use of anesthesia during the removal of fatty deposits. Here, contraindications include postponed myocardial infarction and stroke.

Concerned. Arm lift incisions are getting thicker and look redder/irritated 9.5 weeks post op
9.5 weeks post-oeprative scarring
Sep 6, 2017
Unusual to have scars wide so soon after surgery. All scar tissue is pink during early healing phases but raised and wide scars may have too much tension on the closure of wound or risks of hypertrophy. I agree with the silicone scar therapy and no scratching wounds. You should be seen often and may need steroid injections to reduce the scarring.

It is not expected that a patient would be experiencing burning sensations beyond the normal time of healing, 9 to 12 months. You may have thickened hypertrophic scars and these can be uncomfortable.

How To Get Liposuction For Free?

While it may sound too good to be true, there is such a thing in this world as free liposuction.

Of course it really depends on how you define free because while it might not cost you anything financially there is a time and effort commitment to hunting these free liposuction offers.

There are several opportunities that might allow a prospective patient to receive a plastic surgery procedure without having to foot the bill and are usually driven by promotions of one sort or another.

Free Liposuction for Early Birds

When a plastic surgeon decides to open their own private practice from scratch they will need to build a client base.

This is where the offer for free liposuction surgery might become available for the first handful of patients.

The plastic surgeon gets to build their reputation and develop a new client base while the patient receives the treatment they desire without having to pay thousands of dollars.

Often there will be a requirement to provide a testimonial and release the before and after pictures for the surgeon to use as material for future marketing campaigns but that is a small price for free treatment.

There is the concern of undergoing a surgical procedure with a plastic surgeon that might have very little experience since this is a new practice.

But as long as the surgeon has the proper credentials such as a board certification you can be sure they have gone through rigorous training and examinations. This greatly increases the chances that your surgeon has been properly trained and prepared to perform liposuction surgery.

Win Free Liposuction Surgery

There will be promotions being held through various media outlets, although it is usually radio stations, which offer free plastic surgery as a prize. Many of these events are regional so it certainly helps to live in a larger metropolitan area where demand for plastic surgery is high. There are websites online that track the various contests, sweepstakes or raffles and you can filter through to the ones you wish to participate in. Generally there is no cost to participate but it might take a bit of persistence, guts and a hint of luck before becoming the next winner.

Free Plastic Surgery through Online Donors

While this method is not as common with liposuction patients, many young women in particular are turning to online social sites to meet donors who contribute money toward their plastic surgery goals. The most popular treatment utilizing this strategy is breast augmentation and it is obvious why.

There are websites where a young woman will post her desired treatment and how much it will cost. Men will donate various amounts that accumulate until the woman reaches her goals. Usually there is conversation and photo exchange from the woman in return for the generosity of her donors.

Some sites use different business models such as charging per message exchanged as opposed to simply collecting contributions.

Regardless of how it works, many young women are happy about being able to get the plastic surgery procedure they desire at no cost to themselves.

Of course becoming the next 100th caller on a particular radio station during the free plastic surgery giveaway might not be the easiest way to get the body sculpting treatment you desire.

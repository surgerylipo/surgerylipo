Liposuction, lipoaspiration, liposculpture – all these are the names of one of the most popular plastic surgery in the world.

The essence of it, regardless of the name, remains unchanged: the surgeon uses special techniques to remove excess fat tissue, adjusting the contours of the body.

It’s no secret that there are zones that are extremely difficult to change with the help of diets or exercises. For example, the so-called “fat traps” in women – the area of “ears on the hips”, the inner side of the thighs, the upper part of the knees or the lower part of the abdomen.

These accumulations of adipose tissue are caused, first of all, by hormonal processes. They are the last to be affected by dietary restrictions and increased physical activity. And more often it is safe and forever to cope with them only surgically.

36 Year Old Woman Treated With Liposuction PreOp & PostOp With Dr. Theodore Nyame, MD, Charlotte Physician

But it is important to understand that liposuction in Charlotte only helps to remove local deficiencies. This method is not suitable for people who need to lose weight or lose weight “in general”.

But for those who have almost no extra pounds, but there are problem areas, this technique will be an ideal solution.

Slender people in principle, it is difficult to lose weight: they do not have large surpluses of adipose tissue in the body, so diets and increased activity in their case do not have a very pronounced effect.

That is why delicate surgical treatment, that is – liposuction, in such cases becomes a method of choice, allowing patients to obtain perfect contours of the body.

To prepare for surgery, it is enough to come to a consultation with a surgeon, and then to have a few simple tests. Liposuction is performed under local or general anesthesia, depending on the area of exposure, volume of work and patient’s wishes.

To remove fat, the surgeon uses special finest cannulas that are inserted into the right areas of the body or face through tiny punctures. An experienced doctor will perform all the intervention quickly and accurately, forming new, harmonious body contours. However, there are no visible scars on the face or body after liposuction.

The operation is well developed and studied, therefore it is well tolerated and usually does not cause any unnecessary consequences. Many patients feel so well after the intervention that they express a desire to leave the clinic a couple of hours after the anesthesia stops.

Often, patients are asked whether liposuction of the whole body is possible in principle, and how much it can cost. Here it is well understood that in one operation it is possible to do liposuction of several zones, but still this is not a method of total weight loss. This operation is ideal for local correction of the body.

Amazing Result You Can Receive From Abdominal Liposuction

In the course of one intervention, it is possible to improve contours and abdomen, and backs, or, for example, knees, hips and shins. Such combinations allow you to immediately get a complex result and literally transform the body.

If we talk about combinations of techniques, then liposuction of the cheeks or chin can be carried out simultaneously with various types of face lifts. This allows you to give the person a refined outline and at the same time get rid of signs of age. A liposuction of the body, especially the abdomen, is often combined with mammoplasty. Especially relevant is a combination for young mothers who want to quickly return the body of the girl’s outlines.

Where is the best place to perform liposuction? First of all, look for an experienced surgeon who often conducts similar operations and has an impeccable reputation. Find out the exact cost of the intervention can be on face-to-face consultation. The fact is that the price directly depends on the volume of liposuction in Charlotte.

INDICATIONS FOR LIPOSUCTION

Liposuction is performed in the presence of local excess of adipose tissue, which are difficult to correct by conservative methods. Popular areas of exposure – the outer and inner sides of the thighs, shins, knees, abdomen, back, waist, buttocks, upper arms, face.

CONTRAINDICATIONS TO LIPOSUCTION

Pregnancy, lactation, any infectious diseases in the acute stage, malignant processes, severe blood diseases, systemic lesions of internal organs, exacerbation of chronic diseases.

RECOVERY AFTER LIPOSUCTION

Due to the delicacy of the impact, rehabilitation after liposuction usually take place quite easily. In the early days you may be a little worried about swelling or bruising. But they quickly disappear, and in two weeks you can completely return to the ordinary way of life. And the final result can be estimated approximately in two-three months after operation.

Can IPL hide skin cancer?

IPL may reduce redness and pigment but will not hide the abnormality of precancer to the trained dermatologist.  There are other signs such as roughness, irregular margins and vascularity that will not disappear with IPL.  As mentioned, IPL can be used in conjunction with Levulan to treat precancerous lesions.

Can you use HA or Prp as effectively for glabellar lines as Botox
Jul 31, 2019
The answer is simple, glabellar line are "dynamic" wrinkles caused by muscle movement which is directly addressed by Botox, which relaxes the muscles.  HA can be risky in the glabella because there are large arteries in that area that can be damaged by injection with a filler.  PRP is not sufficient to alter frown lines.

Which type of filler is best for/ last longest in the tear droughts and the nose?

Best is not the only question, but best and safest.  In the tear trough and nose the safest are hyaluronic acid filler because they can be dissolved if there is a problem, but the longest lasting and best for correction is Radiesse, but it cannot be dissolved so it must be done by someone very familiar with its proper placement and amount to be certain it is safe.

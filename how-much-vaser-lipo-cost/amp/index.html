How Much Vaser Lipo Cost

The cost of Vaser Liposuction depends on the body area treated. Pricing for Vaser Lipo is not like purchasing laundry detergent by comparing prices between Target and WalMart, because the answer is “it depends.”

It depends on how many areas from which you want fat removed, your general health and other factors, so it’s impossible to give a flat quote on a forum like this without seeing you in person. The biggest question to ask is whether the board certified plastic surgeon you choose has extensive experience with Vaser Lipo, and how his before and after photos match up to your expectations for your own results.

That’s the question to ask. You can use financing to budget the procedure over time. (Paul Vanek, MD, Cleveland Plastic Surgeon)

Cost of Vaser Liposuction

Costs can vary depending on the provider performing the procedure, the area of the body undergoing the treatment and the area of the country you reside. For the Washington DC metro region we may have slightly higher fees than other areas, especially if being performed by a Board Certified Plastic Surgeon and not by another specialist who may not be as thoroughly trained in aesthetic surgical procedures.

Often the fees charged relate to the time that it takes to perform the liposuction. For example, the abdomen will take 2-3 times as long to contour as the inner thighs and the cost will reflect that.

Typically the procedure is performed under twilight sedation or general anesthesia, both of which are administered in our practice by Board Certified Anesthesiologists. The cost of not only anesthesia but also for the operating room and the Vaser equipment itself needs to be factored into the final outline of costs.

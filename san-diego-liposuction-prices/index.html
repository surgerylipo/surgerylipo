San Diego Liposuction Prices

Liposuction in San Diego is an effective method of surgical correction of body contours.

The popularity of liposuction in our days is undeniable, because the violation of the contours of the body due to the presence of local fat deposits is observed in many people.

The technique of the operation is relatively simple, the traces after the operation are almost invisible.

It is worth noting that liposuction is not a method of combating obesity or weight loss method.

With the help of liposuction, the problem of removing local fat accumulations in those areas where neither diet nor sport “does not work” is being solved. The weight of the patient going to the liposuction should be close to normal.

The result after liposuction is persistent. That is, the new contours of the body that the patient acquires as a result of liposuction persist for a long time.

Remote fat does not recover over time. But we also need to understand that if the patient significantly gaining weight after the operation or vice versa sharply grows thin – as a result, the contours of the body can change.

INDICATIONS FOR LIPOSUCTION

The main indication for liposuction is the presence of local fat deposits, which violate the contours of the figure. The most problematic areas for women include: the thigh area, the abdomen and waist region, the double chin and so on. Men often get rid of excess fat in the waist and abdomen, on the back, chest.

The technique of liposuction provides for observance of certain rules and has its own peculiarities depending on the forms of fat deposits and their localization. Small operations (2-4 liposuction zones) are often performed under local anesthesia. With liposuction of a larger number of zones, the operation is performed under general anesthesia.

Preparation for the operation:

  • Consultation with a surgeon
  • You have test in the clinic, the duration of blood tests is 10 days.

Operation:

  • The patient comes to the clinic for the operation, concludes the contract of treatment and makes payment. The patient is in the ward.
  • The surgeon is doing the markup. The surgeon photographs you to compare the results after the operation.
  • Anesthesia: local anesthesia, intravenous anesthesia.
  • The duration of the operation is 30 to 120 minutes.

Where can I pump out fat deposits?

Liposuction is practiced when it is required quickly, successfully and without damage to health to remove fat deposits from such areas as:

  • Submandibular region (chin and neck);
  • Cheeks;
  • Area under the shoulder blades;
  • Waist (lateral surfaces of the trunk);
  • External surface of thighs;
  • Inner thighs;
  • Area of the shins and knee joints;
  • Lateral and posterior surface of shoulders;
  • Anterior abdominal wall.

In women, problem areas are usually: abdomen, upper arms, thighs (external and internal), knees, flanks, buttocks.

In men unwanted fat deposits are located in the chin area, in the subscapular region, in the anterior and lateral areas of the abdomen, as well as in the excessively developed mammary glands (gynecomastia). In our clinic, a variety of liposuction is successfully performed: liposuction of the hands, liposuction of the abdomen, liposuction of the back and sides, liposuction of the legs, knees, hips, buttocks and so on.

Liposuction in San Diego is not a method of fighting obesity!

And this is important to understand from the very beginning! The purpose of liposuction is to correct the contours of the body by eliminating “unnecessary” fat deposits from certain problem areas.

This technique is aimed at correcting individual zones of accumulation of fat cells, not to remove all fat “stocks”. Liposuction can be achieved only localized removal of excess fat.

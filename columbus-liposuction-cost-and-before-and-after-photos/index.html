2. Syringe liposuction.

This type of liposuction is used to pump fat in small areas, for example, on the knees, in the area of the fatty hump on the back, and also on the chin.

The duration of this operation is about thirty minutes, there are few swelling and bruises, there are practically no complications. The result of syringe liposuction can be seen in a month.

3. Ultrasonic liposuction.

This operation is that the fat deposits are broken up to the liquid state with the help of ultrasound, and then removed by titanium cannulas with the help of vacuum suction.

The skin with this type of liposuction is minimally injured, and the skin under the influence of ultrasound is better reduced, which provides a more even surface after the operation. The procedure is performed under local or complete anesthesia. The recovery period is rather short, the patient is in a hospital all day. Within two months after ultrasonic liposuction need to wear a compression garment.

4. Tumescent liposuction in Columbus.

This type of operation does not require general anesthesia.

The operation is as follows: in the problem area, using a syringe, a special formulation is introduced from epinephrine, ice medicine and saline solution, the amount of which must twice exceed the amount of fat processed.

Then begins the destruction of fat and its aspiration. With tumescent liposuction, blood loss and trauma are less than, for example, with classical liposuction. The patient after surgery performs one night in the hospital, and the postoperative period is very short. Within two weeks, you will need to wear special compression underwear.

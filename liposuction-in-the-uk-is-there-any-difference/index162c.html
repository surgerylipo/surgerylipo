Liposuction In The UK: Is There Any Difference?

Getting a liposuction in the UK is generally no different than getting it locally. Surgeons in UK and in the US basically use the same lipo techniques and procedure.

In addition to that, the same factors affect the procedure’s total cost.

Just like in the US, liposuction cost will vary depending on the body area that needs to be treated and the amount of fat the patient intends to have removed.

A liposuction in the UK will likely cost you the same amount of money than when you’re having it done in the US.

With an average cost of around £3398, this includes the surgeon and anesthesiologist fees, as well as the facilities and hospital fees.

The surgeon’s fee will depend on his or her qualifications, experience and popularity. So, if you choose a highly qualified surgeon for a liposuction in UK, you will likely be paying more. Facilities and hospital fees are also included, especially if the procedure requires several days of confinement in the hospital.

Moreover, the anesthesiologist’s fee will also be factored in the total cost. The type of liposuction procedure or technique that will be used will also influence the overall price of the treatment. If you opt for new liposuction techniques, it will definitely cost higher depending on the type of equipment that will be used to perform the procedure.

If you think having liposuction in the UK will be less costly than having it in the US, then you should be reminded to consider that there are a lot of factors that will affect the liposuction costs and you might even end up paying more.

For all other countries that offer liposuction UK included, you may also need to take note of the travel cost, especially if you live in the US or other countries for that matter.

Of course, traveling to another country is way more expensive than going to another state, with all the passport and travel requirements needed, not to mention your plane fare and food costs. There’s also the question of where to stay when you’re in UK.

Unless you are visiting with your family, you’d have to spend for a hotel for the days before and after the procedure. And you should also consider that some airlines have strict policies for patients travelling after surgery.

A UK liposuction may not be such a good idea after all, if you consider of the hassles that you need to go through as well as the expenses that you may have to shoulder. But of course, if you are living in UK, then there is no need for you to worry about all of these since you will be just availing of the cosmetic treatment in your own country.

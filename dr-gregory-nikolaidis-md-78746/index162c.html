Are there side effects associated with Voluma?
Voluma Side Effects
Dec 16, 2013
Voluma is like any other dermal filler in that patients may experience bruising or swelling because it is usually injected with a needle. But because this product is not hydrophylic (doesn't pull in any water) patients don't experience swelling from the product.
Is Voluma an effective treatment for crows feet and 11 lines?
Voluma Not For Crows Feet or 11's
Dec 16, 2013
Voluma is only FDA-approved for mid-face volumization. A more appropriate treatment option for crow's feet and 11's is Botox.
Can Voluma be used as a preventative measure for volume loss in the cheek area?
Voluma as a Preventative Measure
Dec 16, 2013
If you haven't already experienced volume loss in your face, you would not benefit from volume restoration. However, by the age of 35, most people have lost about 10% of the volume in their face. Any point after that, an aging face could benefit from a Voluma treatment.
How often will I need to get Voluma treatments?
Voluma results duration
Dec 16, 2013
Voluma is FDA approved to last two years. Unlike other fillers available, patients only need a single treatment with optimal correction.
I would like to know that does Botox reduces the wrinkles permanently or for some years?
Botox Does Not Last Years
Nov 21, 2013
The effects of Botox typically last 3-4 months. You would need to repeat the treatment if you are looking for long-term effects. Similar products, including Dysport and Xeomin, also last about the same amount of time.
Am I a Good Candidate for Chin Laser Lip?
Candidate for Chin Lipo
Jun 17, 2013
Based on your photos, you do appear to be a potential candidate for chin liposuction. Neck and chin lipo procedures can significantly improve facial contour. With age, the skin loses elasticity. This should be evaluated in person by the provider of your choice. It may be helpful for you to see someone who performs the Precision Tx treatment because it allows for direct application of laser energy to the skin, therefore providing better skin tightening.
Am I a Good Candidate for Coolsculpting?
CoolSculpting right for you?
Jun 12, 2013
It's always best to schedule a consultation with the physician you choose, but from your photos, you do look like a good candidate for CoolSculpting. Your provider should assess an appropriate treatment plan for you that may include multiple treatments depending on the area of concern.
Is It Safe to Undergo Ultherapy After Cheek/Jaw Implants?
Ultherapy Treatments Near Implants
Jun 10, 2013
I would not advise receiving an Ultherapy treatment directly over the implant, but the treatment could be done on areas of the cheek where the implant is not directly placed. It could also be performed on other areas such as the neck or forehead. In general, Ultherapy is not performed directly on the jaw, in part because the reflection of the heat is painful, but also because there are important nerves that we try not to treat directly over.
How Long Should I Wait Between Fraxel Re:Pair and Ultherapy?
Wait Time Between Fraxel Re:Pair and Ultherapy
May 23, 2013
Hello Molly - I use both the Fraxel Re:Pair and Ultherapy lasers in my practice. My patients wait about a month between the two treatments in order for the skin to reepithelialize. You will likely have a bit of swelling and redness, and this will allow time for that to heal as well.
Can my New Cover Up Tattoo Be Removed?
PicoSure can remove blue wave tattoo
May 16, 2013

Yes, all or part of your new tattoo can be removed. When you treat the new tattoo, the original black tattoo underneath will also be removed. The new PicoSure laser works well on blue ink. PicoSure generally takes about 4 treatments spaced 3-4 weeks apart.

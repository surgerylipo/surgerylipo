Can I Just Get Liposuction On My Love Handles Before & After Considering A Tummy Tuck?

You can absolutely get liposuction on the hips and love handle areas, as a matter of fact this is the best area for liposuction with the least amount of side effects and complications.

Liposuction in this area is often done at the same time as the tummy tuck to improve the overall shape of the trunk, but doing liposuction first in this area would not interfere with a tummy tuck should you decide to have one later. (Farhad Rafizadeh, MD, Morristown Plastic Surgeon)

It is certainly possible to perform flank liposuction as a stand alone procedure and save the tummy tuck for a later date.

This is a procedure under IV sedation which can be performed in an accredited office based facility.

I would not recommend oral sedation alone both for safety and comfort reasons. (Robert L. Kraft, MD, FACS, New York Plastic Surgeon)

You can have lipo of the waist at the same time/before/after a tummy tuck. I prefer to do it at the same time as the tummy tuck. (Steven Wallach, MD, New York Plastic Surgeon)

YES you can do one area. It is actually much easier to do it this way as your recovery will be minimal.

Isolated lipo treatment of love handles

Absolutely yes, you can have any part of your body independently suctioned. That is the beauty of liposuction and actually that was its original intent — to target specific bulges of fat and eliminate them.

Many surgeons like to combine areas just for convenience for the patient — ie get it all done at one time. But there is no reason not to do just one particular area if that’s what you want to do. (Elliot W. Jacobs, MD, New York Plastic Surgeon)

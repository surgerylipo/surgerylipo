Cost Of Power Assisted Liposuction

The cost of Power Assisted Liposuction, and essentially all aesthetic procedures, will consist of the surgeon’s fee, the anesthesiologist’s fee, and a facility fee.

This can vary greatly depending upon the skill/experience of the surgeon, the number of areas to be treated, the type of anesthesia chosen, and the type of facility utilized.

I do discount my fee when multiple areas are treated, but it is important to know that there is a limit to the amount of liposuction volume that is considered safe in an outpatient setting.

When a specific treatment plan can be formulated, he/she will be able to provide you a detailed cost breakdown of the proposed procedure. (Scott D. Holley, MD, FACS, Portage Plastic Surgeon)

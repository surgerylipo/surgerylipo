How long is too long to keep drains in after mommy makeover?
How long is too long to keep drains
Sep 3, 2015
Thank you for your question. Drains are kept in until the fluid production slows down enough to be absorbed by the body itself. Usually, this is about 20 cc per day. Being more active will cause your body to make more fluid and prolong the need for the drains. I advise close follow-up with your Plastic Surgeon and adhere to his/her post operative instructions closely. Best wishes.

Is microaire Lipo for male breast reduction usually sufficient?
Is microaire Lipo for male breast reduction usually sufficient?
Aug 27, 2015
Thank you for your question. For males who have true gynecomastia, liposuction alone usually is not enough. Most will require the excision of the hard, glandular tissue around the nipple. However, if you have just excess body fat in the chest area (called pseudo-gynecomastia) then liposuction alone may be adequate. I would wait 2 to 3 months to allow the swelling to go down and then judge the results. Best wishes.

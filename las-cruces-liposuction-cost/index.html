Liposuction is one of the most common operations in plastic surgery, which allows you to remove excess fat tissue in various places of the human body.

Despite the visible effect – this intervention is not a treatment for obesity, since it does not affect the cause of the onset of the disease.

However, this method is the best removal of fat deposits, which can not be achieved by other methods in the shortest possible time.

There are a lot of ways of liposuction, however, the principle is one – a special tube (cannula) is inserted under the skin, and under pressure, which creates a special apparatus (sometimes syringes), and with some solutions, adipose tissue is removed.

Missouri Liposuction Cost And Photos

Liposuction is a section of plastic surgery aimed at correcting the body and creating a visually harmonious silhouette of the body by removing fatty surpluses.

The term “liposuction” literally means “pumping out fat”, which in general characterizes this operation.

Liposuction in Missouri can be performed on various problem areas of the body, such as the buttocks, stomach, hips, waist, back, arms (upper part), knees, and cheeks and chin.

In the treatment of gynecomastia in men, liposuction is also used to remove fat deposits in the chest.

To make a liposuction in our clinic means to get rid of problems that bring trouble, insecurity, develop complexes and interfere with harmonious, balanced life.

Liposuction of the second chin, liposuction of the sides, other problem zones completely eliminates discontent with yourself, and after a few weeks makes you admire your new, slender and tight silhouette and face contour (if it is liposuction of the chin or cheeks).

Doctor Michele Koo, Missouri Plastic Surgeon Liposuction Results Before And After

The latest technologies, experience of surgeons and modern materials are the basis of our work and guarantee of safety and tremendous effect, which will provide liposuction, conducted by our specialists. After the procedures and minimal recovery of the abdomen, sides, ” breeches” and other problem areas will not occur!

Liposuction is able to change the silhouette of the body, however, its application is possible only locally and does not relieve obesity and fat deposits for good. In addition, in this operation it is not possible to remove a large amount of fat immediately, since this involves a risk to the patient.

Sometimes in addition to fat deposits on the body of the patient’s excess skin is present. Since liposuction alone can not cope with this, in addition to it, abdominoplasty is used.

Dr Charles R. Nathan, MD, FACS, Saint Louis Plastic Surgeon – Liposuction Of Waist

Liposuction can be of different types: ultrasound, laser, radio wave, water jet. The expediency of using this or that kind is best discussed with the doctor.

Liposuction is contraindicated in persons over 18 years of age, pregnant and lactating women, as well as people with chronic or acute diseases and allergies to any medications.

Liposuction is a serious surgical intervention, before which it is necessary to consult an experienced doctor.

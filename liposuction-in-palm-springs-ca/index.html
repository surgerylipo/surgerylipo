Liposuction In Palm Springs CA

Liposuction in Palm Springs is the removal of excess subcutaneous fat with the purpose of surgical correction of the figure. The most common methods of liposuction are vacuum and ultrasound, in which fat is somehow sucked through special tubes.

But recently laser liposuction has become more common, which has a number of advantages in comparison with conventional methods, and sometimes serves as an addition to them. Laser liposuction in Palm Springs is one of the most effective methods to date to combat overweight, obesity and cellulite. Unfortunately, very often people exhaust themselves with diets, hunger strikes and training, and extra pounds remain “with them” – due to hormonal imbalance, age, heredity and various other factors.

The only salvation then is the help of qualified specialists and minimally invasive technologies – laser lipolysis.

With this method, doctors work miracles – removing excess fats in the necessary areas, they thereby simulate body volumes, “mold” it, like a sculptor.

High-precision equipment allows you to work with small, difficult to handle areas, such as: “second” chin, “ears” on the sides, inside of the thigh, knee, ankle, forearms.

The essence of the method is the targeted destruction of fat cells with the help of laser energy.

At the same time, coagulation of capillaries penetrating fatty tissue occurs, which minimizes tissue trauma and bruising. In addition, the thermal effect on tissue collagen promotes lifting and healing of the skin in the treatment zone.

Liposuction of the abdomen

Our perception of the ideal female and male body shape is determined by the contour and silhouette of the upper abdomen, lower abdomen and lateral surfaces of the abdomen.

Nowadays, as in the distant past, fashionable glossy magazines usually take models with ideal body contours, which dictates that everyone should have perfect shapes.

The upper abdomen of the ideal male and female body contains little fat. The lower abdomen has a slightly convex contour closer to the navel. The side surfaces of the abdomen in women should have the form of an hourglass with a transition to the hip.

Liposuction of the chin in Palm Springs is one of the most effective interventions which allows you to remove the second chin. Laser Liposuction Technique

Laser liposuction is performed under local anesthesia and when performing surgery in small areas – outpatient. Directly before liposuction, photography and marking are performed on the area of the forthcoming exposure.

Through miniature punctures with a special needle, fatty tissue is processed by a laser. Laser energy is delivered through a hollow needle, which is inserted through the skin.

The hollow needle moves back and forth, the laser energy destroys the fat, then aspiration is performed. With laser lipolysis, the destroyed fat cells are not removed, the destroyed fat cells themselves disintegrate and are removed from the body.

The end of the hollow needle is always noticeable due to the special red illumination visible through the skin, this allows the plastic surgeon to control its movement.

Laser energy also affects the skin, as a result of which collagen fibers and skin as a whole are compressed (the effect of lifting). Usually for one area of the body, one procedure lasts from 45 minutes to 1 hour. Recovery after laser liposuction is shorter in comparison with traditional, therefore on inspections in the clinic after the operation it will be necessary to come just a few times.

The result of applying the laser

The use of the laser guarantees not only the rapid and uniform removal of fat cells, the main trump card, is a thorough skin tightening that occurs from the inside. No wonder the surgeons themselves call the method of laser liposuction “sculptural plastic”.

Results may be noticeable one week after the procedure, the overall effect and speed of its onset depends on the general condition of the body. In the place where liposuction was performed, the fat layer no longer increases, even if the patient dramatically increases his weight.

The problem of the presence of fat deposits on the abdomen worries many, it is solved in many ways. The method of liposuction of the abdomen is considered effective.

What results can be achieved by resorting to such a procedure, in which the peculiarity of the method, to whom is contraindicated liposuction on the stomach, these questions are extremely important.

The peculiarity of the procedure

Miscellaneous equipment, tools, modern radio frequency, laser, ultrasound techniques are used to remove fat, located in the abdominal cavity.

With the development of technology, operations in which incisions are made are replaced by non-surgical methods.

The laser and ultrasound methods are considered to be cardinal. Laser liposuction of the abdomen is a success in the clinic. A special feature of the technique is the cleavage of fat cells and simultaneous skin tightening by stimulating the production of collagen.

After collecting anamnesis, necessary tests, the clinic specialist develops tactics of the operation. The date is assigned, the patient is given some recommendations.

What are the best candidates for liposuction of the abdomen

People who have both a small tummy, and with significant fat deposits resort to liposuction of the abdomen. Men use the services of the clinic no less than women. Frequent patients are women giving birth, and those who do not manage to get rid of the excess belly by other methods. This procedure has contraindications.

Like any surgery with anesthesia, liposuction has contraindications. These are:

  • diabetes;
  • skin diseases;
  • diseases associated with blood coagulability;
  • neoplasms;
  • periods of pregnancy of lactation;
  • mental disorders.

How is the procedure performed?

At the first stage, the finest cannula is inserted through a microscopic puncture 0.2 mm. It conducts a low-frequency laser. Under its influence, lipolysis occurs, rapid destruction of adipose tissue in strictly defined areas, while adjacent zones remain unaffected.

The contents of the fat cells are removed naturally through the vessels. Damaged vessels with a surgical laser are immediately “sealed”, preventing bleeding. Prices for laser abdominal liposuction are not very different from liposuction by ultrasound, however, the effective result and anti-inflammatory effect of this technique is higher.

n the second stage, using the same channel, the laser electrode is introduced. Its action is aimed at warming up the deep layers of the dermis. The production of natural collagen is stimulated. After the liposuction of the abdomen, the skin on the tummy will no longer be sagging. As a result, you get an elastic without wrinkles and an “apron” belly.

The result is obtained as two in one, for one cost to the clients of the clinic will be performed liposuction of the abdomen and at the same time lifting of the problem area.

What anesthesia is used? Modern preparations allow painlessly to carry out liposuction of the abdomen, you can find out the prices in the clinic.

Duration of the procedure

The duration of the session is different in each case. Abdominal area takes more time. The thickness of the fat layer affects the duration of the procedure. On average, surgery to remove adipose tissue on the abdomen takes about one and a half hours.

Recovery period

The patient ordered the procedure for laser liposuction of the abdomen, the price for it depends on the thickness of the fatty tissue. After the operation to remove fat cells, the rehabilitation period begins.

If the session is successful, the patient will be released home immediately. A full recovery takes place 3 days, in rare cases this is a week. With laser liposuction, there is no bruising, swelling, and pain and other side effects, as after a traditional operation.

It will be recommended examination by the doctor after the procedure of liposuction of the abdomen, you need to wear an inexpensive supporting compressive underwear for about two weeks.

From physical exertion for a while it is necessary to refuse. Within six months, the fat cells continue to deteriorate, so the result will be visible immediately and in a month, and the final version of the improved body shape will appear after six months.

Naples Liposuction Prices And Photos

Liposuction in Naples is a kind of plastic surgery aimed at removing excess subcutaneous fat tissue in problem areas.

Despite the fact that at present there are many alternative methods of reducing the thickness of the subcutaneous fat layer, liposuction remains one of the most effective and most popular method of correction of appearance.

Very often liposuction is used with other types of plastic surgery and can be accompanied by a skin tightening.

The operation, as a rule, is performed under anesthesia and its duration is determined by the planned volume of adipose tissue removal, and also by the method of liposuction.

But at the heart of any kind of liposuction is the aspiration of fat with the help of special equipment through specially injected under the skin into different depth cannula.

Doctor Stanley P. Gulin, Naples Plastic Surgeon Liposuction For Legs With Laser

Depending on the type of equipment used and the technique for removing adipose tissue, several types of liposuction are distinguished:

Aspiration or vacuum liposuction is the most effective and most traditional form of liposuction, in which incisios not more than 1 cm are made on the skin, and through them a thin probe (cannula) with a rounded end and several apertures through which aspiration of adipose tissue occurs.

Preliminarily, a special solution with lipolytic drugs is introduced into the subcutaneous fat of the planned intervention zones, which contribute to reducing the viscosity of adipose tissue. Thanks to this, when the surgeon performs small reciprocal movements, the fat tissue is easily removed through the holes in the cannula.

Lipoplasty Can Be Performed On Several Areas Of The Body, Including The Abdomen, Arms, Thighs And Neck

Ultrasonic liposuction is based on the destruction of fat tissue by ultrasonic waves with strictly prescribed parameters, after which it is removed by aspiration. This kind of liposuction has a number of side effects (including on the surrounding organs), so it is not used often.

High-frequency lipo-modelling combines the introduction of solutions of lipolitics and local exposure to high frequency currents, after which the destroyed fatty tissue is also removed through thin cannulas.

This method is used to remove small volumes of adipose tissue, it gives a uniform modeling effect, and the holes for the introduction of thin cannulae practically remain invisible and do not require suturing, and the postoperative period also decreases.

Laser liposuction is based on the destruction of cells of adipose tissue by laser energy, followed by the gradual excretion of the products of decay by the kidneys, after partial deactivation with their liver. In this regard, this technique is used only for small excess fatty tissue, for example, when correcting the chin area.

Radio wave liposuction combines radio wave destruction of fat cells with subsequent vacuum aspiration.

Syringe liposuction is essentially a kind of aspiration, but a vacuum is created when the syringe piston moves, and the removal of a small amount of fat occurs not through a cannula, but a normal needle. This method is used to remove small local accumulations of fat, for example, in the chin zone, the knee joint area and so on.

The main contraindications for liposuction in Naples:

  1. Pregnancy
  2. Disorders of blood clotting,
  3. Malignant oncological diseases,
  4. A pronounced polyvalent allergy,
  5. Acute infectious diseases or exacerbations of chronic diseases, as well as diseases in the stage of decompensation.

Postoperative period and its possible complications:

Depending on the volume of liposuction performed and the patient’s state of health, you is in hospital for several days, or several hours after it is discharged home. Cosmetic stitches, which are removed after a week may be imposed on the place the incision. A bandage is applied from above, which is fastened with an adhesive patch. It is compulsory to wear special compression underwear, which is selected in advance.

Dr. Elizabeth Fox, Naples Plastic Surgeon Abdominal Liposuction Before And After

After the operation, the temperature may rise for a short time, and the patient may be bothered by local pain, to reduce which analgesics are prescribed. There is also swelling and the presence of hematomas in the zones of liposuction and adjacent tissues, which gradually disappear within a few weeks.

Complications develop rarely if the patient does not follow the surgeon’s recommendations. It can be the formation of bruises, seroma, infection of wounds, violation of skin sensitivity and skin pigmentation in the operation area, uneven skin relief.

But with timely detection of such complications are successfully cured, although it is better not to bring it to the fore.

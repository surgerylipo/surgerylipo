What is the difference between a mini lift and a full lift?
Mini-facelift vs Full-facelift
Jan 20, 2018
A mini-lift addresses the mid-face, nasal labial fold and jowl areas. It is performed through a shorter incision and is a good operation for patients who don't need a lot of work on their neck. A full facelift utilizes a longer incision and can allow for the correction of more issues of the neck. Consult with a board-certified plastic surgeon for best results.
Good Luck!

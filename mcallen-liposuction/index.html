Methods and progress of the operation. Power-Assisted Liposuction.

Liposuction can be carried out by various methods in Mcallen TX. The most common of these is the Power-Assisted Liposuction, which involves the suction of fatty tissue from local areas with the help of a vacuum created.

A moist, or tumescent, way of performing power-assisted liposuction involves subcutaneous injection of a special solution that includes adrenaline, lidocaine, and saline.

This injection provides a reduction in pain sensitivity of tissues and reduces bleeding. In addition, the substances included in the solution, contribute to the dilution of fatty tissue and swelling of tissues, so that the work of the surgeon is facilitated.

Anesthetic drug lidocaine while having the right effect on the tissues, is removed from the body together with fatty tissue, before it reaches the bloodstream.

If exhausting diets and aesthetic methods of correction does not give the expected result or purely hypothetically seem ineffective, you probably came to mind the idea of an operation such as liposuction in Tampa.

No doubt, do liposuction – it means not only eliminate excess weight, but also improve the body shape, with obviously 100% guaranteed results.

It is represented by the following types:

– Power assisted liposuction,
– Electronic,
– Ultrasonic,
– Syringe,
– Tumescent.

It is important to remember, that deciding on liposuction is not the same as, for example, the procedure of mesotherapy or algal wrapping. This is a serious surgical operation that has a rehabilitation period of 6-8 weeks.

Taking the final decision about the operation, experts strongly recommend that you once again get acquainted with the non-surgical methods of getting rid of the imperfections of the body: laser and ultrasound liposuction. These are more sparing and safe methods.

Do not harm your health, but at the same time get rid of hateful fat deposits forever? No sane person will dare to do this. Therefore, it is very important to read the indications and contraindications to liposuction.

Indications for surgical liposuction:

  • The presence of fat deposits in certain areas of the body, causing a violation of the contours of the body;
  • Carrying out other plastic surgeries, for example, abdominoplasty;
  • Mammoplasty.
  • There is a misconception that the main goal of liposuction is weight loss, but with it, mainly, correction of problem areas is achieved.

Contraindications to the removal of fat tissue:

  • diabetes;
  • hypertension;
  • Oncological and mental diseases;
  • Pregnancy and lactation;
  • heart diseases.

Technique of surgical liposuction

In most cases, it is carried out under general anesthesia. The type of anesthesia is chosen based on the amount of intervention in the operation.

First, an incision is made of soft tissues with a cannula, a special surgical instrument. The incision does not exceed 1 cm. A probe is then inserted into the incision zone, which destroys the fat cells and mixes them with the liquid.

As a result of these actions, an emulsion is formed, which is removed from the tissues by vacuum aspiration.

Postoperative period

On how the rehabilitation will take place, the success of the whole operation depends.

Liposuction involves surgical intervention, so you need to consider that in the early days you will feel some discomfort, such as:

  • Bruises and swelling (within 7-14 days);
  • temperature increase;
  • Fluid accumulations eliminated by punctures.

The volume of the removed fat determines the course of the postoperative period: the less fat is removed, the patient feels better.

Sutures are removed one week after liposuction in Tampa. To ensure the maximum effect of liposuction, the patient, on the recommendation of a doctor, wears special compression underwear for a month.

In addition, physiotherapy and massage are prescribed. Liposuction does not mean to relax and continue to eat food in the same volume as before; Does not mean that it’s worth neglecting a healthy diet!

It should not be considered as one of the methods of treating obesity – it is not so. Observance of the basic principles of dietetics will help to improve and consolidate the result obtained after the operation.

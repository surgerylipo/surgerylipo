When Does Swelling After Breast Augmentation Begin to Subside?
When Does Swelling After Breast Augmentation Begin to Subside?
Oct 30, 2010
Hello,

After breast augmentation, the breasts will become moderately swollen and quite a bit firmer than normal, both from the presence of the addition volume of the implant, and also from the general puffiness that accompanies handling / traumatizing the chest wall tissues. The general direction the breast goes is less firmness, with relatively small decreases in absolute size as healing progresses.

How Many Units to Botox Crow’s Feet?
How much Botox to treat Crow’s Feet?
Oct 30, 2010
Hello, 

The first time I treat a patient for crow's feet in the area of the lateral lower eyelid, I usually start with 5-6 units per side, spread over two or three injection sites. The dosage can be raised for more dramatic and sustained effect, but it is important not to weaken the muscle that crinkles the eye so much that the lower lid sags, which can be uncomfortable or unattractive.

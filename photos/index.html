There are a sufficient number of methods for treating obesity. Liposuction, although not related to such, helps to get rid of excess fat. When liposuction does not affect the pathogenesis of obesity and does not remove a significant amount of fat.

But, it is worth noting that the effect of liposuction can be significantly higher and more noticeable than the loss of the same mass through other procedures. Liposuction is a method of vacuum removal of local accumulations of fat. Medicine provides several types of liposuction, but all of them are united by one principle. In the subcutaneous space is placed a special tube, a cannula.

Under the influence of negative pressure, which is created by a specially designed apparatus, through this same tube and the surplus of adipose tissue is removed. The operation is performed under anesthesia, general or local, depending on the wishes and condition of the patient. The number and size of the treated areas also affect the choice of anesthesia.

Liposuction of the abdomen is an operation with increased demand. Probably, there is not a single person who would not have a tendency to excess weight.

Not necessarily it can be an innate feature of the body or the result of an incorrect diet – the age and hormonal changes in the body may well lead to the appearance of excess weight.

Therefore, liposuction of the abdomen is probably one of the most famous and demanded plastic surgery today. Being one of the most problematic areas, the abdomen area is difficult to return to an elastic and taut condition, and surgical removal of excess fatty tissues can speed this process.

Liposuction is not a way to lose weight

Lipo Surgery

The most common reason for liposuction of the abdomen is the inability to return the aesthetically pleasing contours of this area of the body with the help of sports. Most often, because of the lack of noticeable results after hours of training in gyms, men decide to perform the operation. Among women, a large percentage of newly born mothers are due to the fact that the belly after the birth of the child becomes more disposed to the accumulation of adipose tissue and strict diets do not always help. Features of the liposuction procedure are:

  1. The relative simplicity of the procedure.
  2. High efficiency and a high percentage probability of obtaining the expected result.
  3. Imperceptible scars even after serious volumes of removal of fatty masses.

But it should be remembered, that liposuction of the abdominal region is not a simple way to lose weight. Weight loss due to fitness and fat suction procedures do not have an equal sign. Liposuction of the abdomen is an operation to give the body new contours by removing excess fat masses from the waist zone. Not all problems can be solved with the help of liposuction.

Although the operation of removing fat masses in the waist region is quite popular, not everyone knows that the rounded abdomen is not always that because of the accumulated fat masses. The frequent cause of the sagging abdomen is the weakening of the muscles of the abdominal cavity.

In this case, the liposuction of the abdomen will not bring a noticeable improvement in the client’s body, for this there are other plastic procedures. In the case if the cause of an unattractive appearance of the abdomen are fat deposits, liposuction allows to conduct an effective procedure for the return of aesthetically pleasing contours of the waist region. And the result obtained with proper care of yourself can be preserved for many years.

Methods of performing the liposuction procedure

Lipo Of The Chin Before And After Pic

In plastic surgery, there are several types of liposuction of the abdomen. Depending on their features, they are divided into the following:

  1. Ultrasound liposuction, this extra fat mass is destroyed by the impact on them with special sound frequencies.
  2. Classical or vacuum liposuction is the most common option, in which fatty tissues are dissolved with special mixtures, and then pumped out.
  3. Laser liposuction – the destruction of fat by laser light.
  4. Radiofrequency liposuction – excess of lipid cells destroy by the cannula, and then on the abdomen are exposed to the radiofrequency attachment.
  5. Enhanced liposuction is a special version of the classical vacuum procedure, in which special manipulators are additionally used.

Obstructions for liposuction

Any kind of surgeries, including cosmetic ones, is contraindicated for people with diabetes, arterial hypertension, atherosclerosis, diseases of the circulatory system and a number of other dangerous ailments.

Also, the reason for refusing to perform liposuction is a recent surgery on the abdomen or lung disease. It should be noted that there are no age restrictions for the artificial removal of fat masses from the abdominal region, but in the elderly, due to a decrease in skin elasticity, the procedure may not give the expected effect.

The maximum volume of adipose tissue, which can be disposed of in one procedure is about two liters. When removing large volumes in the body, a protective function activates the active set of fat tissues.

Duration of the operation to remove fat from the abdomen

The procedure for removing surpluses of lipid masses stored by the body can last for half an hour, and can last for up to three times longer. It directly depends on the removed volume of fatty deposits.

The choice of anesthesia option remains with the patient.When choosing a variant with puncture of the abdomen, traces of the operation will disappear two to three months after the procedure.

In addition, after going through the liposuction of the abdomen, it is highly recommended to wear compressive clothes during recovery period.

Methods of performing the liposuction procedure

In plastic surgery, there are several types of liposuction of the abdomen. Depending on their features, they are divided into the following:
  1. Ultrasound liposuction, this extra fat mass is destroyed by the impact on them with special sound frequencies.
  2. Classical or vacuum liposuction is the most common option, in which fatty tissues are dissolved with special mixtures, and then pumped out.
  3. Laser liposuction – the destruction of fat by laser light.
  4. Radiofrequency liposuction – excess of lipid cells destroy by the cannula, and then on the abdomen are exposed to the radiofrequency attachment.
  5. Enhanced liposuction is a special version of the classical vacuum procedure, in which special manipulators are additionally used.

Obstructions for liposuction

Any kind of surgeries, including cosmetic ones, is contraindicated for people with diabetes, arterial hypertension, atherosclerosis, diseases of the circulatory system and a number of other dangerous ailments.

Also, the reason for refusing to perform liposuction is a recent surgery on the abdomen or lung disease. It should be noted that there are no age restrictions for the artificial removal of fat masses from the abdominal region, but in the elderly, due to a decrease in skin elasticity, the procedure may not give the expected effect.

The maximum volume of adipose tissue, which can be disposed of in one procedure is about two liters. When removing large volumes in the body, a protective function activates the active set of fat tissues.

Duration of the operation to remove fat from the abdomen

The procedure for removing surpluses of lipid masses stored by the body can last for half an hour, and can last for up to three times longer. It directly depends on the removed volume of fatty deposits.

The choice of anesthesia option remains with the patient.When choosing a variant with puncture of the abdomen, traces of the operation will disappear two to three months after the procedure.

In addition, after going through the liposuction of the abdomen, it is highly recommended to wear compressive clothes during recovery period.

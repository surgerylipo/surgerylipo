Liposuction In Baton Rouge Louisiana

Among all types of plastic surgery, liposuction is the most popular today. This is not surprising. Fashion dictates its demands – and among them the need to have a beautiful, tight body at any age.

The liposuction procedure in Baton Rouge helps to surgically correct the outline of the figure by removing excess fatty tissue. Of course, with general obesity, liposuction will not help – no even the best liposuction clinics will perform “whole body liposuction”.

Liposuction is by no means a method of losing weight. This is an aesthetic operation. A plastic surgeon will prescribe liposuction in cases where excess fat deposits are located locally – in one or more areas and break the contours of the body.

This so-called reserve fatty tissue is postponed in the “problem zones” and, as a rule, does not go away either in the sports club or after the diet.

If the contours of the body are far from ideal, liposuction brings brilliant results, instantly relieving the abdomen from fat, cellulite on the hips, “ears” on the sides or the second chin.

The benefits of liposuction are palpable: self-esteem rises, self-confidence, many problems of the psychological plan are eliminated. Therefore, the popularity of liposuction for both men and women is constantly growing.

How is liposuction performed?

The operation is quite simple. Selecting and marking the zones for liposuction, the plastic surgeon inserts a special solution into the fatty tissue through an incision of 5-7 mm on the skin, which destroys the fat cells, turning them into an emulsion.

During the operation, this emulsion is sucked off using a cannula – thin tubes, whose diameter does not exceed 2-4 millimeters attached to the vacuum suction.

Lipoplasty Might Well Be The Answer For Body Contouring

It is also practiced using the thinnest needles, whose diameter does not exceed 2 mm, which allows to accurately simulate the contours of your new body.

Often, liposuction is performed under local anesthesia, but when removing large amounts of fat tissue or in cases of simultaneous liposuction of several zones, it is better to use general or intravenous anesthesia. In addition to the solution, a drug is introduced into the operated area, which prevents the appearance of edema after liposuction.

The procedure for liposuction lasts from 30 minutes to several hours. After surgery, cosmetic sutures are applied to the incisions. If necessary, a combination of liposuction with abdominoplasty, breast augmentation, facelift, laser polishing or other plastic surgery is possible.

So, if there is an excess of skin during liposuction, it is removed. Therefore, liposuction of the chin is often combined with facelift. To achieve outstanding cosmetic effect, specialists successfully practice the combination of various plastic operations.

Postoperative period after liposuction in Baton Rouge

The recovery period after liposuction takes place quite quickly and painlessly. The first time after liposuction, the patient remains in the clinic under the supervision of a d octor: such rehabilitation after liposuction lasts from several hours to 3-5 days, depending on the volume of the removed fatty tissue and the technique of liposuction.

As a rule, on the second day after liposuction the patient is discharged home and he can return to his habitual way of life. Sutures are usually removed about a week after liposuction.

However, recovery after liposuction usually takes about a month (all depends on the volume of the procedure and the patient’s body). During this time it is recommended to wear special compression underwear after liposuction, undergo a course of lymphatic drainage massage, and refuse active sports, bath and sauna. Sunbathing after liposuction in the sun or in the solarium can not be a month. Of course, going to the beach after liposuction is also not recommended.

Many patients are concerned about the results of the liposuction.

If you carefully follow all the doctor’s recommendations, wearing special underwear after liposuction and undergoing the necessary restorative procedures, the results of liposuction can be estimated after about a month or one and a half.

This is due to the fact that skin tightening, healing of the scars and alignment of the body contour after liposuction is not immediately: some fat cells destroyed in the process of liposuction, dissolve later, in addition in the postoperative period, swelling and bruising after liposuction, also disappearing within the first month.

Water Assisted Liposuction Before And After

View successful results of Water-Assisted Liposuction (Body-Jet, Aqualipo, Water Liposuction, Water Jet Liposuction) liposuction patients who have been treated by top plastic surgeons.

Body-Jet is a new FDA approved, water-assisted liposuction technique that has become available in the United States during the past year.

Body-Jet offers many advantages over conventional liposuction. Unlike traditional liposuction, Body-Jet uses a pulsating spray of water to dislodge fat cells without the force utilized in traditional liposuction.

This makes the technique less traumatic and typically results in less bruising and discomfort following treatment.

In addition this technique may provide more accuracy for the surgeon because there is less swelling and this enables the surgeon to be able to sculpt the area more precisely.

Furthermore, patients can usually be treated under local anesthesia and report less discomfort and a quicker recovery than with traditional liposuction.

An added benefit of body-jet is that the fat cells can be harvested making them more suitable for fat grafting in other areas.

Because the fat cells remain intact, they can be transplanted with a better survival rate and used for augmenting other areas of the body, such as the face, hands, and buttocks. As with any cosmetic procedure, the qualifications and experience of your plastic surgeon will significantly improve the outcome of your treatment.

Body Jet is another good technique and can be a good option for liposuction

Body jet uses a water spray to gently wash out the fat cell. Smart Lipo uses a laser to melt the cells, and power assist liposuction uses a vibration motion to help remove the fat.

Surgeons use all techniques, and sometimes combine them, depending on the patients specific needs.

For example, the body jet can be also employed to save the fat cells for injection into areas that have lost volume – face, hands etc Smart Lipo will produce extra tightening, particularly noticeable in smaller areas such as the neck.

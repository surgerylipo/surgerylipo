Contraindications to liposuction

As with any other operation, liposuction has a number of contraindications, which include:

  1. diabetes
  2. violation of blood clotting function
  3. pregnancy and lactation
  4. severe somatic diseases (diseases of internal organs)
  5. thrombophlebitis (varicose veins in the area of the proposed operation)
  6. Recently carried out operations on the site planned for liposuction
  7. oncological diseases.

Postoperative period

The recovery period after liposuction in Wilmington, NC is short and painless enough. Depending on the volume of the removed fatty tissue and the liposuction technique, the patient, in most cases, with good health, can go home on the same day, and he can return to his habitual way of life. During the recovery period it is recommended to wear special compression underwear, undergo a course of physiotherapy.

For the time of restoration, sports are not forbidden, because they speed up recovery. But it is better to abstain from visiting the sauna and solarium. The effect of liposuction is enhanced within 6 months, depending on the individual characteristics of the patient and the correct postoperative care.

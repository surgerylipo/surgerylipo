Classical (vacuum) liposuction in Boulder

The standard operation to correct the fat areas of the body is carried out under general anesthesia and can take place in one of three ways:

Dry method. This variant was invented by the very first in 1974. Through incisions 3-5 mm long, a special instrument is inserted into the area with fat deposits – a cannula, which can be straight or curved depending on the surgeon’s decision. The cannula is connected to a device acting like a pump.

The tool is injected and pulled out in a fan-like manner, which destroys the fat, and the machine pulls it out.

This is the most traumatic way of liposuction, while a decent amount of blood is lost, large hematomas are formed. After 2-3 months, the scar will look like a small reddish strip or circle.

Lipoplasty Is A Procedure That Uses A Tube And Vacuum Device To Remove Unwanted Fat Deposits And Enhance Body Shape

Wet method. It is similar to dry, but before surgery, a local anesthetic is injected into the fat layer, which softens the fat deposits. This method is less traumatic and more popular.

Tumescent method. The most popular of the standard methods of liposuction. It is carried out in the same way, but blood loss and trauma are minimized due to a preliminary dissection of the treated zone with a special solution. It consists of saline, adrenaline and anesthetic. This mixture for 30-40 minutes dilutes fat, which facilitates its removal. However, the recovery period remains the same – 2-3 months.

Laser liposuction in Boulder

This kind of liposuction was invented by Italians, it is based on a complex effect on connective and fatty tissue, together with the coagulation of blood vessels. Laser radiation is then fed through a micro-cannula (1 mm). From the mechanical and thermal irritation, the integrity of the fat membranes is destroyed and the vessels are cauterized.

The positive aspects of this method are:

  1. Minimal number of bruises.
  2. Collagen production for natural skin tightening.
  3. Low traumatism.

Local anesthesia is used, the restoration takes 3-4 days. However, after that, a long time will have to use compression underwear.

The average cost of the procedure is 2500-3500 dollars per area treated.

A completely different method is liposuction with a cold laser – SplitFat-system. This is already a non-surgical technique for removing excess adipose tissue.

Liposuction – what is it?

So, liposuction in Salem is a plastic surgery, the purpose of which is to remove superfluous fatty tissues.

Simply put, then the question of liposuction, what is it, you can answer that this procedure is an introduction under the skin of a vacuum pump with the subsequent pumping of excess fat.

Abdominal Liposuction By Dr. Dann Leonard, Salem Plastic Surgeon

In places of accumulation of superfluous adipose tissues a hollow tube of sufficient length (cannula) is inserted, which is connected to the vacuum device.

Indications for the described procedure are the presence of excess fatty tissues in the form of folds on the abdomen, hips, sides, neck, arms and so on.

It is worth noting that such symptoms appear not only in inactive people and in those who like to eat well. The accumulation of excess fat tissue is observed in many diseases, and in situations with metabolic disorders.

However, the procedure of liposuction will help to cope with the problem by far not all. The fact is that there are restrictions on the amount of excess fat removed. It is worth noting that it is dangerous to remove more than 18.8lb of adipose tissue, and, in addition, in this case, it is also necessary to tighten the skin.

Dr Keith C. Neaman, Salem Plastic Surgeon Non Surgical Liposuction

Let’s also note that liposuction is indicated to those people who can not achieve weight loss by running and other physical exercises.

Liposuction techniques

Liposuction is an operative intervention. Because during the operation, anesthesia is used.

There are four main techniques:

  • Dry liposuction in Salem, in which nothing is injected into the tissue, and the patient is in a state of general anesthesia.
  • With dry liposuction, an anesthetic is injected under the skin in places of necessary intervention. In this case, you can not use anesthesia.
  • But intravenously, pain medications can be administered to make the patient feel comfortable.
  • With super-wet liposuction, a larger volume of solution is injected under the skin. At the same time, its concentration is lower. However, the dose of adrenaline as a constituent solution rises.

Liposculpture Side Effects

In the modern world, people are ready to do almost anything to always remain beautiful, young and in good shape.

They make hair removal, apply cosmetic products to the skin, exhaust themselves with diets and physical loads, do various operations.

Liposculpture also applies to these procedures. What it can threaten future patients, we will look further.

Causes of complications after liposculpture

The reasons for why there are complications after liposculpture can be a variety of situations, namely:

  • technical mistakes of medical staff. This can be uneven pumping of subcutaneous adipose tissue, excessive pumping out of fat, non-compliance with asepsis rules and so on;
  • incorrect pumping of fatty deposits, leading to tuberosity of deposits, to hematomas can lead to the destruction of blood vessels;
  • If the rules of asepsis are violated, necrosis or inflammation may begin;
  • if the specialist will touch the nerve endings guaranteed increases the sensitivity of these places on the body;
  • sometimes the consequences result from various properties of the patient’s body, such as blood coagulability and skin healing, although the percentage of such consequences is negligible.

Possible early consequences after liposculpture

Complications after liposculpture can be divided into early and late, based on the time of their manifestation. The early ones are those that appear before the end of the recovery period. These include:

Seroma. This is one of the most unpleasant disadvantages of liposculpture. It appears very quickly: in just a couple of days.

The cause of this complication can be excessive use of a solution used to facilitate the breakdown of body fat. Sometimes this happens from inaccurate work of the surgeon, which violated the integrity of the lymphatic system, resulting in a lymph flow under the skin. The decision of such an unsuccessful liposuction is a normal drainage for several days.

Hematoma is another complication after liposculpture. The reason is clear to everyone: blood vessels are damaged. Small bruises do not pose any problems: they will go off on their own for some time. But, if the hematoma is large, you should not be delayed. It is necessary to show it to the doctor, who should conduct blood aspiration. If you do not turn to a professional, there may be bad consequences in the form of inflammation up to suppuration, which later turns into fistula or even septicemia.

Necrosis and septic complications are consequences of liposculpture with violation of aseptic norms. If such complications arise, then complex treatment with the use of antibiotics is not just necessary, but indispensable.

Sometimes the photo of the effects of liposculpture presents our eye spots on the skin. This complication can also be called hyperpigmentation. It can occur after excessive exposure to the sun, or independently.

Unfortunately, everything depends on the individual characteristics of the organism. And no one is able to remove this complication after liposculpture. Such pigmentation will remain for life.

Another complication after liposculpture, which is also often present is hyperesthesia. In these cases, it manifests a special sensitivity in the zone where liposuction was performed. Although it disappears fairly quickly, but there are times when pain is prolonged for a year or more. Then you need the help of a good doctor.

Possible late complications after liposculpture.

Imagine, the joyous moment has come: you are at home, seem to be healthy and well. But after a while your horror begins to manifest other disadvantages of liposculpture, manifested much later:

On the body appear unevenness after liposculpture. The causes of this complication can be technical. This is either the doctor unevenly pumped out fat, or the problem creates the patient, violating the regime. That is, this can happen if you have not used compression underwear for a long time or have given your body any physical load. It is also necessary to always remember that in some places of our body the fat layer is much thinner. This may be the cause of irregularities due to liposculpture.

Loss of proportionality of the body. It must always be remembered that we gain weight from the fact that our fat cells do not multiply, but increase in volumes. Therefore, there is an opportunity to gain weight, but already quite elsewhere.

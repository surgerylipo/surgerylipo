What is laser liposuction?

One of the varieties of liposuction in Honolulu is a laser, interest in which is shown by full and thin women who have excess fat deposits in various parts of the body. Laser liposuction is very common in developed countries, where the ladies already appreciated its advantages and were satisfied with the results. Fat cells are removed by laser energy.

Before And After Laser Lipo In Honolulu

The procedure is performed under local anesthesia or general anesthesia is used. The type of anesthesia depends on the amount of fat to be removed.

The method of laser liposuction is not complicated and safe if the procedure is performed by an experienced doctor. The working tool in the hands of a professional is an optic-fibrous very thin laser probe. Having made a small puncture, the doctor inserts under the skin a thin hollow cannula, the diameter of which does not exceed 1 millimeter.

Then a probe is injected into the cannula, emitting laser energy, which realizes the process of lipolysis, which damages fat cells. From the destroyed cells, fat enters the bloodstream and, together with blood, passes into the liver, where it is naturally neutralized.

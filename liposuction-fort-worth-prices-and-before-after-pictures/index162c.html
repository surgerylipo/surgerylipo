The body easily part with what he no longer needs (for example, with hair). But the main strategic reserves are always reluctant.

And on the stomach lie just vitally important tissues, both from the point of view of protection, and from the point of view of metabolism.

This is due to evolution: since the primates have risen to their feet, the stomach has become the most vulnerable place, and he needed a “safety cushion”.

That’s just such a pillow and serves as fat on the stomach: it protects the internal organs from damage. In addition, fat is easier to “get out” from here and use the internal organs, for example, producing hormones, because the products of fat cells are required for their formation.

25 Year Old Woman Treated With Liposuction By Doctor Steven M. Camp, MD, Fort Worth Plastic Surgeon

Therefore, no matter how it evolved human figure, but fat tissue will still be located in the first place, on the abdomen. In women with wide hips, the fat is evenly distributed and the stomach does not bulge very much. But those who have the so-called body shape “apple”, the situation is simply catastrophic, when with narrow hips and relatively thin legs in front formed dense prominent forward hemisphere. And it is quite difficult to remove.

In men, the situation is even more complicated: the peculiarity of their physique is that even with a sporty body shape and a low percentage of fat relative to the total body weight, the fat deposits “grow” primarily on the abdomen.

Abdominoplasty or liposuction – problems of choice.

Many patients think about what operation to choose – abdominoplasty or liposuction. A plastic surgeon makes a decision, depending on the condition of the subcutaneous fat of the patient and the aesthetic problem that worries him.

Dr. Steven M. Camp, Fort Worth Plastic Surgeon Vaserlipo

Abdominoplasty

It is shown when a large amount of adipose tissue and skin is excised, the navel is re-formed, the skin is stretched to eliminate the so-called apron (sagging lower abdomen).
At the same time, a small but scar remains on the body (in the clinic it is performed by a microsurgical suture with the use of special optics).

Liposuction

With liposuction on the abdomen, only two small punctures are made. Through them in tissue injected Klein solution, which liquefies body fat. This liquid substance is then removed through the same apertures by vacuum suction. The limited effect of liposuction is that at the same time you can remove not more than 0.79-1.06 gallon of fat.

It should be remembered that liposuction removes fat deposits, but does not guarantee a 100% reduction in skin.

Lipoplasty Slims And Reshapes The Body By Removing Excess Fat Deposits

So, abdominoplasty allows you to remove a large amount of adipose tissue, cut the skin flap, form a beautiful navel, but the scar remains on the abdomen.

Classical liposuction allows you to remove less adipose tissue, does not remove excess skin, hanging “apron”, but does not leave scars on the body, because there are no incisions – there are small punctures.

How to combine the advantages of both methods while avoiding their disadvantages?

Laser Liposuction Abdomen

In the Fort Worth clinic, laser liposuction is used to remove excess adipose tissue on the abdomen. It combines two surgical procedures:

– First, laser pills are injected into the skin through the punctures, fat is liquefied by laser radiation and is removed through the cannulas inserted into the same punctures.

Ultrasound Liposuction With Doctor Sacha Obaid, Fort Worth Plastic Surgeon

– Then, through the same channels, the laser electrode is reintroduced and the internal surface of the skin is carefully treated, heating the lower layers of the dermis.
Thus, a part of the fat tissue is destroyed, a new collagen is produced in the process of tissue regeneration after heating, its fibers create a skeleton that tightens the skin. Here  is the solution to the problem of “apron”!

The patient turned to the clinic to correct the unsuccessful result of conventional liposuction. After the classical liposuction, the fat deposits on her stomach were significantly reduced, but the unpleasant consequence was the sagging skin and the appearing crease in the lower abdomen.

Using laser liposuction, it was possible to significantly tighten the skin, while eliminating the additional volume of adipose tissue.

Anesthesia for Laser Abdominal Liposuction

Laser abdominal liposuction can be performed with local anesthesia, but plastic surgeons of the clinic believe that the best solution is a “medical dream”. Special medicines will simply “oversleep” the operation. Sometimes patients, waking up after the end of laser liposuction, are disappointed to ask: “How, have not you started yet?”

The safety of anesthesia in the clinic is provided not only by modern medicines, but also by the high professionalism of the full-time anesthesiologist-resuscitator physician who has extensive experience in plastic and reconstructive surgery.

Recovery period after Laser Abdominal Liposuction

After the laser liposuction, the patient is still in the clinic for another day and only then, after the plastic surgeon has conducted the examination, you can go home. Some discomfort after laser liposuction can deliver the wearing of compression underwear. This period ranges from two to four weeks, and most patients tolerate it quite easily.

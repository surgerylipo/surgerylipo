There are clearly visible biological disorders and harm caused after surgical correction. But there is another series of complications that require analyzes and additional medical research to determine their cause. We will dwell on several of the complications of both groups below.

The most common complications that also indicate natural or regular complications include swelling after liposuction. Swelling occur almost at all, but how quickly they subside, depends on the metabolism in the human body. On average, from a week to 10-12 weeks to different people depending on their constitution and the size of the liposuction zones may be required to completely part with this problem.

Accelerate the process can wear compression unerwear, as well as massage the problem area in an easy form. It is also recommended to avoid while taking hot baths, but drink at the same time advise not less than 2 liters of drinking water per day (but not tea, coffe).

Only with rare exception such complications as hematomas are not present in the zones of liposuction in the postoperative period. This problem is eliminated within 4-8 weeks by natural restoration of blood supply. There is also a variant of accelerating this process by certain means for external use, which you can learn from the surgeon.

Depending on the elasticity of your skin, after the correction of the shape, the appearance of unevenness of the skin can occur. As a rule, there may appear so-called hillocks, waves, as well as more harmless wrinkles and folds.

These problems can be dealt with through a special massage or, if you do not achieve the result, you may need to tighten the skin in the area of adipose tissue removal.

The next complication, which periodically occurs in the photo of victims of plastic surgery, is usually referred to as zone asymmetry. With the removal of fatty deposits, there is a similar risk, due to asymmetric features of the parts of the body. And, if this risk exists, the patient is warned about this variant of the development of events even before the operation.

At the same time, if you exercise due care for your body (sports in an easy, but permanent form), then such asymmetry will begin to level within six months. If the cause is poor qualification of the surgeon, it may be necessary to re-operation to remove the fat layer.

Completing the first part of the list of complications after liposuction, one should also mention the accumulation of tissue fluid – seroma – in places of fat removal. Such a complication is rare, in 1-2 people out of a hundred, and it is resolved fairly quickly and painlessly – the fluid is removed by the surgeon by inserting the needle into the problem area.

Stubborn Fat Pockets May Be Difficult Or Impossible To Eliminate

Then we will discuss more serious problems after liposuction in Mesa, as well as those that require additional tests and medical research in order to discover the true causes of their occurrence. These complications include a violation of skin sensitivity.

Visually, this is not expressed in any way, but it feels rather noticeable – the patient can feel either numbness or pain and tingling in the skin. Normalization of skin sensitivity is usually restored in the first 2-3 months.

One of the rare complications that can be caused by liposuction is recognized as fat embolism. This occurs when the fat particles fall into the blood vessels that are affected during the operation, which leads to their blockage. An example of symptoms may be shortness of breath and difficulty breathing in pulmonary embolism, when fat particles fall along the blood vessels in the lungs.

If there are such symptoms, you should immediately apply for medical help, otherwise – the probability of disability or even death is high. Most often, this complication occurs due to the small experience and qualification of the surgeon. For the same reason, there is such a rare and dangerous complication, such as a puncture of internal organs, for example, when the intestine is touched by a mini-tube of suction and injured.

Coming to a conclusion, we shall mention also such complication, as a necrosis of a skin. This extremely rare problem is due to technical errors, that lead to a violation of blood circulation in the liposuction zone. This threatens primarily by the fact, that the skin dies, and the infection begins to develop. It should be noted, that infections can accompany any kind of surgery, and plastic surgeries are no exception. Therefore, after the correction of the body shape by surgical means, it is mandatory to take antibiotics to secure and prevent infection of the body as a whole.

To summarize, I would like to add that sometimes patients who have problems with fatty deposits hide any diseases that are contraindications to the operation. This inevitably leads to serious (or less serious) problems in the distant (or in the near future).

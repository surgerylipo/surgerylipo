Liposuction In Gainesville FL Cost

Liposuction in Gainesville is the removal of excess fat on separate parts of the body. It is easy to guess that men are most concerned with the stomach, and women – the stomach, buttocks and hips.

This is purely a “local” operation. It has nothing to do with the overall “systemic” weight loss. Therefore, you can not expect that you will get rid of all your fat deposits.

For this there is another proven way: yes, it’s diet and exercise! Liposuction can not serve as a substitute for an active lifestyle.

But then why do you need it? Simply speaking, this is the most extreme case. All of us are born with a certain and invariable amount of fat cells.

Dr. Richard L. Nesmith, Gainesville Plastic Surgeon Arm Lipo

If you, say, gain weight, they increase not in number, but in volume. In some people these “bulky” cells are distributed disproportionately. And they do not want to “descend” from certain stubborn areas of the body.

Fat tightly sticks, say, to the stomach. Diets can not remove fat deposits, as you do not limit yourself.

All the rest (including muscles) can “melt”. But fat will stand until the victorious end!

Liposuction May Be The Answer To Get Your Body To Resemble The Healthy Way You Feel On The Inside

Here in such cases, liposuction helps: it “corrects” the disproportionate fatty deposits, in other words – removes the “defects of the body.” Superfluous cells are sucked off, and even if then you gain weight, the fat will be distributed over the body more evenly.

In general, the phrase “fat suction” sounds quite frightening. In the head there are shots of “horror movies”: a huge hose is inserted into the unfortunate patient, and … By the way, earlier it was like that.

On the skin, large incisions were made, large diameter tubes were inserted.

But since then, technology has moved far ahead. Today, thin cannulas penetrate the adipose tissue through apertures 3 mm in size, “hidden” in the navel or under the buttock.

So huge postoperative bruises and scars are a relic of the past. In addition, now before liposuction in the “target” zone, salt and epinephrine are injected: this reduces blood loss and increases the amount of “recoverable” fat. Sometimes for one procedure surgeon can “pump out” up to 22 lb!

Liposuction Procedure Is An Outpatient Surgery

The goal of cosmetic surgery is to help a person look better or younger. But all plastic surgeries make sense only with a healthy lifestyle. And it depends not on the surgeon, but on the patient.

Liposuction in Gainesville is a valuable tool in “aesthetic” medicine, but it must be understood that this is just a “varnish”.

Well, the lacquer is imposed, as you know, in the last place, when everything else is in order. Otherwise, something like a pre-sale preparation of the car turns out: everything glitters on the outside, but inside it is a complete devastation.

The operation should be decided when you have already done everything in your power: strengthened muscles, approached your ideal weight. Plastic surgery helps only those who are ready to seriously work on themselves – both before and after surgery.

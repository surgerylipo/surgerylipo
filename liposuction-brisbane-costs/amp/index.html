What is this procedure?

Liposuction is a kind of correction of the body by pumping fat from the problem areas of the body. It can be the stomach, thighs, chin and so on.

Only once you visit a liposuction session will you really feel light and see the real effectiveness of plastic surgery. For example, the waist will become thinner by about 4 cm, the shape will visually decrease, the muscles will tighten.

But you will see the result of the operation six months later, when all the processes of decay of adipocytes will be completed.

Liposuction Procedure Will Not Improve Cellulite

Liposuction is your step towards self-confidence.

To remove adipocytes (fat deposits), various methods can be used. Liposuction (the price depends on the chosen technology of correction of your body) can be of different types.

Classical – the cannula is used as the main instrument (a complex consisting of a needle, a tube and a vacuum device). It is inserted into the previously made incision – the fat cells are destroyed.

Laser liposuction – in the cannula there is an additional built-in beam, destructively acting on adipocytes. Disintegration products are removed through the excretory system or incisions. In addition to the correction of your body shape, due to the intensive production of collagen, there is a so-called skin tightening process.

Ultrasonic liposuction – the defeat of fat cells is due to the influence of waves. The adipocytes themselves are evacuated by vacuum.

Study the feedback and follow the experience of specialists

Deciding on the cardinal transformation of the figure, it is worthwhile to understand that you can not “throw” yourself in the future. Laser liposuction is effective, but it is important to first study the experience of people with similar problems, take into account all contraindications to the procedure, carefully prepare for the operation.

Basically, the services of surgeons are resorted to:

  • women after childbirth. In this case, the abdomen is most susceptible to negative effects – aspiration of fat and abdominoplasty is shown;
  • people with irregularly shaped body proportions;

  • those who had previously attended such procedures and the outcome was unsuccessful due to the inexperience and irresponsible approach of the doctor;
  • Athletes who, due to an incorrect approach to training, have acquired a disproportion of body parts.

Laser liposuction is contraindicated for people:

  • with acute chronic diseases;
  • with disturbances in the work of the kidneys, heart;
  • having problems with blood vessels;
  • With hepatitis, asthma, tuberculosis;
  • With oncological diseases;
  • with problems of blood clotting and diabetes;
  • with mental illness;
  • prone to allergic reactions.

The surgeon receives a full picture after a comprehensive survey – then all the pros and cons are weighed, possible risks, are foreseen.

Recovery period is a significant final step.

How long will you be restored after the procedure, depends on many factors, among which the size and feature of the area on which the operation was performed, the patient’s immunity and the general status of his body at the moment.

Laser liposuction for some can become a procedure for half a day (when the client is already discharged in a couple of hours). But someone can stay under the supervision of specialists and up to 3 days.

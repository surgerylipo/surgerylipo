Liposuction In Detroit MI

With the help of liposuction in Detroit, MI, you can remove excess fat deposits almost everywhere.

The main areas of fat removal are: lateral, posterior, inner thighs, buttocks, abdomen.

Liposuction is also done on the face, neck, chest, underarms, front thighs and even on the knees.

Many people mistakenly believe that liposuction will help them lose weight to the desired weight, this is not so. Liposuction is a means of adjusting the body shape.

To see the positive result of the procedure, you first need to lose weight and only then decide in which place the intervention of the plastic surgeon is needed.

Are there any contraindications to liposuction?

As with any other surgical procedure, it has contraindications. Liposuction is forbidden to minors, people suffering from diabetes mellitus, suffering from chronic diseases.

In the period of illness the operation is also prohibited, even if it is just a cold. Another contraindication is temporary: during the menstruation, liposuction is prohibited, at this time the risk of bleeding increases.

If you do not have elastic skin, liposuction is also contraindicated. Any type of liposuction is prohibited during pregnancy and lactation.

Types of liposuction and area of operation

The most common areas for liposuction is the hips, buttocks and abdomen. Liposuction in the abdomen can not always be effective. If the skin there is stretched after childbirth or strong weight loss, then, most likely, the skin will remain saggy. There are many varieties of liposuction. The most common techniques are ultrasonic, tumescent, vacuum and laser. Recently, this type of liposuction, such as liposculpture, is becoming increasingly popular.

Ultrasonic liposuction in Detroit, MI

Ultrasonic liposuction is suitable for those who want to protect themselves from possible scars and bruises. This method is not associated with surgical intervention. By means of ultrasonic waves destruction of fat cells takes place. With this method, excess fluid is eliminated from the patient’s body naturally. However, the effectiveness of this method is much lower than others, so the desired result can be achieved with the help of several sessions.

Tumescent liposuction

Tumescent liposuction is already a surgical operation, but it is radically different from other types. This option provides some preparation before the operation.

Under the skin of the problem zone, a solution consisting of physiological solution, lidocaine and epinephrine is injected. Due to this composition, the blood vessels significantly narrow, the nerve endings also narrow, which means that fat removal is painless. Usually, the amount of solution corresponds to the amount of fat that the surgeon must remove.

Fat tissue under the action of the solution turns into a liquid emulsion, which is removed by the traditional vacuum method. The proportions should be strictly observed, since a non-removed emulsion can cause damage to the remaining fat layer, which can take several weeks to recover. The operation is performed under local anesthesia. The recovery period after such an operation is only a few hours, and you can return to normal life after 3-4 days.

Vacuum liposuction

Vacuum liposuction is the most common, widespread and accessible technique from all of the above. Through this liposuction, excess fat is removed immediately with a vacuum device. The operation is performed under the influence of general anesthesia.

The surgeon makes an incision on the skin, through which a special surgical suction is introduced. The surgeon makes intensive suction movements to pump out all fat. Fat is collected in a special container with which the pump is connected.

Vacuum liposuction is carried out for several hours, does not require any preliminary training, which reduces the time spent in the hospital.

The postoperative period lasts about three weeks, during which time there may be bruises, swelling, painful sensations appear.

After a surgical operation, it is necessary to wear postoperative compression underwear. Since the connective tissue is formed at the site of liposuction, the fat in this place is no longer deposited.

To avoid unnecessary deformation and you need to wear special underwear, thanks to him the body will retain the desired shape and everything will heal properly.

The result of a surgical operation will not be visible immediately. In two weeks, the first changes will be noticed, and the effect of the performed procedure will be fully appreciated in six months.

Laser liposuction in Detroit, MI

Another method of nonsurgical action is laser liposuction, this is a newer, modern technology.

Under the action of the laser fat cells are destroyed, the by-products formed in the process, are derived from the female body absolutely naturally. Laser liposuction has a number of advantages over other options.

First, this procedure is painless, after it there are no scars and scarring. In addition to those cases where a large amount of fat is removed, vacuum suction can be used in this case.

Secondly, during laser liposuction, the coagulation process is started, the blood vessels are sealed under the influence of high temperature.

This process reduces the risk of hematoma formation and excludes blood loss. And, thirdly, the huge advantage of getting rid of fat with the help of a laser is that in the process of this procedure collagen and elastin are formed into the skin, giving the skin elasticity. Thanks to this, the effect of laser hair removal is permanently fixed.

Liposculpture

Liposculpture is a new procedure, recently appeared on the market of plastic surgery. This is also a surgical operation, but it is more complicated than other procedures.

The essence of liposculpture lies in the fact that the pumped fat from the problem areas by means of vacuum liposuction is distributed later on those parts of the body where it is desirable to add volume.

It turns out, fat does not go anywhere, but the body gets the desired shape. Before deciding on such a serious procedure, weigh those again one and all for and against.

We remind you, liposuction does not fight with excess weight, this means of adjusting the body shape, after losing weight.

Perhaps a balanced diet, regular exercise and your body will be what you want, and surgery do not need.

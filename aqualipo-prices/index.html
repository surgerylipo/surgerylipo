Aqualipo Prices

Liposuction is a special area of plastic surgery, where the rule does not work: the more complex and deeper the surgical intervention, the better and longer the effect.

Here everything is the opposite: the less the trauma and blood loss – the larger the area of the body can be processed and the more fat removed.

Everyone knows, that the classic (so-called “wet”) liposuction is a rather painful procedure. This operation is inevitably accompanied by traumas of connective tissue, ruptures of small blood vessels and nerve fibers.

So, liposuction is always accompanied by extensive swelling, bruising, pain during a long and rather unpleasant recovery period.

Aqualipo (Body-Jet Lipo) is a method of surplus fat removal with minimal health risks. This is not just another method, but a new concept!

28 Year Old Woman Treated With Aqua Liposuction Before With Dr Foued Hamza, MD, London Plastic Surgeon

An operation is performed under local anesthesia and requires less effort from the surgeon, besides, it is possible to evaluate the correction site during the operation, which reduces the risk of reoperative surgery.

The postoperative period after the intervention with the help of the Body-Jet apparatus is reduced to two days, after which the working capacity is fully restored.

Why is this method considered the least traumatic? Because this method uses the power of water – under a slight pressure, a fan-shaped jet separates the fat cells from the connective tissue, without damaging the vessels and nerves, reducing the risk of embolism and other complications.

Operation with the use of the Body-Jet is also more convenient for the surgeon, because it is easier for him to control the process, to evaluate it on the spot, besides, the fat draining into the sterile cannula is ready for further transplantation to the simulated parts of the body, if necessary.

33 Year-old Woman Treated With Aqua Liposuction Before By Dr John S. Alspaugh, MD, FACS, Virginia Beach Plastic Surgeon

It is the softest and at the same time the most effective technology available today.

With the help of the Body Jet device, it is possible to remove up to 2 times more fat tissue at a time than with old methods!
The postoperative period lasts only 2 days (instead of the usual 2-3 weeks!)

Having got rid of bored bulges and folds with the help of the Body Jet device, you can immediately improve your appearance by transplanting fat removed from the problem areas to where additional volume is required. The fat cells obtained with the Body Jet apparatus are an ideal material for lipomodeling.

  • absence of exposure after administration of the solution;
  • the possibility of conducting an operation under local anesthesia, qualitative anesthesia during surgery and after;

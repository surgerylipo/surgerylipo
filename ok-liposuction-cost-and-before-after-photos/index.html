Oklahoma Liposuction Cost And Before After Photos

Liposuction in Oklahoma is an operation to remove local fat deposits with the help of negative pressure (vacuum) with special metal cannulas through small punctures.

For the destruction of fat, ultrasound, radiofrequency and radio wave energy, a laser, a pulsating jet of liquid are also used, but these operations also result in vaccum evacuation of adipose tissue.

Liposuction is a therapeutic or cosmetic procedure, which consists in suctioning fat with local deposits in the subcutaneous fat tissue by vacuum or ultrasonic suction.

In one session, the surgeon removes up to 1750 ml of fat. The undesirable consequences of liposuction are irregular contours, high sensitivity of the skin, skin pigmentation.

Who is the Best Liposuction Candidate?

Liposuction allows you to eliminate fat traps – fat deposits on the abdomen, in the lumbar regions, hips, knees, legs, in the shoulder area at normal or moderately elevated weight. The maximum volume of fat removed should not exceed 5 liters per operation. Obesity requires consultation of the endocrinologist and weight reduction. At a heavy weight, bariatric operations are performed.

How much is the operation painful?

With small local fat deposits, lipoaspiration can be performed under local anesthesia on an outpatient basis. For large volumes, it is advisable to perform liposuction under spinal anesthesia or general anesthesia. The intervention itself is painless. After the operation, there may be pain similar to bruises or overloaded muscles. The intake of analgesics is usually limited to two to three days.

What complications can be caused by liposuction?

Liposuction is one of the safest interventions. When removing large amounts of fat, skin roughness can occur in the area of operation, reducing skin sensitivity. Liposuction does not eliminate cellulite. Very rare serious complications are associated not with the operation itself, but with anesthesia.

After the operation, it is required to wear special compression underwear, to limit physical activity for 1-1.5 months. During the first days, antibiotics are prescribed for the prevention of inflammation and pain medications. There are no further restrictions.

But you need to remember that liposuction is a figure correction that does not affect weight. To maintain its result, you must adhere to the diet and exercise in the future. To achieve maximum positive results 2 months after the operation, it is recommended that a course of vacuum or anti-cellulite massage be performed.

What are the possibilities of lipofilling?

Fat tissue can also be used as a means of filling volumes in areas where there is a deficit in it – to increase the buttocks, shins, breasts, eliminate wrinkles, postoperative defects. Fat is taken from the problem areas, centrifuged and injected through the needle to another location. At high volumes, fat intake is required in two to three sessions.

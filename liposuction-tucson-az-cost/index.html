Liposuction Tucson AZ Cost

Liposuction in Tucson is an aesthetic operation for surgically changing excess fat deposits in a certain area (or several areas), for example, on the abdomen, thighs, buttocks.

This operation allows you to surgically adjust the shape of the body, by eliminating excess in the places where they are locally located.

RESULTS:

If the contours of the body are far from ideal, in the sense of correcting the body, liposuction brings brilliant results, instantly eliminating the abdomen, the flanks of the “ears” on the sides or the double chin.

The operation time is 0.5 – 2 hours.

  • Anesthesia – General or local anesthesia.
  • Hospital stay 1-2 days.
  • Recovery lasts 2-3 weeks
  • The result is a tight body, clear body contours and elastic skin.

INDICATIONS

The presence of excess fat on the abdomen, hips, legs, hands or other parts of the body.

CONTRAINDICATIONS

  • Exacerbations of chronic diseases
  • Manifestations of infectious processes
  • High temperature
  • Severe diseases of internal organs
  • Diabetes mellitus, circulatory diseases
  • Recently transferred operations on the site planned for liposuction
  • Total loss of skin elasticity
  • Violation of blood clotting.

Stages of the operation.

Preparation for surgery

  • Before the operation, the surgeon will conduct a detailed consultation, answer all questions, collect a detailed medical history and perform a medical examination.
  • 2-3 weeks before the operation, you need to have the tests prescribed by your doctor.
  • Preparation for the operation is better to begin no less than a week.
  • On the eve of the operation, you talk with an anesthesiologist. 1-2 days before the operation, try to exclude from the diet heavy food.

Fat Removal Procedure By Dr. Raad M. Taki, Tucson Plastic Surgeon 10 Days Pre-op And Day 3 Post Op

Two weeks before the operation, you should also: give up cigarettes and alcohol (this will increase immunity and have a beneficial effect on blood coagulability), stop taking anticoagulants (drugs that dilute blood), give up perm and hair color, limit physical activity and exercise.

OPERATION

The operation is quite simple. After marking the zones for liposuction in Tucson, the plastic surgeon inserts a special solution into the cellular tissue through incisions on the skin, which dilutes the fat cells, making the cellular tissue softer.

Through a special tube (called a cannula), which is placed in the subcutaneous space, the excess of adipose tissue is removed in a vacuum created by a special apparatus.

In addition to the solution, a drug is injected into the area of the intervention, which prevents the appearance of edema after liposuction.

Liposuction Is Not A Weight Loss Method

Recovery period

For the postoperative period of any method of liposuction, edema of soft tissues in the area of operation is characteristic, which lasts up to 2-3 weeks.
The peculiarities of the postoperative period are the wearing of medical compression products for the prevention of skin deformity throughout the month.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will the fat cells grow back after liposuction?

Answer: Fat in the “fatty traps” may not accumulate, provided that after the operation the patient will carefully monitor its weight. If the body weight increases, fat will accumulate throughout the body evenly.

Question: Should I perform liposuction, if there is direct indications? (double chin, “ears on hips”)?

Answer: If the defect does not interfere with normal living, does not cause any complexes, does not harm health and does not carry an aesthetic threat to the contours of the body, then liposuction can not be done.

It should always be remembered that any surgical intervention entails certain inconveniences, especially in the postoperative period. It is best to deal with a defect in a natural way: diet food, sports exercises. Only in certain situations, when liposuction improves the condition in terms of getting rid of psychological discomfort or a serious aesthetic defect that prevents a person from living normally, one should decide on an operation.

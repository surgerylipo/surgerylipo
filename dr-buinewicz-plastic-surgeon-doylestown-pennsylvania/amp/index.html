How long does it take for Botox results to be seen? I got 27 units in my forehead and frown line
Time frame for Botox results
Oct 31, 2018
Botox typically start working within 24-48 hours. Full results are acquired at 2 weeks. If you still see movement at 2 weeks you should make a follow up appointment with your Plastic Surgeon.
How Long Before Botox Results Can Be Seen?
Full results of Botox
Oct 31, 2018
Patients see full results from the Botox injections at a 2 week period of time. Typically patients start to see changes within 24 to 48 hours however. Patients have been getting Botox injections for years with no long term issues. The recommendation is to make an appointment with your Plastic Surgeon for an assessment of short and long term goals.
How Quickly Should Bactrim DS Stop Cystic Acne?
Cystic acne with treatment of Bactrim
Oct 31, 2018
Cystic acne can take several weeks to get under control. Expect improvement in a 6 week period of time. Schedule a follow up appointment after a full 6 week course with your Dernatologist.

Dermal Fillers may result in lip lumps but should be rare. Massaging the area may resolve the lumpiness . After vigorous massaging a physician may inject Hyaluronidase to soften the area . The Hyaluronidase should resolve the issue.

How soon after Botox and dermal filler can I have ThermiTight on the lower face and neck done?
Botox and fillers and ThermiTight
Aug 29, 2016
Good questions - you can have ThermiTight done any time with Botox. The big question is where are the fillers placed? I would wait at least two weeks before considering ThermiTight in an area that recently had fillers. The ThermiTight procedure can generate temperatures beneath the skin that may affect the duration of your fillers.
Does ThermiSmooth shrink the fat underneath facial skin?
Thermi Smooth and fat
Aug 15, 2016
Good question! Thermi Smooth alone does not shrink fat. The Thermi 250, however, has the ability to shrink fat. It is all a matter of understanding the physics of the machine. Thermi 250 has 10 times the power of the Thermi Smooth and depending on what hand piece the physician chooses, you can target different depths beneath the skin (ie targeting skin/dermis for skin tightening, superficial fat for cellulite or deeper fat layers)...

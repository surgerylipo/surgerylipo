There are a lot of ways to lose weight. Some people who wish to “facilitate” their lives experiment with food, and there are so many of those who wish to remake the well-known saying: “How many women, so many diets.”

Others see a panacea in regular sports, grueling jogs and visits gym and swimming pools. Still others simply starve, hoping that this will give them an ideal body. In recent years, such a method of combating excess fat deposits, such as laser liposuction, has become increasingly popular.

Some people think that this is “fast weight loss for the lazy.” Indeed, it seems that it is very easy: came, paid (the average cost of liposuction is $3,200, according to 2016 statistics from the American Society of Plastic Surgeons), lost a liter or two of fat and became a happy owner of fine forms.

It’s true? What do doctors think about this?

First and foremost, liposuction is used only when other methods (sports and diet) solve the existing problem does not work. That is, when available fatty deposits are genetically determined. Liposuction is not a method of treating obesity (this disease is treated by other specialists, for example endocrinologists and nutritionists).

Of course, after surgical intervention, fat deposits in the problem areas will not return, but if you consume more calories than your body needs, hateful folds and rollers will appear elsewhere.

Liposuction in North Dakota is primarily shown to people who have small problems with the body, with a fairly elastic skin and not suffering from diabetes, cardiovascular or pulmonary insufficiency, cancer.

This operation is not performed during the period of exacerbation of chronic diseases and, naturally, during pregnancy. Thus, excess fat is successfully removed on the face, in the “riding breeches” and on the inner thighs, in the anterior and lateral surface of the abdominal wall.

There are several types of liposuction. All of them differ in details, and the essence of the method is as follows: removal of excess fatty tissue through the tube system with the help of special equipment; It is performed under local or general anesthesia.

After any such operation it is necessary to wear special compression underwear or bandages, as well as to strictly follow the doctor’s instructions, which will avoid complications: swelling, bruising, and the appearance of uneven skin.

When a vacuum ( “dry”) removing subcutaneous fat, it is destroyed in the problem zone and is removed through a small incision (1 cm) with a vacuum pump.

Syringe liposuction is used to correct small areas, for example, the zone of the second chin. The advantage of this method is its low traumatism, the disadvantage is a long duration in time in comparison with other methods. So, only a small amount of adipose tissue (0.5 liters) is removed.

Grand Forks North Dakota Liposuction Photos

Great popularity has “wet” liposuction, which allows you to remove a large amount of fat with the least blood loss and traumatism.

In the selected area is injected with a special solution of adrenaline, lidocaine and saline (painful only the first few minutes). After 20-40 minutes the fat tissue, which has become liquid, is removed.

Tumescent liposuction implies the preliminary introduction of a special solution, the volume of which is equal to the volume of tissue to be removed.

With ultrasonic liposuction, the fat cells in the operation area are ultrasonically destroyed and then also removed through incisions with a tube system. There is also liposuction using a laser beam, but this is a rather expensive method, and its popularity is low. Although blood loss and injuries with him are also minimal.

The incisions made during the operation of liposuction are small and imperceptible, after the healing resemble the usual marks of scratches.

It happens that during the operation there are excess of the skin and this has to be corrected by additional surgical intervention. The effect of the operation becomes visible after 1.5 months, and the final result can be bragged in 4-6 months.

There are also methods of non-surgical liposuction. Their essence lies in the fact that under the influence of ultrasound or special preparations fat cells are destroyed and fat molecules enter the bloodstream and are excreted by the liver cells as ordinary metabolic products.

This method allows to remove a small amount of body fat at a time, but has virtually no side effects, is painless, does not require special clothes after the intervention, and does not require much time (including recovery).

Carefully choose the person to whom you are going to entrust your beauty. A good specialist will choose a variant of the operation that is right for you and explain why he considers this method to be the most suitable. Read the reviews, talk with those who have already done liposuction … And let your choice not make you regret about the decision.

Laser liposuction

Modeling of the body with a laser is a non-surgical procedure performed under local anesthesia. Manipulation consists in the following stages:

  • Marking the intervention zone and determining the scale of the manipulation.
  • A thin cannula is inserted under the skin under local anesthesia, which is the conductor for the fiber.
  • The membrane of lipocytes is destroyed by laser energy.
  • One-stage coagulation of blood vessels that feed adipose tissue is carried out.
  • Stimulated the formation of collagen fibers and elastin – provides a tightening of connective tissue and skin.
  • The duration of the standard procedure is half an hour. With large amounts of intervention, the operation can last 2-3 hours. Laser liposuction is an innovative technique for correcting the body, which ensures high efficiency and long-term results.

Radiofrequency liposuction

This method of liposuction in Rhode Island is carried out under the influence on fat tissue of high-frequency radiation. Manipulation is carried out under the control of the computer, which determines the intensity of the impact. This allows you to avoid traumatization of internal organs or burn tissues. The effect is carried out by two electrodes: the first is inserted in a small incision under the skin, the second is placed above it. There is a heating of the fat tissue between them with the help of radio-frequency radiation, which has the following effects:

  • At a temperature of 40 degrees, the envelope of lipocytes begins to break down.
  • “Molten” fat is removed by a suction built into the inner electrode.
  • Heating stimulates the production of collagen – there is a skin lifting, which ensures the absence of stretch marks after the procedure.
  • Possible coagulation of damaged vessels – this prevents the formation of hematomas.
  • There is a special nozzle for each zone of the body.

The main advantage of radiofrequency liposuction is a short recovery period, which does not exceed 2 weeks. If there is a violation of the technology, there is a high risk of thrombus formation and damage to internal organs.

How is surgery performed?

A week before the liposuction, drugs are canceled, which can affect the coagulability of the blood. During this period, the number of smoked cigarettes is reduced as much as possible (on the day of the operation, smoking should be abandoned altogether).

It is important to have general clinical and biochemical analyzes, to undergo a medical examination. The day before the procedure is performed bowel cleansing. Obligatory consultation of an anesthesiologist for determining the type of anesthesia (local or general).

The duration of the operation can vary from 30 minutes to several hours (depending on the zone of intervention, the volume of fat tissue to be removed and the individual characteristics of the organism).

Progress of the operation:

After anesthesia, the instruments are injected through the micro-cuts into the thickness of the subcutaneous fat layer, which destroys the fat cells (using ultrasound, laser or radio frequency radiation).

Under the skin, sufficient vacuum is created, allowing emulsified fat to be pumped out.

Recovery period

Modern methods of liposuction of the body do not require a long stay in the clinic. With a smooth course of the procedure, the patient is discharged 2-3 days after the procedure. With laser liposuction, the discharge is possible the next day.

  4. In the first days after surgery, it is forbidden to take medications that affect blood coagulability.

Oregon Liposuction Cost And Before After Photos

Liposuction in Oregon is the surgical removal of “excess” fat, which can not be corrected with diet and exercise.

To date, this is one of the most common aesthetic operations around the world. Annually, it is used by more than 2 million people of both sexes.

Experts of our clinic use modern low-injury technique – tumescent. We take a saline solution containing a highly diluted local anesthetic, and before starting the operation, we inject adrenaline into adipose tissue.

Due to vasospasm, bleeding tissues during the removal of fiber by cannulas (3-4 mm) is significantly reduced.

Advantages

  1. no need for general anesthesia,
  2. minimal trauma and blood loss,
  3. uniform destruction of cellulose,
  4. pain syndrome and puffiness are poorly expressed.

39 Year Old Woman Treated With Liposuction Operation By Doctor Jarrod Daniel, MD, Portland Plastic Surgeon

In women, the accumulation of fat is observed mainly in the hips and buttocks, and in men – throughout the trunk, and most of all – in the upper half of the body (stomach, torso and neck). To remove the “fat traps” without the aid of surgery is impossible, so in this case it is absolutely justified from both the aesthetic and the physiological point of view.

And our specialists will perform the surgical intervention in such a way that as a result there will be no complications – tuberosity and unevenness of the contours of the tissues.

Important to remember:

Liposuction is not an independent way to lose weight. To maintain the result of the operation, you need to monitor your diet and exercise regularly.

The effect of surgical intervention directly depends on the volume and distribution of cellulose, the presence of excess skin and its elasticity.

So, in patients with fat accumulation only in certain areas, the result will be noticeable, and for people with a clear surplus of skin, their stretching and reduction of turgor this operation will not help.

Postoperative period

Recovery is easily tolerated by patients. The only side effect is swelling. There may also be bruising. We recommend wearing functional compression underwear for quick healing within 1-1.5 months.

Ideally, you should undergo a course of rehabilitation lymphatic drainage massage. The final aesthetic result of the procedure is estimated after 2-3 months from the moment it was carried out.

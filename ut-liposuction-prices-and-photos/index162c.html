Utah Liposuction Prices And Photos

Liposuction in Utah is the correction of the contours of the body surgically by removing the fat by vacuum suction. Most often, liposuction is used for local forms of obesity, which is associated with a violation of the contours of the body.

Liposuction is of several kinds: Vacuum is the oldest method. When using this method, the surgeon works manually. Very much depends on his skill level.

The essence of vacuum liposuction is that special tools are injected under the skin, subcutaneous fat is diluted, and then it is removed by vacuum.

The method is quite traumatic, because in the postoperative period within two months the patient should wear special underwear, bandages.

Ultrasound, the essence of which is that the fat is destroyed by ultrasound. This method makes it possible to get rid of a large amount of fat at once, but causes a thermal damage to the tissues. After its use, patients need to wear special clothes for a month.

Soft-lipomodelling. This method is the most modern and most sparing. In its application, fat is destroyed by high-frequency current and is removed through small punctures.

After the operation, the patient needs to wear bandages for two weeks, but the method does not require a hospital stay. The patient can return to the usual pattern of activity after 2-3 days.

Features of the postoperative period during liposuction

There are some features in the postoperative period:

  • It is necessary to purchase in advance special underwear or bandages, the wearing of which is mandatory. Unauthorized removal of the bandages is unacceptable!
  • after the operation, seals may form in the operated zone, but neither gradually decrease and disappear;

    • Dr. Kimball M. Crofts, Utah Plastic Surgeon Non Surgical Liposuction Candidate

  • In adipose tissue, channels are formed in which blood and tissue fluid accumulate. According to the doctor’s decision, they can be removed within 1-4 weeks or they can dissolve independently. Also, bruising occurs, but they disappear after 2-4 weeks after the operation.
  • The operation process takes 30 to 90 minutes, depending on the amount of fat pumping. The operation is performed under local or general anesthesia. Sutures are removed about a week after the operation.
  • Staying in the Utah clinic is not required, you can go home on the day of surgery or the next day. The final result will be obtained 2-3 months after the liposuction. You can begin your usual activity after 3-14 days, depending on the type of operation and individual characteristics of the organism.

Complications after liposuction

After liposuction, some complications may occur. However, in most people they do not arise at all or disappear quickly and imperceptibly. But still they should be listed.

Uneven contour of the body may result from uneven removal of adipose tissue.

The sensitivity of the skin may be impaired.

Violation of outflow of blood or serous fluid. This leads to the formation of swelling, hematoma.

These complications can occur with traumatic surgery or with great physical activity of the patient in the postoperative period.

You will significantly reduce the risk of these complications if you follow the doctor’s recommendations in the postoperative period.

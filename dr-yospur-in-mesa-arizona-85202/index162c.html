Will my nipples be raised a little more once the implants drop?
Post breast augmentation
Dec 3, 2016
It is very common after a sub muscular breast augmentation that the implants raise higher on the chest initially. This is as a result of contraction of the chest muscles pushing the implants higher. This causes the nipple areolar complex to become lower. As the chest muscles relax the implants drop and the nipple areolar complex elevates. This whole process can take several months.

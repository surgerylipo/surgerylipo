How much PRP do you actually get from your blood?
PRP injections
Nov 15, 2018
We usually draw 10 ml of blood which yields 3 ml PRP. For alopecia, this is injected in the areas of hair loss evenly. There is no standard methodology as of yet so make sure you find a practitioner that has extensive empirical experience in performing this treatment. We typically recommend 3 PRP treatments total at a 3 month time period. Depending on the results we recommend further txs.

Time off lipo

Botox brow drop
Botox brow drop
Jul 23, 2018
Drooping eyebrows are one of the side effects that can occur after botox injections. The forehead is an approved site of injection but sometimes if you inject the frontalis muscle in the forehead it can weaken it and lead to brow drop. As botox isn't permanent, expect the effect to wear off in a couple of months. Hang in there!

Liposuction is a surgical technique for removing subcutaneous fat deposits from various problem areas of the body. This type of operation is a safe and affordable option to eliminate the constitutional deficiencies of the body caused by excess body fat, regardless of the age and sex of the patient.

Let us consider in more detail indications for liposuction, prices, as well as reviews and how long the recovery period lasts.

Indications and contraindications for liposuction

18 Year Old Woman Treated With Liposuction By Doctor Michael J. Denk, MD, Virginia Beach Plastic Surgeon

Liposuction is indicated for those people, who need to correct the body in excess, local fat deposits. The procedure is carried out irrespective of the patient’s sex and makes it possible to eliminate the appearance deficiencies associated with the presence of excessive subcutaneous fat.

It should be understood that the effect of liposuction is primarily cosmetic, and such an operation is not a method of combating excess weight.

Many people believe that this type of surgery is one of the ways to treat obesity, cellulite or other similar problems.

In fact, the main function of liposuction is the correction of the body, the elimination of defects associated with fatty deposits (the so-called fat “traps”, usually located in the area of the outer surface of the thighs, the bottom of the buttocks, abdomen and the back surface).

Doctor Thomas Hubbard, MD, FACS, Virginia Beach Plastic Surgeon – 27 Year Old Man Treated With Liposuction

Contraindications to this procedure are common for all types of medical manipulations: oncological diseases, severe systemic diseases, blood and hemopoietic diseases, endocrine disorders (for example, diabetes mellitus), mental disorders in the patient, alcoholism, drug addiction, pregnancy and lactation.

Types of liposuction in Virginia Beach

To date, the following types (methods) of liposuction are considered to be the most effective and safe for the patient:

  • Vacuum-tumescent, it’s also mechanical. This method of action on fat deposits is carried out by specialized surgical instruments – cannulas, which are injected under the skin through small incisions, with the preliminary action of a special chemical solution.
  • Ultrasound is the effect of using ultrasonic waves.
  • BodyTite – liposuction with simultaneous processing of radio frequency.

In each specific case, the surgeon selects the optimal method of action, taking into account the volume and structure of fat deposits individually for each patient. In addition, in a number of cases, non-surgical liposuction, without skin incisions, can be performed.

Depending on the types of liposuction and the effectiveness of the procedure may differ: the duration of the postoperative period, the amount of fat that can be removed in one procedure.

Liposuction: body parts

In certain areas of the body, modern minimally invasive BodyTite liposuction can be used, which helps to reduce the skin after the procedure. Preparation for the procedure

After the patient has chosen the clinic and determined the zones of the operation, some preparation for the procedure is needed. This preparing includes:

  • General examination to determine the state of health, clinical blood and urine tests.
  • In addition, there is a hardware diagnosis – ECG and chest X-ray.
  • 10-12 days before the operation, it is recommended to stop using alcohol, smoking, restrict coffee consumption.

    • Improvement Of Body Proportions

  • On the day before surgery, it is advisable not to eat heavy fatty foods, dinner should be light.
  • In the preoperative period, it is strongly recommended not to take antibiotics, drugs that affect blood clotting (aspirin), tranquilizers.
  • If the patient conducts medical treatment, assuming the reception of such groups of drugs, the operation should be postponed until the end of the course of therapy.

Progress of the operation

Liposuction is performed under local and general anesthesia, the duration of the operation is from 30 minutes to several hours. Duration depends on a number of factors: the zone of operation, the amount and nature of fat deposits, the individual characteristics of the patient’s body.

For example, the buttocks require less prolonged exposure than the stomach.

Liposuction is performed by sucking off subcutaneous fat with the help of special equipment that allows creating sufficient vacuum. In problem areas, micro-incisions are performed, through which special tools or chemical solutions are introduced, which destroy fat deposits. Then they are pumped out with a vacuum. This technology allows you to minimize trauma and blood loss.

Recovery after liposuction in Virginia Beach

  1. Depending on the type and volume of the operation, the patient can go home from the clinic on the same day or the next day.
  2. In the first days after liposuction, it is not recommended to take drugs that affect blood clotting, take hot baths, visit the pool or sauna.
  3. Also, recovery after liposuction involves maintaining a low-salt diet in the first two weeks of the postoperative period. Depending on the area of operation, the surgeon may recommend wearing compression linen. During the first two weeks, you need to exclude physical activity (including exercise), as well as not to sunbathe.

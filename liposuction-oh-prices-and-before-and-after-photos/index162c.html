Before proceeding directly to the results of liposuction in Ohio, one should dwell on the question concerning the duration of the operation.

It is possible to determine approximately the duration of manipulation to remove excess weight only based on the knowledge of the specific features of a particular part of the body, the type of liposuction chosen, and also on the basis of the volume of fat deposits.

The minimum duration is about half an hour, and the maximum is not limited to hours. Nevertheless, you can see the difference before and after liposuction on the day of the operation. It should also be understood the difference between the cosmetology procedure (non-surgical liposuction) and liposuction, requiring surgical intervention.

At the same time, hardware correction (LPG massage, shock wave therapy, cavitation and injection techniques) is atraumatic, has no rehabilitation period and does not introduce any radical changes into the normal course of life.

West Palm Beach Liposuction Before And After Photos

Liposuction is one of the most popular cosmetic operations for both men and women in West Palm Beach.

Every year in the world about two million people choose liposuction.

The results of liposuction can be noticed almost immediately, which can significantly help to increase your faith in your appearance.

Liposuction is more than just a cosmetic operation, sometimes the term liposculpture is used. Sometimes diets and trainings can not rid you of “stubborn” fat deposits. Then liposuction comes to the rescue.

Liposuction is an elective surgical procedure that removes adipose tissue through vacuum aspiration.

About liposuction is better to think not as a way to remove weight, but as a procedure for creating a new contour (abdomen, thighs …).

Patients are advised not to think about the amount of fat removed, but about the desired shape of the body area.

Indications for liposuction

Indications for liposuction are localized accumulations of adipose tissue.

The most commonly performed liposuction of the following areas:

  • stomach,
  • Thighs,
  • And also: the chin, knees, cheeks, hands …

Operation technique

Liposuction in West Palm Beach is a surgical procedure. During liposuction, small incisions are made in places where unwanted fat is accumulated. Through them, the treated adipose tissue is treated with a special solution, after which the fat tissue is removed by hollow cannula tubes by vacuum aspiration.

Types of liposuction are ultrasonic and hydrodynamic liposuction. They are less traumatic, but require 30-40% more time.

Liposuction can be performed in addition to other surgical operations: the chin with the plastic surgery of the neck, abdomen with abdominoplasty, the inner surface of the knees with shin plastic and so on.

Anesthesia with liposuction

  • Anesthesia with liposuction depends on the volume and location of the fat tissue to be removed.
  • When liposuction of the abdomen and flanks is usually applied general anesthesia.
  • When removing small accumulations of fat from the chin, cheeks, knees, local anesthesia with sedative therapy is used.
  • With liposuction of the hips and knees, a spinal (epidural) anesthesia can be used.

Duration of the operation

On average, liposuction lasts from 30 minutes to several hours, depending on the volume of the operation. Of course, removing a few gallons from the abdomen or thighs takes longer than liposuction of the cheeks.

Hospital stay

After general anesthesia with liposuction, we recommend that you spend the first day in the hospital.
It should be understood that liposuction of large amounts of fat in the abdomen and / or thighs is a serious surgical trauma and may require intensive therapy in the first hours after surgery.

Doctor Kris M. Reddy, MD, FACS, West Palm Beach Plastic Surgeon – Neck And Submental Liposuction Before And After Photos

Of course, with “small” amounts of liposuction of the chin, cheeks, using a local anesthetic, the operation will be outpatient.

Postoperative period and recovery

  • The early postoperative period after liposuction is accompanied by temporary painful sensations, usually occurring within a few days. To accelerate the recovery period and reduce the risks of complications in the postoperative period, you must follow all the recommendations of your doctor.
  • Remember to wear special compression underwear, especially after liposuction of the abdomen and thighs.
  • To a daily life with some restrictions it is possible to proceed 3-4 days after the operation.
  • Within a month we do not recommend sports, solariums, baths.
  • The nearest result of liposuction can be appreciated after 1.5 months, the final result in 4-6 months.

Risks

  • Modern surgical techniques allow to reduce possible risks to a minimum.
  • Possible complications can be found in informed consent to the liposuction operation.
  • If you decide to liposuction, we expect that the information provided was useful for you.
  • For more information on how liposuction can help you, about the indications and contraindications of the method, we recommend that you consult a plastic surgeon who has experience of such operations.

Is it possible to get a breast lift without getting implants?
Breast lift or augmentation without implant.
Oct 28, 2013
Breast lift is a separate procedure from breast augmentation. As much as in minor cases of breast sagging an implant can accomplish a lift effect but that is not a lift. A small amount of breast enlargement can be otherwise accomplished with fat grafting..
Male Breast reduction?
Male large breasts. Gynecomastia
Oct 23, 2013
Male breasts that are too large are not uncommon. Treatment for this condition has evolved over the years and at this point it is best to consider liposuction over surgical excision in most instances. A combination procedure may be necessary in some cases. Excess skin would retract in younger patients eliminating the need for lifting except in extreme circumstances. My preferred liposuction technique at this point is using Laser assissted liposuction.
Is 16 to Young for a Breast Reduction?
Breast reduction under 18
Oct 14, 2013
If your breasts are too large at age 16 they are not likely to get smaller as you age. If your large breasts are symptomatic with neck, shoulder, and back, pain a breast reduction procedure may be beneficial. Keep in mind the small risk of nipple numbness.
I Am 16 with Parental Consent. Will the Plastic Surgeon Operate on Me?
Breast augmentation under 18.
Oct 14, 2013
I strongly recommend to wait at least till age 18. At 16 your body is not fully mature and natural physiology may eliminate the need for this procedure. Breast implants in your age would be more justified in cases of asymmetry or absence of the breast.
Radiesse Side Effects
Radiesse side effects
Sep 25, 2013
Side effects from Radiesse are not common. Most have to do with bruising (could be covered with makeup) occasional lumping (treated by massaging) Rarely asymmetry. Radiesse is best used in the naso-labial folds or in the cheeks. Also successfully used to improve the appearance of prominent dorsal hand veins.

Liposuction in Chula Vista is a body contouring surgery which aims to remove fatty deposits from areas of the body such as the upper thighs or abdomen.

The surgery had become one of the most commonly performed surgeries.

Liposuction was invented in 1974 by Dr Giorgio Fisher a gynaecologist from Italy. It has developed over time into a state of the art cosmetic procedure. Liposuction now has a number of sister surgeries available such as Tumescent and accusculpt.

Although liposuction is fairly new, surgeons have attempted to remove fat cells in a number of different ways. Today technologies involve treatments such as liposculpture, which is a little more time consuming than liposuction with a greater requirement for skill. This procedure creates a much smoother end result, changing the contours of the body or face.

The procedure involves inserting a small tube like syringe through the skin. The fat is then drawn up through the tube using a suction technique leaving a much smoother surface. The procedure is carried out under general anaesthetic.

The surgery aims to beat stubborn areas of fat which are resistant to diet or exercise.

Alternative methods such as the tumescent technique have no need for general anaesthetic. This method sucks fat tissue with thin cannulas. In addition to this power assisted liposuction, laser assisted and water assisted liposuction is also available.

Body shaping via surgery is becoming extremely popular and with such a growing range of methods available you are spoilt for choice. It is however important that more natural techniques are also considered.

Possible Liposuction Complications

Dr. Tom J. Pousti, Chula Vista Plastic Surgeon Abdomen Lipo Before And After

Every surgery has possible risks, and the same is true even with a liposuction treatment. There are several liposuction complications that you need to know and discuss with your doctor. Even new liposuction techniques that use new and advanced technology still have risks involved. One example is Vaser.

Although it is safer compared to traditional lipo methods, the use of ultrasonic energy may injure the skin and deeper tissues when used excessively. Other Vaser liposuction complications may include fat and blood clots.

Severe forms of these risks can lead to the development of serious illness especially if the fat and blood clots migrate to the lungs.

Several surveys and studies have been done to record and show liposuction complications. One of such survey was conducted by the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery, which was published in Aesthetic Surgery Journal on May 2001.

The survey presented the nonfatal complications from lipoplasty and lipoplasty combination procedures for 94,159 lipoplasty procedures done by ASAPS members from Sept 1, 1998 to through Aug 31, 2000. This include skin slough, ultrasound-assisted lipoplasty burns, deep vein thrombophlebitis, pulmonary embolus, excessive blood loss, fluid overload, fat emboli, cannula penetration of abdominal cavity, lidocaine toxicity, and surgical shock.

Liposuction pricing in Chula Vista is largely dependent on the area or region of the body where you want fats to be eliminated. Generally, fat removal from the chest and abdomen will cost more as there are more fatty deposits in this area, and they are usually more difficult to operate on. The liposuction price range for a lipo procedure done on the breast, chest and the abdominal area is around $3000 to $7500.

Cost of lipo treatment per body area varies greatly because there are other factors affecting it as well. This includes the location of the clinic and the technique that will be used, which will be discussed later in the article.

Areas from the hips and waist down to the buttocks and thighs usually cost less, which is priced at around $1600 to $5000. Meanwhile liposuction procedures done on the back, arms, and face may costs somewhere around $2000 to $5000.

As mentioned above, liposuction pricing is also affected by the geographical location of the clinic or hospital where the procedure will be performed. A liposuction procedure done in a more densely populated city or state will cost more due to the high demand in the area.

It goes to say, too, that a less populated state will have lower rates. It all depends on how much the demand for certain procedures is in the area. Cosmetic clinics located in metropolitan and urban areas will definitely charge higher as compared to those situated in small towns.

Also, cosmetic treatments offered in India and other developing countries are much cheaper in comparison to those provided in the US and UK. That said, many consider getting the lipo treatment to save more.

And the last factor affecting the price of liposuction is the type of procedure or technique applied to eliminate fats. This affects the pricing because different procedures make use different equipments. The new liposuction techniques will likely cost less as it uses less equipment (most make use of lasers) and recovery time is significantly less than the lipo treatments using old techniques.

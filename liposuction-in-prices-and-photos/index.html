Liposuction Indiana Prices And Photos

Liposuction, or as it is called, lipoaspiration in Indiana is a successful method of removing excess fat with a vacuum.

Liposuction succumbs to excess adipose tissue in various parts of the human body, and today this method is widely used in plastic surgery.

With the help of liposuction you can remove fat accumulations on the neck, hips (liposuction of the thighs), knees, front and side surfaces of the abdominal wall (liposuction of the abdomen), remove the lipoma.

But it must be remembered that liposuction is still a surgical intervention and not just a method of losing weight or fighting obesity, but an operation aimed at correcting one or another problem area.

The beginning of the liposuction method can be safely called a revolutionary event and a major achievement of plastic surgery of the XX century.

29 Year Old Woman Treated With Liposuction Before & After By Dr. Jeffrey D. Wagner, MD, Indianapolis Plastic Surgeon

Liposuction in Indiana is indicated in the following situations:

– Liposuction as an independent or combined surgical intervention: Liposuction of the lumbar region along with abdominoplasty, liposuction of the chin simultaneously with facelift.

The quality of liposuction and the new appearance of the patient after surgery depend not only on the professionalism of the surgeon, although this is also of paramount importance.

Much in a favorable outcome of this process depends on the elasticity of the skin, its ability to quickly contract and “sit down” to a new, reduced volume. In principle, it is the elasticity of the skin that is the main criterion for the doctor to make a decision whether to perform this type of surgery. There are no age restrictions for liposuction.

Liposuction Can Slim Or Reshape Specific Areas Of The Body

If you are thinking about how to remove fat from the hips, but have the following health problems, you will have to give up liposuction.

Contraindications are any diseases in the acute phase or worsened chronic illnesses, pregnancy, mental disorders, oncological diseases.

Liposuction in Indiana is also not carried out in cases of general obesity, with sagging of significantly stretched skin, with irregularities of the body, which are not related to fatty deposits. Usually, general anesthesia is used for liposuction.

But if correction is required on a small part of the body or with a small accumulation of adipose tissue, local anesthesia may be used.

Liposuction of face and neck

With this type of liposuction, you can effectively get rid of unnecessary adipose tissue and adjust the contours of the face and neck. Liposuction will allow for a long time and significantly improve the skin condition of the face. In young women, liposuction is only applicable to the area of the cervical fold or under the chin, where the fat deposits are located. Complications during this type of operation do not practically arise.

Liposuction For Male Stomach With Dr. Gustavo E. Galante, Indiana Plastic Surgeon

Liposuction of thighs and buttocks, abdominal liposuction

Liposuction of the thighs and buttocks is indicated only with insignificant accumulations of fat in these areas. When performing surgery, the surgeon pays special attention to the lateral surface of the body, as this helps to achieve the optimal effect in the formation of a beautiful body shape.

If the contractility of the skin is reduced, liposuction is supplemented with a skin-plastic operation.

If the fatty deposits are located in the middle part of the abdomen, above or below the navel, then abdominal liposuction or liposuction of the abdomen is indicated. The best results can be achieved in patients with elastic and soft skin.

How Much Can Liposuction Cost?

Fees for a liposuction procedure are highly variable and typically depend on a combination of factors, such as, facility location, board certified surgeon expertise and experience, number of areas to be treated, and technique employed.

In my practice, liposuction fees typically begin around $4,750 and can go up from there, depending upon what is discussed and elected at the time of a patient’s consultation appointment.

Liposuction “bundles” are very common throughout the country as well, since multiple liposuction areas are generally offered at slightly discounted rates for patients.

I would strongly recommend to anyone interested in a liposuction procedure to consult with a board certified plastic surgeon.

Keep in mind that the cheapest deal for liposuction is sometimes more costly in the long run, as price will often reflect the experience of that particular plastic surgeon. (Stephen T. Greenberg, MD, Woodbury Plastic Surgeon)

28 Year Old Woman Treated With Liposuction Surgery By Doctor Jeff Angobaldo, MD, Dallas Plastic Surgeon

Liposuction is a highly variably priced procedure. Why? Liposuction is a combination of artistic sculpting and the use of a machine, i.e. the liposuction machine. A liposuction machine can only remove fat at a certain rate.

Therefore, if a large volume/amount of liposuction needs to be done this will require a longer time in the operating room.

Unfortunately, reputable certified operating rooms are expensive. Thus, the more liposuction that need to be done; the longer it takes in the operating room; and the more it costs.

Depending on the area(s) you want treated by liposuction it may require position changes during surgery. Position changes take time in the operating room and also add to the cost.

Why does this matter? Because while performing additional areas are typically less; if the additional areas require position changes then it may not be as significant.

Doctor Delio Ortegon, MD, San Antonio Plastic Surgeon – Liposuction Surgery Of Abdomen

Great liposuction is separated by the artistic eye and artistic sculpting. This usually requires delicate shadowing, blending, and feathering.

This takes time. And again, time incurs costs in the operating room. My best advice that I give to my family and friends are: Good Quality and Quality Control incur an expense.

At some point, in order to cut cost the quality gets cut and more importantly the Quality Control or Safety gets cut. (Matthew J. Nykiel, MD, Ontario Plastic Surgeon)

Liposuction cost varies based upon the number of body regions treated and the time it will take to perform surgery. Costs may run as low as $3K for just neck liposuction, but will run higher as the number of body regions increases.

Average may be around $6K, but could go up to around $10K. (Michele A. Shermak, MD, Baltimore Plastic Surgeon)

Liposuction costs vary depending the number of areas treated. Some surgeons charge according to time spent per zone. An in person consultation is best to assess cost for this procedure. (John Nguyen, MD, FACS, Houston Plastic Surgeon)

Generally discounts are applied when you choose to have liposuction on multiple areas. Prices range based on the area and the expected length of surgery.

Prices also vary widely by regional area. Best to obtain multiple consultations from board certified plastic surgeons. (Richard Zienowicz, MD, Providence Plastic Surgeon)

When researching liposuction options it is very common to find a range of pricing depending on the surgeons office preference. I typically will price liposuction by the area for my practice.

I have found that basing liposuction pricing by the area my patients feel more in control of their surgery cost as well as the procedure.

One area constitutes both sides of the body which means if we are focusing on the inner thigh area the price quoted will encompass treatment to both the right and left inner thigh.

As part of your quote you will find the facility fee, anesthesia fee, as well as your post-operative kit which includes everything you will need for post-operative care included. By including all fees in one quote there is much less confusion on what is included.

Average cost of Liposuction

The average cost of liposuction depends much on the area suctioned, how much is suctioned and the complexity of the case involved. Getting a consultation with your plastic surgeon, would better narrow your cost for a better rate.

However, a ball park estimate would be somewhere between $2500 to $8000. Discounts are dependent on the office and physician policy. (Alexander G. Digenis, MD, Louisville Plastic Surgeon)

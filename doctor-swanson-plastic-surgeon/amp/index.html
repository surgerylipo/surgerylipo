Do I have tubular breasts?
Do I have tuberous breasts?
May 4, 2017
You do not have tuberous breasts.  They are large, droopy and have enlarged areolae. A minimal incision lift (lollipop), which would allow reduction in the areolae and reshaping of the breast tissue, would give you a very nice improvement. Liposuctioning the anterior axillary folds (the fat pads along the pectoralis above and lateral to each breast0 would further enhance the result. Good Luck!!!
What procedure would be best for me?
What procedure would be best for me?
May 4, 2017
Your genetics and your pregnancy have determined your breast size and shape, rather than your clothing or activity choices.  A combination of breast augmentation, to give some upper pole fullness and a breast lift will markedly improve the appearance of your breasts.  It will be important to select a surgeon with extensive experience in these combination procedures.  A Board Certified PS and Member of the American Society of Aesthetic Plastic Surgeons would be your best bet.

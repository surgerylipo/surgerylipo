Liposuction in Fort Wayne is a procedure for removing subcutaneous fat.

With it, you can get rid of fat deposits in the abdomen and waist, on the outer and inner surfaces of the hips, in the shoulder girdle and back, in the cheek and chin zone.

Age is not a limiting factor for the operation, although with a pronounced weakening of the skin tone, the result may not be as favorable as in young girls with local fat deposits, which have a normal, elastic skin that can be easily contracted after liposuction.

For one procedure, a doctor can remove not more than three liters of fat. Otherwise, the body will suffer serious damage.

This surgical intervention is not designed for patients who suffer from general obesity.

Do not forget that liposuction can not eliminate the causes of the appearance of extra pounds.

In addition, it does not relieve obesity, cellulite, stretch marks on the body.

Contraindications to the procedure of liposuction

Liposuction can not be performed:

  • people with diabetes;
  • people who have a clotting disorder;
  • a person with any physical illness (in other words, with illness of internal organs) in severe form.

What is Liposuction: Methods of Surgery

To date, there are many different methods and ways to perform this procedure.

Between themselves, they differ in the principle of the effect on fat cells for their destruction, the possibility of removing different volumes of tissues. Practically all liposuction techniques involve the introduction of a special solution into the subcutaneous tissue, which contributes to the destruction of fat and reduces blood loss to a minimum (the exception is the dry method of liposuction, which is very rarely used).

Vacuum liposuction in Fort Wayne

Currently, it is considered the most common and effective. This operation consists in the following: the surgeon performs small incisions (their length is less than one and a half centimeters), and then injects under the skin a cannula (tube) having one or several holes at the end.

With regard to the diameter of the tube, its shape and the specific size of the incision, it must be said that these parameters are directly determined by the amount of fat you plan to remove, as well as from the skill of the surgeons. By performing a forward movement (backwards-forward), the surgeon mechanically destroys and removes the fat tissue with a vacuum device.

During the operation under consideration and after it, as a rule, there is no excessive bleeding, nerve fibers, connective tissues are not damaged. The probability that after the procedure there will be hematomas, bruises and pronounced scars, is almost zero.

Ultrasonic liposuction

In this case, the fat deposits are destroyed by ultrasonic waves. In this case, the tissues are converted into an emulsion, which is removed, as in the previous case, by a vacuum method. The length of punctures with ultrasound liposuction is not more than two millimeters. Currently, doctors do not resort to this procedure so often, since ultrasonic waves act not only on the fat layer, but also disrupt the functioning of other organs and systems of the body.

Electronic liposuction.

In the course of this procedure, the discharge of electric current is applied to the effect on fats. As for the technique of the operation, it is similar to ultrasonic liposuction, but the surgeon uses a current rather than a laser.

Syringe Liposuction

Laser Lipo Fort Wayne IN

This procedure is designed to remove fat from small areas, for example, on the knees, chin, on the back in the area of the fatty hump. During the operation, the surgeon inserts a special needle into the place where excess fat is accumulated and starts to carefully pull the piston, creating a negative pressure. This type of correction is very accurate, less traumatic than all previous variants.

Tumescent liposuction

This operation is carried out as follows: doctors inject a special solution into the problem areas with a syringe, including adrenaline, lidocaine and saline solution (its amount should be several times greater than the volume of the treated adipose tissue). After that, the fat is destroyed, and aspiration is performed.

Non-invasive (non-surgical) ultrasound liposuction

This type of procedure is performed with the help of a special device, in particular, Ultra Shape Contour I. Liposuction of this kind is painless: the patient feels only a slight tingling, warmth. The device, acting on problem areas, destroys them. In this case, fat cells get into the blood and are excreted from the body in about two to three weeks. The duration of the liposuction procedure is on average from half an hour to an hour and a half (it all depends on the volume of the operation).

Complications of liposuction

Liposuction is considered a safe operation. In most patients complications, if any, occur in a sluggish, subtle form. The main types of complications include:

  • the formation of an uneven contour of the skin, which is connected with the fact that surgeons unevenly removed fatty tissues;
  • violation of the outflow of blood, leading to significant swelling, bruising, seroma. These pathologies are manifested in situations:
  • when the operation was conducted in an excessively large volume;
  • when during the surgical intervention the surrounding tissues were severely damaged;

  • if the patient exhibited excessive physical activity at the first time after liposuction.
  • violation of the sensitivity of the skin;

Significantly less after the procedure occurs:

  • anemia (observed when large volumes of fatty tissue are removed);
  • thromboembolism, that is, blockage of blood vessels (occurs if the patient has a clotting disorder);
  • Fat embolism (the vessels are clogged with fatty tissues). It occurs in most cases after ultrasonic liposuction.

To exclude the appearance of the above and other complications, it is necessary to strictly follow all the recommendations of the doctor.

Liposuction is a safe plastic surgery that allows you to get an excellent result. After the operation, the fat tissues are not restored.

Liposuction Washington DC Cost And Before After Photos

Liposuction in Washington is a plastic surgery, the task of which is the removal of local fat deposits in so-called “fat traps”.

Problem zones, as a rule, are the abdomen, thighs, buttocks, chin, hands, back, the area above the knee joints.

When it is impossible to get rid of by diet and exercise from fat deposits, the most radical method of solving this problem is using liposuction.

Liposuction occurs as follows: a special cannula (tube) is inserted into the subcutaneous fat layer through small (not more than 0.5 cm) punctures that are made in unobtrusive places or natural folds of the skin.

The surgeon produces destruction and simultaneous suction of fat cells to reciprocating movements. Depending on the volume and the zone, the operation is performed under local or general anesthesia.

The duration of the operation is from 30 minutes to several hours. In one procedure, 1.58-2.11 gallon of fatty tissue can be removed without harm to the body.

36 Year Old Man Treated With Liposuction By Doctor Navin K. Singh, MD, Washington DC Plastic Surgeon

Liposuction is often combined with lipofilling (an increase in body areas with insufficient volume of self-adipose tissue). Often such zones are chest, shins, buttocks, hands, cheekbones.

There are several types of liposuction:

Vacuum (classical) – fatty tissues are destroyed by a cannula using a special solution and are removed with the help of the same cannula.
Ultrasonic – the destruction of fat cells is by ultrasound, are removed with the help of a cannula.

Leading surgeons perform both classical and ultrasonic liposuction. This operation is rather complicated, because only plastic surgeons of the highest category are allowed to it, who have extensive work experience behind them.

The recovery period after liposuction:

  • The stay in the hospital does not exceed three days;
  • At first it is necessary to wear compression linen (1-2 weeks) for better skin reduction;
  • In 1-2 weeks, you can return to everyday business;
  • A month later a follow-up inspection is carried out;
  • In a month and a half you can go back to sports;
  • After 4-6 months, the final result will be seen.

INDICATION FOR LIPOSUCTION:

Presence of local fatty deposits.

CONTRAINDICATIONS:

  • Circulatory diseases;
  • Varicose veins in the operation area;
  • diabetes;
  • Infectious diseases of the skin.

In some cases, according to the indications, liposuction can be replaced by:

  • Brazilian FilamentLifting;
  • Abdominoplasty;
  • Cryolipolysis on an exclusive device ZELTIQ – an innovative technology of non-invasive (non-surgical) elimination of fat deposits.

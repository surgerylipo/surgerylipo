Today dozens of clinics of plastic surgery and cosmetology in Raleigh offer to do liposuction. This operation is an advanced surgical technique for getting rid of excess fat deposits.

This method of correcting body contours is suitable for people who have excessive fat accumulation in problem areas:

– At the hips
– Waist
– Back
– Buttocks
– Shins
– Arms
– Abdomen
– Neck
– Face.

Plastic surgeons help people to improve the contours of their bodies and make the shape more attractive.

Liposuction is becoming more popular. This is due to the fact that the use of modern technology allows to quickly and without complications get rid of a sagging stomach, “ears on the hips”, the second chin. The result is also the elimination of psychological complexes, an increase in the level of self-esteem and self-confidence.

Liposuction is not a method of losing weight, it helps to get rid of deposits that persist in defiance of diets, physical exercises and other procedures

What parts of the body can liposuction be used for? It can be done in almost any part of the body where excess fat is present. A prerequisite is the preservation of good elastic properties of the skin at the site of intervention.

Recovery period

Liposuction is, above all, an operation after which the body needs a certain time to recover. Immediately after the procedure for several hours, you need to stay in the clinic under the supervision of a doctor. In a number of cases, namely, when removing a large volume of adipose tissue or if there are any complications, there may be a need for longer follow-up.

In order for injured tissues to be restored more quickly, a minimum of one month of wear should be worn with special compression clothes. It must be purchased in advance, because The underwear is dressed immediately after the operation. At the consultation, the doctor explains in detail the patient what kind of underwear is necessary and what size. In the postoperative period it is strictly forbidden to visit saunas, baths, swimming pools, be in direct sunlight and actively engage in sports.

32 Year Old Woman Treated With Liposuction Before And After By Doctor Brian Coan, MD, FACS, Raleigh-Durham Plastic Surgeon

Lymphodrainage massage courses and other procedures are prescribed. The result becomes noticeable one to two months after the operation.

This is due to a relatively long process of reducing the elastic fibers of the skin, resorption of scars, hematomas and alignment of body contours. The final result can be seen only after four to six months. It is then possible to objectively evaluate the effect of the operation.

Stability of the result depends directly on the lifestyle, nutrition and motor activity of the patient.

Remote adipocytes are not restored, but when overeating, others will increase. This will lead to deposits in other places, and, accordingly, the appearance of disproportionalities of the body. If there is no significant weight gain, the effect will be long and stable.

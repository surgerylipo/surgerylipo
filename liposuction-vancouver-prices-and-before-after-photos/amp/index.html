Surgical intervention whose purpose is to destroy and remove excess fatty tissue from the human body is called liposuction. For the first time this kind of surgery was carried out in the year 21st century in the knee area, but surgical intervention was not successful at that time, as a result of which a woman who needed specialist help lost a limb.

At present, operative removal of the fat layer is an actively practiced and quite popular operation that is carried out without any serious consequences for human health.

What is indicated for liposuction?

The presence of external local changes due to the deposition of lipid tissue, the struggle with which the conservative has not been successful – this is the indication for carrying out an operational correction.

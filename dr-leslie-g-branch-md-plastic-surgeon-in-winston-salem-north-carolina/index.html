Can CoolSculpting help remove lipomas on abdomen and arms?
Lipomas with coolsculpting
Aug 23, 2017
The coolsculpting may initially reduce the size (similar to liposuction but not as effective), but the risk with both of those is that you have not completely removed the lipoma and has a high rate of recurrence or will continue to grow. Surgical excision is the only definitive treatment
Is it normal to have lines on lower lip after filler, but not on top lip?
Lower lip lines
Aug 23, 2017
Volume helps get rid of vertical lines in the lip, but the trade off is more augmentation of the lip if that is what you are desiring. If you do not mind your lower lip being a little larger, than more juvederm in the lower lip will help.
Was my Voluma applied incorrectly?
Volume asymmetry
Aug 21, 2017
Wait at least 1 -2 weeks. Continue massage in the area. If this persists then return to the injector for assessment in person and possible correction for symmetry. More Voluma should hopefully correct the dimple.
I had Miradry done yesterday at level 5 and I'm 100% dry today. Is this the final result? If not,
Miradry
Aug 21, 2017
3 months is usually your final result, but you may notice similar results before that. You may experience some sweat with vigorous activity but usually at least 80% gone. Definitely wait 3 months at least if you are considering another treatment
Is the tumescent technique the protocol for Miradry?
Miradry tumescent
Aug 21, 2017
Yes, but you shouldn't feel much after the first initial numbing. This technique allows the laser to get rid of more sweat glands in 1 treatment and more comfortable throughout the procedure.
Inner eyelid drop 5 days after Botox
Eyelid drop after botox
Aug 17, 2017
This can definitely happen after botox if it migrated down and affected the muscles in your eyelid. Depending on the muscle affected, your eyelid may be lower but still able to open and close similar to the other side. Unfortunately there is not much you can do except wait it out. Usually 3 months before the botox starts to wear off. You can inject the other side for symmetry but I would not recommend this with the eyelids.
Is this normal after Botox and Juvederm?
Bumps after botox
Aug 17, 2017
Its hard to tell from the picture, but you could have had an allergic reaction to either one. I would ask to be evaluated by your injector to better assess
Still noticing my forehead moving and my crows feet lines are still visible 13 days after Botox 40
Botox forehead
Aug 15, 2017
You will still have some movement in these areas and most people desire some animation, not a complete frozen forehead. If at rest your wrinkles are improved and only have slight movement, you may want to wait and see if you like that. You are far enough out to receive more botox if you would like if touch-ups are needed in certain areas though,.
PicoSure for new large black tattoo?
Black tattoo removal
Aug 15, 2017
Your skin may be more sensitive immediately after the tattoo and that may put you at higher risk for blistering after the laser treatment. Black is one of the best colors to remove with the Picosure laser. The size and type of ink in the tattoo can also determine how many treatments are needed, but on average the tattoo can be mostly removed with 4-8 treatments. There is no guarantee that it will all be removed but your color is at higher likelihood.
How many CO2 fractional laser treatments are required?
CO2 for acne
Aug 14, 2017
The CO2 laser can be more effective depending on the strength and number of passes. I'm not sure how deep your provider is going but this could be part of the reason why it is not working as well. The deeper you, the higher risk of burns, so you have to find a good medium. If they are already doing high settings and you have had 4 treatments, you may want to try a different treatment.
Kybella, worth it?
Kybella worth it
Aug 14, 2017
From the pictures, there is not much fat in your neck but would be better assess by exam. Kybella usually takes at least a few treatments and causes significant swelling afterwards. Kybella is not as significant of a difference as liposuction can be, but since you are thin you would not need that much either way. Either one would probably work for you but you may not get as much of the skin tightening you are likely looking for with the kybella.
Too much Botox?
botox for brow lift
Aug 14, 2017
You will start to notice the botox wearing off and thats when you should schedule the next appointment. That is usually 3-4 months. For people that get frequent injections, they can sometimes push this out even farther.
How many days does Botox begin to soften when the brows drop from too many units injected?
Botox heaviness
Aug 14, 2017
It will wear off by 3-4 months so will slowly start to improve before that. You could ask for less botox in your forehead or higher injections next time. Or possibly some in your brow to counteract the forehead muscles, so your brow and upper lids won't feel so heavy or stiff.
Why has my Botox not worked when any other time I had it administered it does?
Botox not working
Aug 14, 2017
I'm sorry you did not have consistent results. Injection technique (into the muscle), local of injections, dilution of the botox, or age of the bottle if premixed could all have been possibilities. Rarely people do develop a resistance to botox, but more likely one of these other options. I would try a different injector and see if you get different results
Can anything be done to alleviate a heavy brow after receiving Botox in the forehead and glabellar ar
Heavy brow after Botox
Aug 14, 2017
I would wait at least 3 weeks to see if it improves at all. If your brow dropped more on one side, then injections can be done to create symmetry on the other side. You can also inject in the eyebrow to help raise parts of the brow and counteract the depressors. You could ask for your botox to be injected higher on your forehead next time, staying at least 2 finger breadths above the brow.

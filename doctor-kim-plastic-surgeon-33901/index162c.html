Breast Augmentation Recovery: What Can I Expect?
recovery
Nov 18, 2015
My patients are usually back to not weight lifting type work within a wk. There is more pain with sub muscular implants than sub glandular and i almost always go under the muscle. I inform my patients not to do any heavy exercise or lifting for 1 month.. I have seen pts bleed after breast augmentation when working out 3 wks after surgery so in my opinion patients should refrain from such heavy activity for 4-6 wks.scars fade over 6 months - 2yrs depending on skin type.

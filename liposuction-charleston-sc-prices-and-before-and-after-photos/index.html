The procedure for liposuction in Charleston

Liposuction is carried out by various methods. And the order of the procedure depends on the chosen method. The method of performing an operation to remove excess fat is determined by the doctor and agreed with the client.

The quality of liposuction does not depend on the method of the procedure. Also, depending on the location of the procedure, the general condition of the client and the method of fat removal, local or general anesthesia may be used.

The procedure for carrying out vacuum liposuction is as follows:

Anesthesia.

  1. Through small incisions, a thin tube, a cannula, is introduced into the fatty tissue.
  2. The cannula is connected to a vacuum apparatus. Under pressure and with the translational motion of the cannula under the skin, aspiration of fat deposits occurs.

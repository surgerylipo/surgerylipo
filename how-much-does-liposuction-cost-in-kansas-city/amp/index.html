What methods of correction of the body are used in plastic surgery?

There are three main ways to correct the body – liposuction, lipomodeling and dermolipectomy. Depending on the initial situation, your wishes and medical indications, an experienced surgeon will determine which method of correction is most suitable for you.

Liposuction (abdomen, thighs) is a cosmetic operation for changing and improving the contours of the body by removing excess fat deposits from various parts of the body, most often from the abdomen, thighs, buttocks, neck, chin, upper and back sides of the forearms, shins and back.

Contraindications for liposuction

  • Exacerbations of chronic diseases
  • Infectious processes and high fever
  • Serious diseases of internal organs, violation of blood coagulability
  • Diabetes mellitus, circulatory diseases
  • Heart diseases
  • Severe renal and hepatic impairment
  • Recently transferred operations in the area where liposuction is necessary
  • Total loss of skin elasticity
  • Liposuction is completely useless for cellulite and stretch marks.

Preparation

In the process of preparation for the operation (not more than 2 weeks), it is necessary to pass the tests and preoperative tests prescribed by the doctor:

  • General blood test with platelets and duration of bleeding
  • Biochemical blood test (liver tests, urea, sugar, total protein, creatinine)

Operation

It is carried out under anesthesia. Your doctor will recommend the best option for you: local anesthesia, intravenous anesthesia or general anesthesia. This operation applies to simple and is performed through small, inconspicuous incisions. First, a special solution is introduced to reduce bleeding and tissue trauma.

Then a thin hollow tube, called the cannula, is inserted through the incision to detach excess fat by controlled reciprocating movements. After the loosened fat is sucked from the body by vacuum through the cannula.

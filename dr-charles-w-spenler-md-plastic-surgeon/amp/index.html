CoolSculpt after lipo?
Coolsculpting after Lipo
Feb 16, 2017
Thank you for your question and congratulations on your recent procedure! I advise my patients to wait 6-12 months after liposuction before considering a second procedure whether it be additional traditional liposuction or coolsculpting. What's the ideal weight for a breast augmentation?
Ideal Weight for a Breast Augmentation
Feb 2, 2017
Thank you so much for your question! I recommend that my patients are within 10 lbs of their goal weight and have a BMI of 30 or less in order to have the best result. A patient's BMI is directly linked to the probability that they will have some type of complication post surgery. A breast augmentation is an elective surgery and I would want to minimize as much risk as I can by ensuring my patient's are healthy enough for surgery.

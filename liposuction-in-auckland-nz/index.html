Types of surgical intervention for fat removal in Auckland

Plastic surgery divides all types of fat removal into 2 groups. To get rid of excess centimeters on various sites of a body it is possible operational or non-surgical way.

Invasive methods include vacuum and tumescent liposuction. In some Auckland clinics, these kinds of operations are considered obsolete, but despite this they continue to be actively practiced by plastic surgeons today.

The word “liposuction” came to us from Latin. Literally it means “pumping out fat.” The translation accurately conveys the meaning of this procedure. At the dawn of the development of plastic surgery, patients who dreamed to become slimmer were offered only one method of the body correction. It was called vacuum liposuction.

Superfluous deposits of subcutaneous fat were removed with the help of a special device, which, like a pump, pumped them during a surgical operation. The patient was given general anesthesia. Then the doctor made several cuts with a scalpel.

A special tubes were inserted, the free ends of which were connected to a vacuum that pumped out the fat. During one such procedure, you could get rid of 2 liters of excess fat. However, the effects of liposuction were often negative and even dangerous to the health and life of the patient. This method of correcting the figure is very traumatic. The recovery period often lasts from 6 months to 1 year. Vacuum liposuction is a full-fledged surgical intervention, therefore it carries a high risk of postoperative infection, which is difficult to combat, as getting under the skin, pathogens of various diseases quickly spread blood throughout the body.

Often, after using the vacuum to drain fat on the body, there are bumps and scars that require additional manipulations of the plastic surgeon after a while. The obvious disadvantages of classical liposuction made the medical community develop more sparing methods that allow changing the contours of the human body.

To replace the vacuum came tumescent liposuction. This type of body correction also refers to plastic surgery, but is less traumatic for the patient. The difference lies in the fact that during the procedure the surgeon makes punctures of a much smaller diameter than in the classical version. Fat is pumped out with the help of the thinnest tubules, which in medicine are called cannulas.

Tumescent liposuction allows to reduce the area of penetration of tools under the skin due to the special technology of dissolution of cells from which the deposits consist.

Before starting the procedure, a special preparation prepared on the basis of adrenaline, lidocaine and physiological solution is injected into the patient.

This mixture turns fat into a homogeneous mass, reminiscent of the composition of the liquid gel. In this condition, excess tissue is more easily removed by means of a vacuum device.

However, tumescent liposuction allows to remove not more than 0.5 liters of fatty subcutaneous tissue per time. Therefore, most often the procedure must be repeated several times. In addition, this method does not exclude the emergence of infection, because it is a surgical intervention, although it refers to minimally invasive techniques for conducting operations.

Despite the possible negative consequences, tumescent liposuction is recognized as one of the most demanded plastic surgeries in the world. Another popular procedure is microcurrents for the body.

Non-surgical forms of body correction.

The modern beauty industry is developing very quickly. Therefore, surgical methods are replaced by more sparing and less traumatic ways to restore the body to harmony and beauty. Non-surgical technologies include laser liposuction, ultrasound cavitation of the body, injectable lipolysis.

All these variants do not presuppose surgical intervention, but are of the nature of an ordinary cosmetology procedure that takes place in a specially equipped office for a relatively short time. Usually, removal of body fat takes from 15 minutes to 2 hours, depending on the technology chosen.

Laser liposuction is recognized as one of the most sparing methods of combating excess centimeters. Its essence lies in the fact that special rays through the skin affect the fat tissue, breaking it into small particles. This allows you to dissolve excess sediments, which are excreted by the body with the help of the liver.

The procedure does not cause pain to the patient and almost does not cause complications. Laser liposuction for men is carried out as often as for women, since it has long been a universal way of correcting the contours of the body.

The similar action on superfluous hypodermic adjournment renders ultrasound. Low-frequency waves warm the tissue, which leads to a gradual destruction of the cell walls. The released fat enters the intercellular space, then into the lymphatic system and is removed naturally. Unlike such procedures as laser liposuction, ultrasound cavitation is more affordable, but has more possible negative consequences, most important of which is the risk of skin burns.

Another type of non-surgical correction of the body suggests several injections of special lipolytic substances that dissolve excess fat. The components used in the preparations are harmless to the body and contribute to the improvement of metabolic processes.

Dissolved adipose tissue cells enter the systemic circulation and are excreted from the body by the kidneys and liver. This is injectable or chemical liposuction. It does not leave any scar or swelling. Discomfort can deliver only injections. But it is insignificant in comparison with inconveniences which the person tests, having used operational methods of correction of the body.

Chemical liposuction in Auckland , like other similar methods of getting rid of excess centimeters, is not a cure for the reason for the fat deposits. Plastic procedures can partially restore beauty, but not health. Therefore, in all cases of the appearance of excess fat, one should consult an endocrinologist, determine the cause of the disease and start treatment in time, after which there will not be a need to contact plastic surgeons.

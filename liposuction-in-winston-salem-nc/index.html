Liposuction zones

Usually liposuction is performed in places of fat accumulation, the so-called fat depots. Such zones on the body of each person about 25.

Most often, the removal of excess fat is carried on the abdomen, hips, buttocks, chin. In addition, liposuction can be performed at the back of the neck, in the armpit, on the shoulders and forearms, in the scapula, on the knees (internal and external surfaces) and so on.

Excess weight, which is usually due to fat deposits in the body, is not only an aesthetic problem, but also poses a certain danger to the body, since it is a burden on the heart and the entire body as a whole.

“Flanks” Zone

Liposuction of the anterior abdominal wall is also one of the most popular operations. Liposuction of inner thighs

On the inner surface of the thighs there are two zones for liposuction – one at the top of the thighs, the other at the bottom.

Liposuction of the middle part of the inner thighs is not performed, as the accumulation of fat occurs closer to the knees, as well as near the inguinal fold.

It is necessary to be very cautious in approaching this operation for people of advanced age. Skin with age loses its elasticity, becomes more flabby and wrinkled. In such cases, it is also necessary to remove excess skin to get a good result.

Shoulder Liposuction

Often, patients call it liposuction of the hands. At once we will be defined, a shoulder is an area beginning from an elbow and finishing an armpit. In the shoulder area, liposuction on the inner and back surfaces is possible.

Usually this operation is recommended for people with good skin elasticity, in case the skin flabby may need another operation called brachioplasty. Bend your arm at the elbow at 90 C and lift the brush up.

If from the bottom you saw an excess of sagging skin, it is possible in your case to perform brachioplasty, rather than liposuction, and also a combined operation is possible. The decision, of course, is taken by a plastic surgeon. After liposuction of the shoulder, hematomas and swelling can persist for up to 2 weeks. Immediately after the operation, you will need to wear compression underwear.

Liposuction of the chin

Quite often the operation is performed independently, more often – as one of the stages of tightening the lower third of the face and neck. Also, before the operation, it is necessary to pay attention to the skin condition.

If it is sluggish, not elastic, then the liposuction of the chin will reduce the volume of this zone, but the skin will remain hanging, as it hung before the operation. Therefore, the elderly patients liposuction of the chin, as a rule, represents one of the stages of a face lift.

Young patients are able to perform liposuction of the chin and gives a good result. The presence of the so-called second chin at a young age, at times, can cause discomfort, and it is almost impossible to get rid of it by other methods.

Liposuction of buttocks

It is a rather complicated procedure, since the result is formed in 3 dimensions, and not in two, as in most similar operations. Such an operation, as a rule, is attempted by women at the age when the shape of the buttocks changes and instead of round resembles a square or rectangular shape. Nevertheless, do not expect a miraculous transformation from this operation.

It should be remembered that liposuction does not affect the skin of the buttocks, and if it was sluggish, it will remain the same, and its role in the esthetic evaluation of the buttocks is quite high.

Punctures in the liposuction of the buttocks are made in the skin folds and are not completely visible after the operation. In addition, after this operation, it is necessary to wear compression underwear within the time limits set by the doctor.

After operation

Regardless of what kind of anesthesia this operation was performed: under general or local, on the same day you will leave the clinic. Immediately after the operation and for several days there may be minor pain that is removed with anesthetic medications.

Stitches at the puncture site are usually superimposed bioresorbable threads and do not require removal. There is always swelling of the tissues. Sometimes there are hematomas, which can be present up to 2-3 weeks.

During the first month after the operation it is necessary to wear special compression underwear, which has a compressive effect and speeds up the recovery process. Also for 4 weeks it is forbidden to perform sports exercises and other physical activities.

Lymphatic drainage massage can be recommended by a doctor in the postoperative period, as a procedure that accelerates the recovery process after surgery.

This massage is quite specific and must be performed by a competent specialist with experience of performing this procedure.

I am struggling with making a decision between subfascial and submuscular?
Submuscular for the win
Oct 6, 2020
Subfascial is more of a gimmick than an actual operation. The Benelli lift is useful at controlling areola size but has a fairly limited power to lift, plus the purse-string scar can be problamatic. Get more opinions from experienced breast surgeons in your area. Hope this helps, good luck!!!
Age 24, 5’11 and 165 lbs, breastfed two babies. Sub-glandular vs sub-muscular breast augmentation –
Submuscular for the win
Aug 18, 2020
The muscle provides camouflage in the upper pole of the breast hiding any rippling. Also by detaching the breast from the chest for sub-glandular placement you can sometimes get a rock in a sock look. An extended dual plane approach should give you a very nice result. Hope this helps, good luck!!!
Looking at HP silicone implants, 350-375cc. Will I need a breast lift along with my breast augmentat
Side view needed
Aug 15, 2020
Without a side view it is tough to say but it appears your nipples are above the breast but some of the breast hangs below the crease, a condition called glandular ptosis. Those usually don’t need a lift but would benefit from an extended dual plane approach. Hope this helps, good luck!!!

My right implant deflated. I want to get them removed. Is it safe to think that they will look simil
Removing implants
Jul 18, 2020
One of the advantages of saline implants is you can get a preview of what you would look like without them! If you are okay with how the deflated side looks, removing them is a reasonable option. There is no consensus on removing the capsule.
What is the best incision if I’m prone to keloid scars and interested in getting a breast augmentati
Apples and oranges
Jun 16, 2020
How a scar heals on your wrist (a mobile area) is not necessarily an indicator of poor scarring on the breast. You can also mitigate the final scar appearance but using silicone gel (Biocorneum) or silicone sheeting. Each incisional approach has its trade-offs that are best explained during an in-person consultation with an experienced breast surgeon. Hope this helps, good luck!

Lift with implants

Other than the issue of pain relief, there is no urgency to dealing with capsular contracture, unless you have a ruptured silicone gel implant, in which case it should be dealt with sooner rather than later. Hope this helps, good luck!
One of my implants has hardened and the other shifted, should I remove or replace my saline breast i
Removal vs replacement
Nov 21, 2019
The decision to replace implants, potentially leading to more surgery in the future, vs removing them, possibly leading to some degree of deformity, is a difficult one and there is no right or wrong answer. One option for you, since your implants are saline, would be an in office deflation to give you a preview of what life would be like without implants. Not all surgeons will do this, but my patients have found this helpful. Hope this helps, good luck!
I’m wondering if I have encapsulated implants deflated does the hard capsule collapse and feel soft
Deflating saline implants
Nov 19, 2019
Generally speaking, the hardness of an encapsulated breast come from tight scar around the implant. If you deflate the implant the breast should soften, at least temporarily. Hope this helps, good luck!

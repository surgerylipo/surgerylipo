Liposuction or lipomodeling is a method of the body correction.

The word “liposuction” in plastic surgery is used to designate the method of correction of the body, with the help of which it is possible to remove the fat (the professional language – adipose tissue) from the problem zones of the body.

This procedure is performed in a specially equipped operating room by a qualified surgeon and trained medical staff. Despite the fact that, there are several types of this plastic surgery, the essence remains unchanged – the fatty layer is removed from the body section through the thinnest vacuum suction tubes.

The difference, as a rule, is how the adipose tissue is preliminarily prepared to remove the abdomen or sides. The synonym for this procedure is also lipomodeling.

What is the abdominal and waist liposuction?

When diet and proper nutrition does not give any result, the aid comes liposuction is a procedure in which the removal of body fat occurs in the problem area.

The technique provides for two methods of action – surgical (when small incisions are made on the abdomen and sides) and non-invasive (which uses special equipment that acts sur- priately in contact with the skin).

Features of the methods

One of the most popular types of surgery is ultrasonic liposuction in Costa Mesa. Fat cells are exposed to ultrasound, heated and destroyed, and then taken out by vacuum through the tubes or naturally with blood and lymph.

Indications for conduction

Fat in the waist area accumulates in both men and women. True, in the latter, this process is much faster and has a more pronounced character.

The main indication for the use of ultrasound technique is the presence of an aesthetic defect – fatty deposits, from which it is difficult to get rid by other methods. Contraindications

The patient’s desire is not all. It is necessary for the doctor to approve such manipulations, excluding a number of contraindications:

  • pregnancy, lactation, recovery after childbirth;
  • diseases of the cardiovascular system;
  • diseases of the blood, diabetes, oncology, hepatitis;
  • exacerbation of chronic diseases;
  • age to 18 years.

Possible complications

Applying to an inexperienced surgeon for an operation, you run the risk of encountering the problem of side effects and complications:

  • disproportionality of contours;
  • sagging, flabbiness of the skin;
  • skin pigmentation, burns;
  • anemia;
  • inflammatory processes in the body;
  • formation of blood clots in blood vessels.

The main sign of the surgeon’s insufficient qualification is the low price of the services provided. In such clinics, it is not necessary to conduct such procedures.

Preparation and carrying out the operation

Preparation provides for a visit to the surgeon for making an anamnesis. The doctor will collect the necessary information about the patient’s health condition, estimate the amount of work to determine the cost in a particular case. The price may vary, depending on the area of the treated area. Correction of the abdomen and sides consists of several stages:

  • anesthesia,
  • Determine the impact zones
  • puncture of the skin in designated locations,
  • destruction of fat cells by ultrasound,
  • removal of fat through the cannula with a vacuum.

Rehabilitation period

Recovery after liposuction can take from 3 to 5 weeks. At this time, you should limit physical activity, refuse to visit saunas, baths and solarium. It should be a lot of time in the open air, eat properly and wear compression underwear. During recovery, it is also necessary to visit the doctor regularly.

Asian Eyelid Surgery, Hooded Eyes?
hooded eyes at age 18
Mar 20, 2013
Your post suggests that your brow tissues are naturally low. I agree that a browlift at your age is not indicated. Creation of a taller fold with minimal skin excision will expose more of the fold. Seek a consultation with a surgeon who has a lot of experience doing Asian blepharoplasties.
Is There Anything I Can Do to Reduce my Nasolabial Folds Naturally?
large pores and deep nasolabial folds
Mar 20, 2013
The large pores may respond to a retin-A based skin program and possible nonablative laser to follow. Deep nasolabial folds at a young age suggests loss of volume combined with early loss of elasticity in the skin. The above retin-A program can firm up the skin and I would start with this modality. If the nasolabial folds remain deep then you may consider soft tissue fillers.
Under Eye Blue Veins, Skin Thinning, Loss of Fat, and Wrinkles? Only 21 Years Old.
early aging around the eyes
Mar 20, 2013
The larger blue veins can be treated by surgical removal or laser. The dark color under the eyes is due to pigment in the skin itself as well as the thin transparent nature of the skin in this area. This darkness is very difficult to treat and may persist. Until we have a better answer to this problem I would recommend camouflage with cosmetics. Finally, the sunken look around the eyes may respond to soft tissue fillers or fat grafts.
I'm 19 and Trying to Get Rid of Access Fat, Should I Consider Liposuction?
Is liposuction the answer to my excess fat collections
Mar 20, 2013
Luiposuction is very safe when performed by surgeons who know what they're doing. However, liposuction is not a substitute for weight loss. In you case, it would be better to first manage your weight with diet and exercise. Once you have achieved a stable weight that you can maintain for the next 10-20 years then you may be a candidate for liposuction.
Neck Revision or Laser Treatment?
Neck revision or laser?
Mar 20, 2013
I doubt if a laser treatment(s) can correct this problem. Lasers have only a very limited ability to shrink skin. If it continues to bother you then I suggest that you discuss this with your surgeon. The neck portion of your face/necklift may need to be repeated.
Are my Ears Overcorrected?
ears overcorrected after setback?
Mar 20, 2013
It's hard to tell from your images if your ears are overcorrected. Ideally, after otoplasty you should be able to see the helical rim on the frontal view. If this is not the case and it bothers you then a revision may be necessary. There is no urgency to proceed with revision right away. Follow-up with your surgeon to discuss this.
Ptosis Repair- 5 Months Post Op and Ptosis is Back?
recurrent ptosis after ptosis repair
Mar 20, 2013
Your images suggest that the inner contour of the left lid margin is straighter (less arched) then that on the right side. If this persists and bothers you then you may need an additional 1-2 stitches to hike up that area. The decision to proceed with yet another surgery must be balanced against how much it bothers you.
After my Breast Implant One of my Breast is Bigger?
one breast is larger after breast augmentation
Mar 20, 2013
The reason for one breast being larger immediately after breast augmentation is due to either a blood collection, inadequate implant pocket dissection, or a larger implant on that side. Your preop pictures show that your breasts were fairly even in volume before surgery. You need to see your surgeon soon to correct this problem.
Implants Look Weird when Arms Are Up?
cord-like deformity after breast augmentation
Mar 20, 2013
This type of deformity if often seen after breast surgery and will resolve on its own. It is usually due to clotting of the veins under the skin and is not harmful to your health. You can either leave them alone or try various conservative measures to relieve the discomfort (warm compresses, anti-inflammatory medication, etc). I hope this helps.
Had 350 HP Gel Mentor Implants a Year and Half Ago. Contemplating Getting Revision to 450CC HP
changing to larger breast implants
Mar 20, 2013
Most women want to look a natural as possible after undergoing breast augmentation. In order to achieve this look you need adequate amount of soft tissue (skin, fat, breast) to cover the implant. If you currently look great with saline implants then you may not look very natural going to 450cc saline implants. Silicone gel implants are kinder to the look as you go larger but you don't want to outstrip your body's ability to cover the implants. Your surgeon can help...
Is It Okay to Leave Lumps Inside the Orbital Septum?
Lumps after fat grafting to the eyelids
Mar 20, 2013
Fat grafting can help improve the sunken eye deformity but it must be done by someone with a lot of experience using this technique. To get the best result, you may need to repeat the fat grafting several times because only a small amount of fat is injected each time to avoid limps and other deformities.
What Techniques Can Make my Eyes Look Almond or Korean?
Almond shaped eyes
Mar 20, 2013
The shape of the eyes depends on a number of factors including the length of the eye compared to its height, shape of the fold, amount of excess skin, etc. Surgery can help achieve the almond shape. If you don't want surgery then you may try various glues and double-sided tape that is made for this purpose.
Any Hope for Nipple Projection if I Get Breast Implants?
Loss of nipple projection after breast reduction
Mar 15, 2013
Loss of nipple projection is common after undergoing a breast reduction with a free graft technique. If you are happy with your current breast volume then you may consider various procedures to augment your nipple. Breast augmentation may push the nipple out but only to a small degree.
Is It Possible to Reverse Some of What Was Done in a Nose Job?
Not happy after rhinoplasty
Mar 15, 2013
Before discussing any revision it is important to determine exactly what bothers you about your new nose. You need to think about this and then discuss this either with your surgeon or someone who has experience with rhinoplasty revisions. Take time and do your homework rather than just jumping in with another operation because each successive surgery gets more difficult. I hope this helps.
Areola Reduction and Uneven Nipples. Can I Have Both Raised?
areola reduction and uneven nipples
Mar 15, 2013
It is possible to reduce the areola size and reposition the nipples but it does involve external scars. The size of the scars will vary according to the amount of pre-op nipple asymmetry. Your plastic surgeon can tell you what scars to expect depending on the amount of nipple movement that is necessary.

Best Way to Get Rid of Melasma?
There is no cure for melasma just control
Jun 12, 2017
Our office sees patients with melasma from all over the world. We specialize in it. Melasma requires a comprehensive cream program, chemical peels, and sun avoidance. We have great success treating melasma this way and then maintaining creams to prevent the pigmentation from returning. Our controlled depth peel is one great modality used to control melasma for patients who seek perfection. We also have the 3 step peel which significantly helps pigment control when used with the cream program...
Best Laser Treatment for Rosacea Redness?
We specialize in treating rosacea
Jun 12, 2017
First- you must make sure your doctor is treating your rosacea correctly. Constant use of antibiotics ends up making rosacea worse in the long run.
For the redness of rosacea, A PDL (laser) can also be used very well but needs to be repeated once per month to help control it. Sunscreen and avoiding heat/saunas, etc also help with the redness.
How to Remove Black Marks on my Face From Pimples?
Black marks after acne are PIH
Jun 10, 2017
Post inflammatory hyperpigmentation is not permanent but occurs in non-Caucasian skin tones after acne has resolved. Sun avoidance, sun screen, a comprehensive cream program can keep this at bay. Chemical peels such as the 3 step peel assist in this great.
Risks from Ablative CO2 Laser Resurfacing?
Fully ablative C02 laser is rarely performed today
Jun 10, 2017
Many years ago, full C02 laser ablation was utilized a lot. But we were seeing a lot of side effects like scarring, pigmentation issues, persistent redness, and a marble like quality to the skin. Now with fractional laser ablation technology- resurfacing can be performed in a much more safer way. In my practice, we utilize fractional laser resurfacing for its safety and efficacy.
Fraxel Acne Scars- Does it Work?
Fraxel works great for some acne scars
Jun 10, 2017
Frazel works well for acne scars but must be used in the right way and with the right settings. I see many patients in my clinic who have had fraxel that has done very little for their acne scars. We combine fraxel with a controlled depth peel and we are aggressive with fraxel to get adequate leveling of the scars.
When to Expect Accutane to Start Working?
It takes several weeks for Accutane to start working
Jun 10, 2017
Accutane does not start working immediately. In most cases, the first month your acne may be worse or may flare. After about 6 weeks is when I start seeing the major benefits from Accutane. It is important to be patient while on this medication.

Which peel would be the best treatment for my melasma?
I prefer peels for melasma rather than laser
Jun 7, 2017
Lasers can make melasma worse. I would use a comprehensive cream program and then consider TCA based chemical peels. After such procedures it is important to continue the treatment program to keep it at bay.

Effective treatments for acne scarring and hyperpigmentation?
Control the acne and then consider laser resurfacing
Jun 7, 2017
I would get he acne under control and put you on a comprehensive cream program. Due to the types of scars you have I would consider in some cases PDL to remove redness of scars and some fibrosis. An additional leveling procedure like fractional C02 laser would be helpful, too.
Which laser treatment works better on hyper pigmentation and melasma?
Avoid laser for melasma
Jun 6, 2017
We rarely use laser for melasma because lasers can make melasma worse. I prefer to treat melasma with creams and peels. I have had great success with this approach.
Juvederm in lips: when are the final results seen?

Jun 6, 2017
Lip filling can cause swelling which can take a few days or up to a week or two to settle down. I would wait about 1-2 weeks to see the final result. Its OK to loop back in with your MD if you feel you want more, etc.
Can Juvederm spread when put in nasolabial folds if you're a stomach sleeper?
It is rare for that to happen
Jun 6, 2017
I haven't seen filler move to the extent that you are describing. BUT- I would touch base with your MD to let him know your concerns. Juvederm can be reversed but you may not need that- a clinical examination is important for you.
Do I have a chemical burn from jessners peel? How should I treat?
A jessner's peel is a superficial peel
Jun 6, 2017
I would always encourage you to see your provider to discuss questions especially post-peel care. A jessner's peel is an epidermal peel and can only really cause some exfoliation of the skin. It doesn't penetrate into the dermis. Loop back in with your MD to discuss post-peel care and your concerns.

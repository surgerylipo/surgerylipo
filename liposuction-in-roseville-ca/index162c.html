The history of the appearance and development of liposuction

Correction of the figure of man began to gain popularity in the beginning of the 20th century.

Liposuction is nothing more than an operation to remove excess fat. The first operations to remove fat suggested mechanical punctures in the skin, through which the fat was removed.

A Body Contouring Procedure That Permanently Removes The Localized Fat That Does Not Respond To A Healthy Lifestyl

Also, in some documents, one can find a reference to the procedure for excision of fat and skin patches, which resulted in traumatism and a large number of scars.

In 1921, the procedure was first carried out, which at the present time we can describe as the first procedure for liposuction.

In the first surgery for liposuction a curette was used, with the help of which it was supposed to remove fat deposits from the ballerina’s knees.

The result of the operation was deplorable – the limb was amputated due to damage to the blood vessel. Then in the 70s of the last century, the Italian gynecologist Fischer applied the same curette, accompanied by vacuum sucking for abortion for a liposuction operation designed for his wife.

In 1977, the results of the first successful surgeries were published. The French specialist Illouz started using cannulas with blunt tips in his experiments, which surgeons use to this day. Since that time liposuction has become a painless and safe procedure for removing excess fat deposits.

In connection with the successful experiments in the 80s of the 20th century, the demand for liposuction began to increase.

Cannulas of a smaller size have perfectly adjusted the body shape, and postoperative scars have become less noticeable. In 1987, a special chemical composition was developed, which catalyzes the process of fat liquefaction and reduces the soreness of the operation. Since then, liposuction has gradually begun to become a mass service.

Modern methods of liposuction are diverse and allow satisfying the vagaries of the most diverse patients. It can be non-surgical, ultrasound and other types of liposuction of a wide variety of zones – the chin, abdomen, buttocks, thighs and so on.

Standard Liposuction Procedure in Roseville

Our conversation about the types of liposuction will begin with standard liposuction, which has lost its relevance in the modern world. Of course, in some clinics you can still meet such a procedure, which is distinguished by a huge number of clinical and aesthetic complications. Standard liposuction does not involve the use of expensive equipment and expensive medicines.

The standard procedure of liposuction is the cheapest and has two modifications – dry and wet.

Dry liposuction procedure. Nothing more than a classic version, which was born back in 1974 when Dr. Fischer used gynecological cannulas. We have already described this method, but once again we will briefly outline its essence. Through small incisions, the cannula is inserted under the skin, attached to a vacuum device that creates pressure, which pumps out fat. The amount of fat that is absorbed in one such procedure should not exceed 3 liters.

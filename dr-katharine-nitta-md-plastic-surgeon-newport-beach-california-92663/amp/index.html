Breast Lift with Implants
Feb 17, 2015
If your breasts are very ptotic (low), then it is best to do the lift and augmentation separately. There is less risk of losing sensation to the nipples, and the worst would be to loose the nipple all together due to poor vascularity. Doing the procedure separately insures that you will get the best results. If you have the procedure done together then you must always be prepared for a possible second surgery.

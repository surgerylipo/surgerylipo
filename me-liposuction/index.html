Maine Liposuction

Everyone knows that some features of the body, alas, can not be corrected either by exercise or by diet.

What can I do if regular exercises in the gym and a specially selected diet do not give the desired effect?

Plastic surgeons have long found the answer to this question: body liposuction – partial removal of subcutaneous fat.

Liposuction is an operative procedure to remove excess fat from the most problematic areas.

These are primarily the hips and abdomen (inner and outer thighs, belly and waist zone), although aspiration is often used to remove excess fat in other areas of the body, and even the face, for example, can be performed on the knees, chin, cheeks.

Indications for liposuction in Maine:

Patients, who come to the operation for lipomodeling the body, want to remove the extra “fat pad”.

“Fatty pillow” is the so-called mute zones, adipose tissue in which it is impossible to drive away with diets and physical exercises. It can be a zone of the abdomen, a zone below the navel, a hip zone, knees, chin and even arms.

Liposculpture is the final touch on the road to the ideal. Take it as a method of losing weight is impossible.

Manipulation is carried out in the case when the patient has reached his comfortable weight. There are cases when patients come with excessive body weight, with a pronounced fat thickness, and they want to lose weight with this method. It’s impossible.

First you need to reach your norm, only then do lipomodeling of your body. As such, there is no medical indication for surgery, only aesthetic.

Contraindications to liposuction

Contraindications to lipoaspiration are general somatic diseases:

  • hypertension,
  • diabetes,
  • any systemic diseases.

What is lipomodeling?

Lipomodeling is one of the stages of liposuction, when your own fat is redistributed between parts of the body, correcting, where necessary, the body. After all, this is an ideal material for embossed plastics. But, of course, in small quantities.

Liposuction Localization

Zones for lipomodeling of their body are distributed from the most minimal (buccal, chin area of the face) to large (thighs) zones. That is, in other words, wherever there is fat and there is an opportunity to reduce the thickness of the fat pad. The most popular zones of lipoaspiration are the abdominal zone, the lateral abdomen, the outer and inner thighs, the patella zone.

Laser liposuction and its technique (laser lipolysis):

An alternative to vacuum lipoaspiration is laser liposuction in Maine. It involves the destruction of adipose tissue by laser exposure. This method allows to avoid incisions or punctures, because the laser rays transform fatty tissue into a liquid mass, which is subsequently eliminated from the body naturally – through urine and sweat.

With the help of laser lipolysis, the flabbiness of the skin of the hands, abdomen, hips (inner and outer surface), chin is usually effectively eliminated. A significant advantage of laser lipolysis is the anti-cellulite effect, which is achieved by tightening the skin in the laser exposure zone.

However, the result of laser lipolysis is noticeable not immediately, but in a few months, as the fat tissue is removed from the body gradually.

Also, many experts note that such an operation works well only in delicate places, where fatty tissue is small and careful work is required (for example, surgery on the chin).

But when it is required to remove the excess fatty tissue in a larger volume, from more “volumetric regions” such as the hips, the belly-laser lipolysis works worse and the manipulation lasts longer (and hence anesthesia is longer) than, for example, the classical operation.

Although, for some aesthetic surgeons this is not an obstacle, and they use laser aspiration in all areas. Prices for laser lipoaspiration: prices depend on the volume of the treated area, and often higher by an order of magnitude than other types of surgical removal of adipose tissue.

What Is The Cost Of Smart Liposuction?

The cost of Smart Lipo varies widely based on the following factors.

A. Where is the surgery being performed?

1. Certified surgical suite will create an additional fee also called facility fee. This is for your added safety. Facility fees usually will add 1,000 to the total cost.
2. Exam rooms, on certified procedure rooms – often no facility fee, but you are taking chances with developing complications.

B. What type of laser is being used for Smart Lipo?

1. There is only one “Smart Lipo” on the market made by Cynosure. They invented laser liposuction. Their device creates the most skin tightening, least downtime and is currently the safest product on the market.

The latest version of Smart Lipo is called Smart Lipo Triplex. You will pay a little more for this superior technology. It is well worth it.

2. Other inferior brands: Won’t get the same results but will pay less. Beware of physicians using the “Smart Lipo ” name to lure you in only to use some other brand.

Always ask to see the device and make sure you they are using what they are claiming to have.

C. Who is performing the surgery?

  1. Board certified plastic surgeon (body) or facial plastic surgeon (face)- the cost will be higher for a plastic surgery specialist for that body area.
  2. Non plastic surgeons tend to charge a lot less. Beware of rock bottom pricing and physicians saying “board certified”- board certified in what? Eye doctors, OBGYN’s, pediatricians and internists are all performing liposuction and laser liposuction these days.

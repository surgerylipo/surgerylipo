Contraindications to surgical removal of fat

Absolute contraindications to the use of this method of correction of the body, regardless of its type, are recognized:

  • abnormal metabolism, including obesity and diabetes mellitus;
  • pregnancy and the period of breastfeeding;
  • impaired renal and hepatic function;
  • malignant tumors;
  • low coagulation rate;
  • infectious diseases;
  • hypertension;
  • thrombophlebitis;
  • anemia;
  • peptic ulcer of stomach and duodenum;
  • age to 18 years.

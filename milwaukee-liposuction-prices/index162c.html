Milwaukee Liposuction Prices

You can have different attitudes to surgical liposuction in Milwaukee, it’s still a medical operation, rather than a hike in the beauty salon.

By cons are a long period of rehabilitation, a blow to the body – anesthesia, possible side effects and complications.

The pluses can include a quick solution, and in the case of large fat deposits – almost the only way to radically solve the problem of body correction. They say that Spanish women do liposuction almost every year – and nothing, they are alive.

The concept of “liposuction” comes from Latin lipos – fat and English suction – absorption. The beginning of the epoch of liposuction is the 1980s. It was then that the first most successful operations were performed.

Cool Liposuction Before And After With Dr Maida Parkins, Milwaukee Plastic Surgeon

An effective and relatively inexpensive technique quickly gained deserved popularity. The technique of its implementation is constantly being improved, but the principle remains unchanged – the local removal of the deposits of subcutaneous fat and the correction of the contours of the body.

All types of liposuction can be divided into two groups. To the first, methods based on mechanical crushing of adipose tissue are included. A classic example is vacuum liposuction.

The second group is the ways in which fatty tissue is destroyed due to various chemical and physical factors, for example special solutions or ultrasound. There are classical, tumescent, ultrasound, syringe liposuction and hydrolysis sculpture. A separate point is the method of electronic lipomodeling.

Standard (vacuum) liposuction in Milwaukee is a pioneer among other types of fat removal. It’s unlikely that there are those who have never heard of this procedure. Perform it as follows: through small incisions, a special hollow needle (cannula) is inserted into the subcutaneous fatty tissue.

Doctor Kenneth Dembny, II, MD, Milwaukee Plastic Surgeon – Liposuction Abdomen And Flank

With cautious movements, the specialist moves the cannula under the skin, thus destroying the fat cells, which are immediately removed through the vacuum apparatus. However, you will not feel anything because you will be fast asleep. The operation is performed most often under general anesthesia.

Simple at first glance, the technique is quite traumatic, but with the help of vacuum liposuction you can get rid of such a volume of adipose tissue, that you will see an impressive result immediately after removal of compression underwear.

Pros. You can get rid of the very large volumes of fat (up to 2.64 gallons). Low cost compared with other types of liposuction.
Minuses. As a rule, general anesthesia is used. The highest probability of bleeding and complications (hematomas, seromas, fat embolism, in rare cases, leading to death).

Dr. Tracy E. McCall, Milwaukee Plastic Surgeon Liposuction For Stomach And Back

Tumescent liposuction almost does not differ from the vacuum method. The only difference is that before the procedure the surgeon introduces into the problem zone a special preparation (Klein solution), consisting of physiological solution, anesthetic and vasoconstrictor.

As a result, the blood vessels narrow, and the fat cells, on the contrary, swell, which facilitates their further removal. If during the procedure, not standard cannulas are used, but thin hollow needles, this method is called hydrolysis sculpture. As a rule, it is used as the final procedure after other types of liposuction.

Pros. It is possible to remove large enough volumes, blood loss is insignificant.
Minuses. As a rule, general anesthesia is used. Often complications of an aesthetic nature (violation of body contours, pigmentation, chronic puffiness).

In the method of ultrasonic liposuction, the fat deposits are first broken using a special ultrasonic probe, which is introduced directly into the fat layer. Then the fat cells are removed by cannulas.

Pros. Minimum blood loss, the effect of lifting the skin.
Minuses. The risk of complications is high (burns, seromas, necrosis of the skin in the operation area).

Syringe liposuction is used in cases, where it is necessary to remove a small amount of fat (up to 0.08 gallon). The operation is performed manually – instead of vacuum pumps, the surgeon uses syringes. The intervention itself lasts a long time, sometimes five to six hours, but thanks to the use of thin syringes on the skin there is no bruising and swelling.

Pros. Used only local anesthesia.

Dr. Paul W. Loewenstein, Milwaukee Plastic Surgeon Fat Liposuction Results Photo

Minuses. It is not possible to remove a large amount of adipose tissue.

Electronic lipo-modelling many experts call a sensation in liposuction. The method is based on the action of a high frequency current, that melts the fatty tissue. This is as follows. Thin needles are introduced into tissues attached to a generator creating an electric field. Liquefied fat is removed using very thin cannulas.

Additional bonus: the current promotes the acceleration of metabolism in adipocytes (fat cells), so you will lose excess weight for several months after the procedure. Note that no incisions will be made. Two or three very thin punctures are sufficient, after which no traces remain on the skin.

Pros. Using local anesthesia. The prolonged effect of weight loss, plus skin tightening.

Minuses. The procedure is not designed to remove large amounts of fat.

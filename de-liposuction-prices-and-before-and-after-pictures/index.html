There is an opinion that if you quickly return to normal business, the result of the operation may change – something shifts, it is wrong to grow together and so on. No, it’s an erroneous theory that after the operation you need to lie down, otherwise something will happen.

The Internet is full of stories that after liposuction, fat “appeared” in other places. Is it a myth or a reality?

There is such a mistaken belief that after liposuction a woman no longer needs a gym and proper nutrition and can exist in a relaxed state, because in these places fat no longer forms. In these places, yes, but it is formed in neighboring places, and they begin to “swim”.

Liposuction Of Body By Dr Abdollah Malek, Delaware Plastic Surgeon

The number of fat cells is strictly programmed in the body and can not increase. But the cells themselves can grow in volume, and this explains the “formation of fat.”

However, where they have already been removed, they will not appear again. And when they say that “I did liposuction, but I got out in another place” – this is wickedness.

Most likely, the woman just relaxed and stopped supporting the body in the form. Liposuction is a good way, but it’s a way to help. By itself, it does not solve the problem and will not allow you to abuse food in the future and lie on the couch. After 25-30 years of age, diet and sports are our companions.

We do not promise a waist, our task is to change the contour of the body. Of course, the volume will also change, but it is impossible to predict in centimeters, it can be a false deception for a person. Of course, there are contraindications, for example, with some serious chronic diseases, or in aggravation.

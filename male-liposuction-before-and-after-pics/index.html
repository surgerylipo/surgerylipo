Passed by those times when liposuction concerned only the beautiful half of humanity.

As it turned out, men have fewer problems – they are both a hated “beer” belly, and a second chin, and a nose that must be corrected after a fracture, and much more.

Indications and contraindications for male liposuction

Male liposuction is not possible in a number of cases:

– in the presence of severe diabetes;
– there are vascular pathologies;
– problems in the oncology plan;
– skin diseases in problem areas;
– inflammatory or infectious processes.

Chin

The second chin is a scourge of modern men, especially those suffering from excess weight. In this case, only the operation with masking of the incisions and seams in the folds of the skin rescues. The specialist in such cases uses extremely thin cannulas. About a week after the operation, puffiness remains.

Gynecomastia

This is a correction of the mammary glands that men seek to reduce. Surgical intervention is the only effective way, because weight loss does not help, and training only aggravates the situation.

Gynecomastia is divided into 2 categories:

  • true – excessive amount of glandular tissue;
  • false – an overabundance of fatty tissues.

The second case is much more common, but the operation allows you to get rid of the problem. A little more difficult with true gynecomastia, but here the effect is good. Preliminary the doctor conducts a mammogram, along with other analyzes, to avoid the case with breast cancer, which although rare in men, but precedents are known.

The most popular today is classical tumescent liposuction. The surgeon introduces a special solution into the breast, which activates the process of loosening the fat deposits – in the future it is much easier to pump them out. The effect of the operation is a 50% reduction in the gland. After the operation, you can wear special clothes.

Stomach

This problem is no less common. Male liposuction in this situation can be used to remove fat, and to correct the “beer” abdomen, although in the latter case, one can not do without abdominoplasty. Nevertheless, it is not necessary to identify liposuction and weight loss – the final shape of the abdomen, mainly depends on the state of the abdominal wall. During the operation, several punctures are created, but with exceptionally thin cannulas, in order to avoid bruising and gross scars. There is also the possibility of non-surgical correction.

Cost in the case of men is higher than for women, because men are larger, and fat elimination lasts longer, and the operation is more complicated.

The result

Liposuction is probably the most effective option for removing fat and correcting the body shape for both men and women.

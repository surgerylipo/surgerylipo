Two weeks after my double eyelid surgery?
Too soon to tell
Dec 15, 2013
2 weeks is generally too soon to judge the result of your operation. Swelling is greatest within the first week of surgery but the swelling will continue to go down until 3-6 months. You should visit with your plastic surgeon periodically to determine whether you are making appropriate progress.

Yes, but the caveat is...
Apr 19, 2019
Cleft palate repair can be peformed at any age. Unfortunately, not having the palate fixed early often means its much more difficult to speak properly even after its fixed. Without a working palate your body adapts by learning other ways to create the sounds. Intensive speech therapy will needed to "unlearn what you have learned".

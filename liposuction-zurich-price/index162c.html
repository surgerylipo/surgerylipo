Types of liposuction in Zurich

The volume of adipose tissue removal during liposuction can be different: someone needs to slightly adjust the area around the knees, and someone needs to remove a significant “fat depot” in the thigh area.

To solve each of the problems, there are three main types of liposuction.

Dry liposuction

During liposuction through a small puncture under the skin, a thin cannula is inserted. At the other end of it there is a vacuum apparatus, due to which the subcutaneous fat is removed (“sucked off”).

During this manipulation, the fat tissue is not subjected to any additional action, except mechanical – due to the movement of the cannula.

That’s why with the help of dry liposuction you can remove relatively small volumes of tissue (1-1,5 l), usually in the region of the hips, knees or chin.

Wet, or tumescent (tumescent) liposuction

During this procedure, you can remove up to 5 liters of fat. In adipose tissue is introduced Klein solution, which contains lidocaine, adrenaline and saline solution.

Lidocaine relieves pain, adrenaline causes a spasm of blood vessels, which reduces blood loss, and saline is needed to “break” the fat tissue. The latter is performed using ultrasound and laser devices, and then the fat is removed through the cannula.

Super-wet liposuction in Zurich

Removal of a large amount of adipose tissue is complicated by the fact that it contains blood and lymph. “Extruding” these fluids from adipose tissue, you can make liposuction more effective.

To do this, a solution is introduced into the adipose tissue, similar in composition to the Klein solution, but also containing a special component – the buffer. It as it squeezes blood and lymphatic vessels, not allowing the release of their contents into tissues. Fat cells, in turn, are surrounded on all sides by the surrounding liquid, which facilitates their removal.

In the process of super-wet liposuction, it is possible to remove up to 10 liters of adipose tissue, which helps to reduce body weight and create an optimal contour.

Preparation for surgery

Preparation for the operation begins with a visit to the doctor, which finds out what exactly the patient wants to do, from what zones to remove adipose tissue, assesses how possible such an operation is. An examination is mandatory to clarify the patient’s body weight, skin condition, condition of problem areas.

If the surgeon comes to the conclusion that the patient’s problem can be solved with the help of liposuction, then preparation for the operation begins.

In particular, you need to have a series of tests (blood and urine tests, ECG), you may need screen of internal organs, and in case of any problems, consult a specialist. Be sure to have a separate consultation with an anesthesiologist. When all the results are obtained, the doctor decides on the operation.

Liposuction Is A Surgical Procedure That Allows Excess Fatty Tissue To Be Removed

Before it starts, the problem areas are marked out: the doctor does this directly on the patient’s skin. After this, the patient is ready for anesthesia.

How is liposuction performed?

Liposuction is usually performed under general anesthesia, but with small volumes can be performed under epidural anesthesia with sedation.

After the patient falls asleep, the doctor treats the skin with an antiseptic and begins to perform injections (if wet or super-wet liposuction is chosen).

The next stage is the introduction through the small incisions of the cannula and the actual removal of the subcutaneous fat.

The duration of the operation is from 30 minutes to several hours: it all depends on the amount of fat that must be removed from the body.

After completion of all manipulations, the doctor imposes small seams – about 5 mm in length. And directly in the operating room, while the patient is still on the table, the doctor puts on the patient compression underwear.

Liposuction Is One Of The Most Effective And Successful Methods For Quick And Permanent Elimination Of Problem Areas

It is important

Wearing compression underwear is the most important time of rehabilitation after surgery. It is necessary, because as a result of the removal of adipose tissue between the skin and the muscles there is a kind of free space.

If you do not wear compressive garment, then this space can easily fill with liquid, swelling will begin. In addition, compression helps the skin to tighten faster and not to sag after the operation.

The third important point is prevention of blood clots, metabolism and circulation of fluids in tissues. When wearing compression underwear, tissue healing and recovery is faster.

Recovery

Since the operation is performed most often under general anesthesia, the patient must stay in the hospital for at least 24 hours under the supervision of the surgeon. At this time, pain can occur, sometimes the temperature rises, which is the norm.

During the next 2-3 days, the swelling of the tissues usually increases, which begins to shift downwards by about 7 days. Puffiness generally can persist for three months, especially if the patient breaks the diet and abuses salty foods or does not comply with the regime of wearing compression garment.

Sutures are usually removed on the 7th day (if they were not performed by absorbable threads). Always wear knitwear for a month, then go to the regime indicated by the doctor in charge. The first results of liposuction can be assessed only after three months, when the swelling subsides and skin tightens.

Liposuction results

Can I forget about the regimen, diet and sport after successful liposuction? It is believed that the fat tissue in the treated problem zone no longer “grows”. However, this is a dangerous delusion that can quickly destroy the surgeon’s efforts and devalue all the costs of the operation.

First, during liposuction, the doctor does not remove all, but only some of the fatty tissues. Unbalanced nutrition and insufficient physical activity lead to the fact that the fat layer can again increase in volumes already in other areas.

Secondly, sports significantly improve the result obtained in the framework of liposuction. And third, the latest research shows that the body can synthesize new fat cells. Is not this a good incentive to follow the regimen after liposuction?

There are a lot of misconceptions around liposuction, and most important of them is the idea of it as an operation aimed at reducing weight. The main purpose of liposuction: modeling the contours of the body, removing adipose tissue from the problem areas.

This explains all the restrictions that have operations. Today, medicine offers the most nontraumatic options for its conduct, which give a good result. And compliance with all the recommendations for recovery allows you to fix it for a long time.

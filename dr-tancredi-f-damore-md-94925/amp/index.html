Should Sientra HSC textured implants be massaged?
Sientra Textured Breast Implants should not be massaged.
Jul 21, 2014
This is an interesting question that comes up in our practice often. Textured Sientra breast implants have a textured surface that allows some ingrowth and therefore it is preferable to avoid massaging the implants and allow them to become one with your breasts. This is in contrast to smooth implants that were used for many years that were allowed to move inside a rather large capsule.

Does Bella Injectable Filler work permanently?
Bellafill lasts
Feb 28, 2019
Bellafill is indicated to last through 5 years in a FDA approved clinical trial with 1,008 patients. It's been studied longer and on more patients than any other dermal filler. Bellafill is made of bovine collagen (which gives the immediate results) and pmma microspheres (which stimulates your body to make collagen over time). As your body breaks down the bovine collagen, it's stimulated by the microspheres to make your own collagen filling the space where the bovine collagen was.

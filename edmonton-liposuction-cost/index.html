In the modern world, the appearance of a person plays a very important role.

With the development of plastic surgery, many, even the most complex procedures and operations become more accessible and common.

Not surprisingly, in recent decades, the popularity of plastic surgery has increased several times around the world.

One of the most popular and common operations to date, both among men and women, is the operation of liposuction (synonyms: lipomodeling, liposculpture, lipoaspiration).

There are four main methods of lipoaspiration:

  • Classical vacuum;
  • Laser or laser lipolysis;
  • Ultrasound;
  • Vibration.

There are also some other, more rare methods of lipoaspiration, which are practically not used by plastic surgeons. Classical vacuum liposuction and its technique:

This operation involves the introduction of a buffer solution through small punctures. The action of the solution causes swelling of the adipose tissue.

With the help of a special small cannula, subcutaneous fat is destroyed and sucked off with a special device – vacuum suction.

Vacuum aspiration has proved to be a very effective procedure, because for a relatively short time it allows to correct the most problematic areas of the figure. The operation usually takes no more than an hour.

Days or two after the manipulation should be carried out in the hospital. Within a month after the operation, it is necessary to wear compression underwear.

Ultrasonic liposuction in Edmonton

Ultrasonic liposuction has proven to be a very effective and safe method to remove excess fat. Ultrasonic lipoaspiration is performed by influencing the subcutaneous fat of ultrasonic waves of a certain length. Waves destroy adipose tissue, while the surgeon fully controls the course of the process and can act by ultrasound on separate areas of the body and not affect others.

This allows you to achieve the best aesthetic effect and to form the desired proportions of the body. Under the influence of ultrasonic waves, fat is converted into an emulsion, which is sucked off with a vacuum or is removed by means of a special roller. The advantage of this operation is the minimal loss of blood and lifting effect, obtained by reducing and tightening the skin.

In addition, ultrasound aspiration has other advantages: it can affect not only the deep subcutaneous fat, but also the more delicate zones (for example, the chin, cheeks), in addition, this type of lipoaspiration also effectively fights against cellulite. The final result of ultrasonic liposuction becomes visible after about 3 months, although the first changes begin to appear already towards the end of the second week after it.

Unlike vacuum classical aspiration, such an operation can be performed in older people, although earlier such manipulations have not been carried out because of the increase in the thrombogenic potential of the age-related organism and the reduction of skin elasticity. However, it should be borne in mind that surgical removal of adipose tissue is only a cosmetic operation, it helps to correct the proportions of the figure and remove excessive fat deposits, but it is not designed to combat excess weight. Practice has shown that ultrasound lipoaspiation on the hips, in the lower abdomen and on the chin is most effective.

Radio wave liposuction

The main task in the operation is to remove fat cells with minimal trauma to surrounding tissues. That is why the classic mechanical destruction of fat tissue is replaced by more and more new hardware techniques. Another variant of the destructive effect on fat cells is radio frequency radiation.

The procedure is performed using a special BodyTite device. The main functional part of the device is a bipolar nozzle (two electrodes, between which radio waves pass through). Through a puncture on the skin, one of the electrodes is injected inside. Its diameter is less than 2 mm, which is much smaller than the usual cannula used for mechanical destruction of fat cells.

The electrode introduced under the skin acts as a melting element. It transfers the surrounding fatty tissue to a more mobile state, so that it can be easily removed by aspiration. Radiofrequency radiation not only destroys adipose tissue, but also coagulates small vessels. Thus, trauma is reduced due to smaller internal dissections, and due to a reduction in hematoma.

Reducing the volume of hematomas is one of the central problems of all types of liposuction. The destruction of tissue inevitably leads to the rupture of blood vessels and, consequently, internal hemorrhage. The duration of the rehabilitation period, the overall health of the patient depends on how carefully manage to maintain a network of blood vessels.

Water jet liposuction in Edmonton

Water-assisted liposuction (WAL) is another method of fat loss in the subcutaneous layer. As the name suggests, the action is carried out using water jets. But for this use not simple water, but a specially developed solution containing prilocaine, epinephrine and other anesthetics and narrowing vessels of the substance. The solution is injected under the skin at the desired angle under high pressure.

As a result, the fat cells are destroyed, and the pain sensations are stopped by the anesthetics injected with water. Aspiration, the solution is removed from the subcutaneous layer together with the released fat cells. Fat, as it were, is washed out of the body.

Each technique is worthy in the hands of a surgeon who owns it. But the general recommendations are as follows. The chin zone is best done with laser liposuction – it is more delicate. In general, it is better to do more delicate and non-voluminous places with a laser.

More voluminous, such as the abdomen, thighs – no longer matters.

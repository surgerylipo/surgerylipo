Liposuction In Gainesville VA

Often in the news about the show business, we hear discussions that this or that star has made herself a liposuction. And what does this fashionable word “liposuction” mean? Liposuction in Gainesville is a procedure for removing fat deposits.

When diets and fitness can no longer cope with a lot of fat, liposuction will come to the rescue.

If the skin is very sagging and has lost its elasticity, then together with liposuction you will be offered to excise excess skin.

Therefore, liposuction at a younger age is more successful and the result is more effective than in older people, when the skin has already lost its elasticity.

Contraindications to liposuction in Gainesville.

Liposuction is contraindicated in people who are overweight and who want to get rid of it.

Areas That Tend To Accumulate Fat

Since liposuction is a way of shaping the contours of the body, and not a method for losing weight.

– People taking medications that affect wound healing (in the treatment of edema).
– If you do not have enough elastic skin, then you can also refuse to conduct the procedure, the doctor must necessarily examine your skin for elasticity upon examination.

How is the procedure performed?

First the doctor makes anesthesia, since this procedure is still a surgical operation. Anesthesia can be general, and when working with individual sites, local anesthesia is used.

In the folds of the skin, small incisions are made. To avoid the appearance of a tumor, saline is used with the content of lidocaine and epinephrine, which prevents postoperative bruises.

In the incisions of the skin, a narrow metal tube is inserted, which is connected to a vacuum pump.

Goodbye To Stubborn Fat

The surgeon should very professionally pump out fat in equal portions. Otherwise, on the body will remain uneven skin and the contours of your body will be broken.

The consequences of liposuction.

The incisions heal very quickly. But on the site of liposuction there are bruises and swelling. Hematomas disappear within 10 days, but swelling may not go down to 3 months. Painful sensations appear after the end of the anesthesia and disappear after 2 hours. In the first days after the procedure, you can not strain, wear heavily tight clothes and take anti-inflammatory drugs.

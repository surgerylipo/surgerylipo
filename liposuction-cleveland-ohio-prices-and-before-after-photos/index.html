Liposuction Cleveland Ohio Prices And Before After Photos

Liposuction (“pumping fat”) is a plastic surgery aimed at removing fat deposits from the body and giving the body an attractive shape.

The procedure requires surgical intervention, but it is characterized by high efficiency and allows to achieve the desired results.

Most of the excess fat is removed on the abdomen, sides, hips and buttocks, less often – on the back, chest, under the knees, under the armpits.

Recently, liposuction of the face is gaining popularity – women resort to surgery to remove the second chin, correct the shape of the cheeks and neck.

How much does liposuction cost? Prices for liposuction can vary significantly depending on the area of exposure and the volume to be removed.

Indications for conduction

Liposuction in Cleveland is performed with:

  • The presence of “fat traps”, which include the stomach, thighs, “second chin”;
  • some diseases: gynecomastia, climacteric hump (it is also called the widow’s hump or the wit of the accountant);
  • the need to remove the formed lipomas – benign formations, formed from adipose tissue.
  • The operation is recommended in cases when conservative methods do not allow to get rid of the problem.

Contraindications to liposuction

Types of operation

In the clinic of plastic surgery there are several types of surgical intervention:

  • Syringe liposuction – fat is sucked off with syringes. This method will only help with insignificant deposits of fat (less than 0.13 gallon).
  • Tumescent liposuction – a plastic surgeon makes miniature incisions on the skin and uses a very thin cannula to inject into the fatty tissue a solution that has a softening effect and consists of lidocaine, adrenaline and sodium chloride. The operation minimizes blood loss, promotes rapid healing, does not require long-term recovery (3-4 days is enough).
  • Water-jet liposuction – the fat cells are separated from the connective tissue by an aqueous jet flowing under a strong head and a special solution. It minimizes the traumatism of the body and promotes rapid recovery. The type of surgical intervention is chosen by a plastic surgeon on the basis of the individual characteristics of the patient.

How is surgery performed?

Before performing a surgical procedure, make a general or local anesthesia. Then, marking out the areas requiring correction is performed.

The final stage is the evacuation of fat by vacuum, which resembles the work of a vacuum cleaner. If you want to remove a large amount of fat, then the operation is repeated after a while.

The duration of the procedure depends on the type of surgery and can range from a quarter to three hours. Within 1 day the patient remains in the hospital under medical supervision. The final result can be estimated only after a couple of months.

Possible complications after liposuction in Cleveland

The best surgeon carries out all procedures in strict accordance with the protocol, which contributes to the desired result and to the absence of postoperative complications. If the operation is not performed correctly, or the patient does not follow the doctor’s recommendations, hyperesthesia or increased skin pigmentation, bruising, the appearance of seroma and fat embolism, impaired skin sensitivity, infection of the surgical wound.

Postoperative period

For several days after surgery, pain, swelling and a slight increase in temperature can occur. But soon they will disappear and everything will return to normal. To prevent complications, several rules should be followed during the postoperative period.

Within a week, you will have to limit physical activity, refuse to take a shower, visit the sauna, pool and solarium, and for a month wear a compression corset. If necessary, the doctor can recommend recovery measures, including massage, mesotherapy and ozone therapy.

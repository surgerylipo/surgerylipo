What is Liposuction?

Liposuction is an effective way to quickly remove subcutaneous fat from the sides, lower abdomen, thighs, chin, legs, hands, buttocks, inner thighs and other parts of the body.

Liposuction is aimed at removing “fat traps” by getting rid of the excess of subcutaneous fat, and allows you to solve the aesthetic problem in a variety of areas: from the shoulders to the hips.

Liposuction is most often performed for the following zones:

– stomach;
– Hips and buttocks;
– waist;
– Shoulders;
– Lower third of the face;
– knees.

If you plan to do a liposuction of the abdomen, or other areas of the face and body, it is necessary to understand that this method of correction is not a method of treating obesity and does not affect the pathogenesis of the disease.

Combining Lipo With Other Procedures

Presence of excessive deposition of subcutaneous fat in local areas of the face or body.

Contraindications for liposuction

  • oncology;
  • Hypertension in the severe stage;
  • Diabetes mellitus of any type;
  • Viral, infectious and chronic diseases in the stage of exacerbation;
  • Pronounced violations of the internal organs;
  • Loss of skin elasticity;
  • Lactation and pregnancy;
  • Presence of clotting disorders.

Features of liposuction

Before doctors help you get rid of the “fat traps”, you need to undergo a preliminary examination to exclude contraindications to the operation.

Liposuction of the abdomen, knees, cheeks and other problem areas is performed under general anesthesia. The duration of the operation is from half an hour to several hours, depending on its complexity.

Arizona clinic uses all modern methods of liposuction, but the most common technique is vacuum liposuction. To remove subcutaneous fat, a special solution is introduced. On the patient’s skin punctures of 0.5-1 cm are made.

Through them, the surgeon inserts a metal cannula. It is connected with vacuum suction. To get rid of subcutaneous fat, the doctor performs forward movements of the cannula, destroying the fat. With the help of suction, excess subcutaneous fat is removed from the body, and all punctures are made in the natural folds of the skin or in subtle areas.

Recovery after liposuction

After the doctors helped remove excess fat from the face or other areas of the body, the patient is in the hospital for a day. Within a month after liposuction it is recommended to refrain from physical exertion, visits to the solarium and bath.

In the first days after the operation, water should be avoided at the site of incisions. During the recovery period, it is necessary to wear compression lingerie. The preliminary result of liposuction is achieved after 1-2 months, and the final result – about six months later.

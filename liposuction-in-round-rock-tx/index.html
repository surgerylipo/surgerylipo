Liposuction In Round Rock TX

Many men and women, having tried the most different ways of fighting fatty deposits, resort, in the end, to liposuction in Round Rock – a surgical operation to remove accumulations of fat.

Today we will talk with you about what is liposuction, what liposuction techniques are the most in demand and what are their advantages and fundamental differences.

So, liposuction is a fairly radical and very effective method by which you can remove excess fat from the abdomen, chest, back, hips, pubic area, hands, and even from certain areas of the face, such as cheeks or chin.

However, liposuction can not help everyone, since there are various contraindications to the operation.

Liposuction is not performed by minors, people with diabetes, or patients with complete loss of skin elasticity.

Also, one should refrain from surgical intervention in the presence of some chronic diseases or during acute manifestations of inflammatory processes.

Now a lot of different methods of liposuction have been developed all over the world, but in our country vacuum, tumescent and ultrasound techniques have been most widely used.

Vacuum liposuction.

The most accessible for most is the vacuum method of liposuction. In the process of vacuum liposuction, a special needle is introduced into the subcutaneous fat layer, which serves to destroy fatty deposits.

Lipoplasty Sculpts Away Unwanted Fat Deposits That Can Remain In Spite Of A Healthy Lifestyle

They are immediately withdrawn by means of a vacuum device, which allows one to remove up to 10 liters of fat in one session.

Ultrasonic liposuction

Dr M. Scott Haydon, Round Rock Plastic Surgeon Liposuction Thighs And Love Handles

Ultrasonic liposuction is less traumatic, since the destruction of fat cells in this case is carried out by means of ultrasonic waves.

Thus it is not necessary to do incisions of tissues, and the liquid formed as a result of destruction of cells, can leave a body naturally.

No postoperative scars and bruises, but the effectiveness of this procedure is slightly lower than with vacuum liposuction, so several sessions may be necessary to achieve the desired result.

In some cases, with acoustic liposuction, the incisions still do to affect the fat deposits more effectively.

Tumescent liposuction in Round Rock

Tumescent liposuction fundamentally differs from the above methods by preoperative preparation. First, a special solution is injected into the fatty deposit zone, which provokes the contraction of the blood vessels and swelling of the fatty deposits. This preliminary preparation ensures the effectiveness of the effect on the fat cells.

If general anesthesia is used for vacuum liposuction, then only local anesthesia is used for tumescent liposuction. It should also be noted that tumescent and ultrasound liposuction does not require a long stay in the hospital: a few hours after the procedure, the patient is already released home.

When using surgical techniques, the postoperative period lasts up to three weeks, during which pain can be observed, bruises, swelling. The effectiveness of removing excess fat in liposuction, as plastic surgeons say, you can fully evaluate only after 5-6 months, although the first results will be visible after 10-14 days.
